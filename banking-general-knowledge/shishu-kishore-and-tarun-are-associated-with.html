‘Shishu’, ‘Kishore’ and ‘Tarun’ are associated with

(A) SBI

(B) RESERVE BANK

(C) IDBI

(D) ** MUDRA BANK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana. The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana aims to enable the following financial companies and banks to provide Low Rate Loans to eligible entities.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) Shishu : covering loans upto 50, 000/-Kishor : covering loans above 50, 000/-and upto 5 lakh. Tarun : covering loans above 5 lakh and upto 10 lakh.

Concept note-3: -Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency (MUDRA) Ltd. Bank was launched on 8 April, 2015 as a subsidiary of SIDBI.

Concept note-4: -Given as a part of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, the Mudra Tarun loan can help you secure funding of anywhere between ₹5 to ₹10 lakhs. You can use this money to set up a new business, expand your operations or even meet heavy working capital requirements.

Concept note-5: -MUDRA was launched by the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi on 08 April 2015 at a function held at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. 2015-16
