(A) Gross Batch Settlement

(B) ** Deferred Net Settlement

(C) Priority Net Settlement

(D) Domain Net Settlement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: NEFT is an electronic fund transfer system that operates on a Deferred Net Settlement (DNS) basis which settles transactions in batches.

Concept note-2: -NEFT stands for National Electronic Funds Transfer. Started in November 2005, NEFT is an electronic funds transfer system set up and managed by the Reserve Bank of India. NEFT allows the online transfer of funds from one NEFT-enabled bank account to another.
