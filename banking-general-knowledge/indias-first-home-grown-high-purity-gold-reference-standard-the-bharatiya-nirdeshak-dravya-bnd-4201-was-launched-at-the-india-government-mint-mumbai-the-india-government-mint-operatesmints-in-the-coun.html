India’s first home-grown high purity gold reference standard-the Bharatiya Nirdeshak Dravya (BND-4201)-was launched at the India Government Mint, Mumbai. The India Government Mint operates____mints in the country for the production of coins.

(A) five

(B) three

(C) ** four

(D) two

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The BND-4201 is reference material for gold of ‘9999’ fineness (gold that is 99.99% pure with impurities of only 100 parts-per-million). It will be beneficial to the consumers and public at large to ensure purity of gold.

Concept note-2: -Bharatiya Nirdeshak Dravya is the trademark of Certified Reference Material (CRM) produced by CSIR-NPL in association with Reference Material Producers (RMPs).
