Where can Baroda Reloadable card be used?

(A) ATM

(B) POS

(C) E-commerce website

(D) ** all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Baroda Reloadable Card can be used at ATM, POS (any RuPay certified merchant establishment in India) and E-commerce/Online platform.

Concept note-2: -Many people are reaping the benefits of using prepaid credit cards. One of the main advantages is that you can use them at ATMs just like a normal credit or debit card.

Concept note-3: -Using a reloadable debit card is fairly simple. You buy the card, then follow the activation instructions that come with it. Once the card is activated, you can load money onto it. Depending on the card, you may be able to load money online, by phone, using direct deposit, or in-person where you bought it.

Concept note-4: -GPR cards are reloadable, so when the money on the card is used up, you can add additional funds. Gift cards are often not reloadable. GPR card users can withdraw cash from their cards at an ATM.

Concept note-5: -A reloadable Visa Prepaid card is the quick, easy and secure way to pay online or in person. The all-purpose Visa Prepaid card is a prepaid card that you can use to withdraw cash, pay bills or make purchases anywhere Visa Debit cards are accepted, in-person or online.
