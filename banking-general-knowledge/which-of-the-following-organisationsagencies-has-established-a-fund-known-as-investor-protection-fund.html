Which of the following organisations/agencies has established a fund known as Investor Protection Fund?

(A) ** RBI

(B) SIDBI

(C) Bombay Stock Exchange

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority was established by Government of India on 7 th September, 2016 for administration of Investor Education and Protection Fund under the provisions of section 125 of the Companies Act, 2013.

Concept note-2: -12.1 The Government has established an Investor Education and Protection Fund (IEPF) under Sec. 205 C of the Companies Act, 1956 under which unclaimed funds on account of dividends, matured deposits, matured debentures, share application money etc.

Concept note-3: -Investor Education and Protection Fund(IEPF) According to the act, the company which has completed seven years in the business should hand over all the unclaimed fund dividends, matured deposits, and Debentures, share application money etc. to the Government through IEPF.
