Message notifications

(A) ATM

(D) ** Text Alerts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Text alerts, or SMS alerts, are short text messages businesses send out to employees or customers alerting them to essential information. They are one of two primary ways that a business can use text messaging as part of their day-to-day operations.

Concept note-2: -The primary difference between push notifications and text messages is where they are sent from and arrive. Push notifications come from apps installed on a person’s phone. On the other hand, texts are sent to phone numbers from another individual’s phone number or from a business application.

Concept note-3: -How to Fix Text Message Notifications Not Working Android 12? If you find Text message notifications not working on Android 12, simply head to the settings application and click on Notifications. Scroll down, select the default text messaging app, and ensure the notifications are turned on.
