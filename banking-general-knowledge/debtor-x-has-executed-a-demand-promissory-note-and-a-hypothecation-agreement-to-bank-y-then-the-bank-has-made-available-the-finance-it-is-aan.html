Debtor X has executed a Demand Promissory Note and a Hypothecation agreement to Bank Y. Then the bank has made available the finance. It is a/an

(A) ** Valid contract

(B) Invalid contract

(C) Quasi contract

(D) No contract

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A promissory note is valid for 3 years from the date of its execution. Post this period it will become invalid. Also read: Bill of Exchange.

Concept note-2: -A promissory note must include the date of the loan, the dollar amount, the names of both parties, the rate of interest, any collateral involved, and the timeline for repayment. When this document is signed by the borrower, it becomes a legally binding contract.

Concept note-3: -A home mortgage effectively secures a promissory note with the title to the property in question in case the lender should need to foreclose and sell the property in event of nonpayment. Your lender will keep the original promissory note until your loan is paid off.

Concept note-4: -The note must also contain the terms and conditions between the two parties involved. This includes the amount of money or capital loaned, the interest rate and the repayment schedule. Once the parties address the conditions of the promissory note and sign it, it becomes a legally binding contract.
