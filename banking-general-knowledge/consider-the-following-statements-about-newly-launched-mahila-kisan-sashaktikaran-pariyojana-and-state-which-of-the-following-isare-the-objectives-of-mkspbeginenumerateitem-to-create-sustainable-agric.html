Consider the following statements about newly launched Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana and State which of the following is/are the objectives of MKSP?

1. :

To create sustainable agricultural livelihood opportunities for women in agriculture

2. :

To ensure food and nutrition security at the household and community level.

3. :

To enhance the productive participation of women in agriculture.

(A) Only 1

(B) Only 3

(C) 1 and 2

(D) ** All are true

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The primary objective of the MKSP is to empower women in agriculture by making systematic investments to enhance their participation and productivity, as also create and sustain agriculture based livelihoods of rural women.

Concept note-2: -The government of India launched Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana in the year 2011 under the National rural livelihood mission. Through this scheme, the status of women belonging to the agriculture sector will be improved. Various kinds of opportunities will be provided to women through this scheme.

Concept note-3: -Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana | Ministry of Rural Development | Government of India.

Concept note-4: -As a component of National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) Ministry of Rural Development is extending the scope of Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana (MKSP) to empower the tribal women engaged in NTFP collection.
