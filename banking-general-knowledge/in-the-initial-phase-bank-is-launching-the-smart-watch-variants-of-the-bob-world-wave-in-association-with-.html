In the initial phase Bank is launching the smart watch variants of the “bob World Wave” in association with ____

(A) M/s Fire-Boltt

(B) M/s boAt

(C) M/s Noise Color Fit

(D) ** M/s GOQii Technologies Pvt. Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda launches bob World Wave I Now just Wave and pay.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Bob World Wave. ‘bob World Wave’ wearable payment solution was launched by the Bank of Baroda in December 2021. bob World Wave is designed to ensure seamless digital payments with just one tap on the device.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is City Union Bank. City Union Bank has launched a wearable payment solution called CUB fitness watch debit card. It has been launched in association with smart-tech-enabled preventive healthcare platform GOQii and is powered by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).
