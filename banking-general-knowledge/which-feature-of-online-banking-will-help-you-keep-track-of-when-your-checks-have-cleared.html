Which feature of online banking will help you keep track of when your checks have cleared

(A) Manage and pay bills online

(B) Apply for credit

(C) Compare rates and prices

(D) ** Access account information

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Look up the “current available balance” in your checking account. You can find this information on either your bank’s website or through its mobile app. If you’re using a paper checkbook register, you’ll record this number in the top spot above the spaces you use to log your transactions.
