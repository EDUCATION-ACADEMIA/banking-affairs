How many Payment banks were approved by RBI in 2015?

(A) 7

(B) ** 11

(C) 41

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The external advisory committee headed by Nachiket Mor submitted its findings on 6 July 2015. The applicant entities were examined for their financial track record and governance issues. On 19 August 2015, the Reserve Bank of India gave “in-principle” licences to 11 entities to launch payments banks.

Concept note-2: -RBI gives licence for 11 payments banks.

Concept note-3: -As many as 26 firms, including top corporate houses like Tata Sons, L&T, Reliance Group, Adiya Birla Nuvo, Bajaj, Shriram and Religare, have sought banking licence. The last day for application was July 1.

Concept note-4: -India currently has 6 Payment Banks namely, Airtel Payment Bank, India Post Payment Bank, Fino, Paytm Payment Bank, NSDL Payment Bank and Jio Payment Bank.
