Bank of Maharashtra is a major public sector bank in India. Bank of Maharashtra based in

(A) Aurangabad

(B) Mumbai

(C) Nashik

(D) ** Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Maharashtra is an Indian public sector bank headquartered in Pune. The bank had 29 million customers across the country with 2022 branches as of March 2022. It has the largest network of branches of any nationalised bank in the state of Maharashtra.

Concept note-2: -Currently, the Pune-headquartered bank is ranked 11 th among 12 PSBs in terms of total business.

Concept note-3: -SBI is the largest public sector bank in India and Asia and one of the largest in the world.

Concept note-4: -Motilal Oswal tracks top 7 PSU banks-State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of India, Bank of Baroda, Central Bank, Union Bank and Indian Bank and from the list its top picks are SBI, BoB and Central Bank.
