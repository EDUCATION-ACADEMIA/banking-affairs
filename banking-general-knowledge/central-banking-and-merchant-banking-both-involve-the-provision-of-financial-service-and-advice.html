Concept note-1: -Merchant banking is a financial service provider that offers a wide range of services such as underwriting, issuing of securities, asset management, portfolio management, and advisory services. Merchant banks provide specialized services to large corporations, high net worth individuals, and institutional investors.

Concept note-2: -They do not provide basic banking services. Among all the services, the focus is on financial advice. These banks primarily earn from the fee paid for advisory services.
