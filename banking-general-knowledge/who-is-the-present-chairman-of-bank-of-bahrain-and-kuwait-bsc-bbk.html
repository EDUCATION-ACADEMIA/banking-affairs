Who is the present Chairman of Bank of Bahrain and Kuwait B.S.C (BBK)?

(A) Hassaan Mohammed Burshaid

(B) Abdulrahman Ali Saif

(C) Mohamed Ali Malik

(D) ** Murad Ali Murad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Duan Yongping (Chinese: ; pinyin: Duàn Yǒngpíng; born 1961) is a Chinese billionaire entrepreneur and electrical engineer. He is the founder of both the Subor Electronics Industry Corporation (also the former CEO), and BBK Electronics Group (also the current Chairman).

Concept note-2: -The full form of BBK is the Bank of Bahrain and Kuwait. In both the State of Kuwait & the Kingdom of Bahrain, the BBK was founded in 1971. Its owners comprise the general public & Bahrain’s government.

Concept note-3: -BBK, formerly known as The Bank of Bahrain and Kuwait B.S.C, was established on 16th March 1971 in accordance with the Amiri decree and started operations a year later. BBK has been the pioneer in Commercial Banking for 50 years in the Kingdom of Bahrain.
