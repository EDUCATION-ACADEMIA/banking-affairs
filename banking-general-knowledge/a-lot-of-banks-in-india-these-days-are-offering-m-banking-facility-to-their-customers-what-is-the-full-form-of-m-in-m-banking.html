A lot of Banks in India these days are offering M-Banking Facility to their customers. What is the full form of ‘M’ in “M-Banking’?

(A) Money

(B) Marginal

(C) Message

(D) ** Mobile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mobile banking is a service provided by a bank or other financial institution that allows its customers to conduct financial transactions remotely using a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet.

Concept note-2: -What Is Mobile Banking? Mobile banking is the act of making financial transactions on a mobile device (cell phone, tablet, etc.). This activity can be as simple as a bank sending fraud or usage activity to a client’s cell phone or as complex as a client paying bills or sending money abroad.

Concept note-3: -The primary source of income for banks is the difference between the interest charged from the borrowers and the interest paid to the depositors. Banks usually collect higher interest from loans than the interest they provide for deposits.
