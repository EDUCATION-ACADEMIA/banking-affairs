Credit Cards are a convenient substitute for which of the following?

(A) Cash

(B) Cheque

(C) ** Both A and B

(D) None of The Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Credit Cards are a convenient substitute for Cash, Cheque.

Concept note-2: -Share: Debit and credit cards are both used to pay for goods or services without paying in cash or writing a check. The difference between the two is where the money to pay for the purchase comes from.

Concept note-3: -There are essentially two types of credit cards: general credit cards and retailer credit cards.
