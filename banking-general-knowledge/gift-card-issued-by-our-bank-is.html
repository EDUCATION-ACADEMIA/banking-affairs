EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Platinum Debit card (RuPay/MasterCard/Visa) Enjoy the prime facilities with our Union Bank Platinum Debit Card and become part of Digital Economy. Avail Airport lounge facility twice in a quarter with a spend of only Rs. 2/-with Rupay Platinum Debit Card.

Concept note-2: -The RuPay Prepaid Platinum Card is a prepaid card designed to help you manage your expense – both personal and professional. The card comes with various features and benefits including offers from leading merchants.

Concept note-3: -Baroda Gift Card is welcome everywhere at RUPAY certified merchant establishment in India. However, it cannot be used to withdraw cash from ATM Machines as per current guidelines of Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-4: -Processing fee: For RuPay debit cards, since the processing of a transaction takes place domestically, the processing fee is relatively cheaper. With regard to VISA debit cards, since VISA is a foreign card associate, the processing takes place abroad, making it a little more expensive that a RuPay debit card.
