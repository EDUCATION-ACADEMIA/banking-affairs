MSME act came in which year

(A) 2005

(B) ** 2006

(C) 2007

(D) 2008

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MSME Definition. The classification of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises is defined under the MSMED Act 2006 amendment dated 01/06/2020. The Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises is based on the Investment in Plant, Machinery or Equipment values (excluding land and building) and Annual Turnover.

Concept note-2: -17. Recovery of amount due.-For any goods supplied or services rendered by the supplier, the buyer shall be liable to pay the amount with interest thereon as provided under section 16.
