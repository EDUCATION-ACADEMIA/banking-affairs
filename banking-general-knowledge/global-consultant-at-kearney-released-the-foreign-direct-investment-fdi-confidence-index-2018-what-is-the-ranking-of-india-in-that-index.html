Global consultant AT Kearney released the Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Confidence Index 2018, what is the ranking of India in that Index?

(A) 6th

(B) 8th

(C) 10th

(D) ** 11th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is ranked 16th in the FDI Confidence Index 2019, compared to 11th in 2018. It slipped down 4 positions from the 2018 ranking.

Concept note-2: -It may be noted that as per the UNCTAD World Investment Report (WIR) 2022, in its analysis of the global trends in FDI inflows, India has improved one position to 7th rank among the top 20 host economies for 2021.

Concept note-3: -India’s Rank in FDI Confidence Index 2022 India placed eighth and joined the top ten in 2017.

Concept note-4: -FDI in India 2022: The highest FDI in India was from two countries Singapore and USA in FY 2021-22, with Singapore accounting for 27.01 percent of the FDI equity inflows in India and US for 17.94 percent.
