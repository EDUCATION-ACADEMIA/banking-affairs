According to the government, how many toilets are targeted to be build under Swacch Bharat Scheme in the next fiscal 2018-19?

(A) ** 2 crore

(B) 5 crore

(C) 8 crore

(D) 11 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of its targets was the construction of 66 lakh individual household toilets (IHHLs) by October 2, 2019. However, this target was then lowered to 59 lakh IHHLS by 2019. This target was achieved by 2020 (see Table 1).

Concept note-2: -According to the Swachh Bharat Mission dashboard (SBM), there are 15.13 crore households with toilets.

Concept note-3: -A financial outlay of Rs 1, 41, 600 crore has been finalised for SBM-U 2.0, including a central share of Rs 36, 465 crore, which is over 2.5 times the financial outlay of Rs 62, 009 crores in the last phase of the mission, the government said in a statement.
