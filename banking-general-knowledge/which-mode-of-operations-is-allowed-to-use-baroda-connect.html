(B) Both Jointly or Survivor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Baroda Connect” can be accessed from any computer (including one from cyber café) having internet facility.

Concept note-2: -Then customer would be required to re-register for the facility as per the procedure laid down for the same. to the customer. vi) Accounts where mode of operation is “joint” as also accounts in the name of minor below the age of 15 years or where minor is a joint account holder are not eligible for Baroda M-Connect.

Concept note-3: -Baroda Connect’ is an umbrella of various services offered electronically to Retail and Corporate Customers.
