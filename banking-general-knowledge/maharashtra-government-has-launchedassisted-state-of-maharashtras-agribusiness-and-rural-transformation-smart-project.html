Maharashtra Government has launched____assisted State of Maharashtra’s Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) Project.

(A) AIIB

(B) IMF

(C) ** World Bank

(D) ADB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of Maharashtra has launched the State of Maharashtra Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) Project to promote farming in the state.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra Government has launched World Bank assisted State of Maharashtra’s Agribusiness and Rural Transformation (SMART) Project to transform rural Maharashtra. This project aims to revamp agricultural value chains, with special focus on marginal farmers across 1, 000 villages.

Concept note-3: -The project aims to transform Rural Maharashtra through “SMART” interventions in Agriculture and Livelihood sectors, assisted by World Bank.
