The largest share of Gross Domestic Product (GDP), in India comes from

(A) agriculture and allied sector

(B) manufacturing and construction

(C) ** service sector

(D) defence and public administration

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The services sector is the largest sector in India. The services sector accounts for 53.66% of the total India’s GVA of Rs. 137.51 lakh crore. The industrial sector is at the second spot and contributing around 31% of the Indian GDP.

Concept note-2: -The services sector accounts for 53.89% of total India’s GVA of 179.15 lakh crore Indian rupees. With GVA of Rs. 46.44 lakh crore, the Industry sector contributes 25.92%. While Agriculture and allied sector share 20.19%.

Concept note-3: -Service sector contributes the most in the Indian GDP.
