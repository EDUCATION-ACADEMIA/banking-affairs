Total priority sector advance of domestic commercial bank should be ____ % of ANBC.

(A) ** 18

(B) 40

(C) 75

(D) 80

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -6.1 For the purpose of priority sector lending, ANBC denotes the outstanding Bank Credit in India [As prescribed in item No.VI of Form ‘A’ under Section 42 (2) of the RBI Act, 1934] and computed as follows: Bank Credit in India [As prescribed in item No.VI of Form ‘A’ under Section 42(2) of the RBI Act, 1934]

Concept note-2: -40 per cent of Adjusted Net Bank Credit or Credit Equivalent Amount of Off-Balance Sheet Exposure, whichever is higher. 75 per cent of ANBC. However, lending to Medium Enterprises, Social Infrastructure and Renewable Energy shall be reckoned for priority sector achievement only up to 15 per cent of ANBC.

Concept note-3: -The applicable target for lending to the non-corporate farmers for FY 2022-23 will be 13.78% of ANBC or CEOBE whichever is higher.
