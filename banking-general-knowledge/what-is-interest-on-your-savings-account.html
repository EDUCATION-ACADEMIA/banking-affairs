What is “interest” on your savings account?

(A) Extra amount of money you have to pay.

(B) ** Extra amount the bank gives you.

(C) The amount of money you currently have in your account.

(D) The amount of money you want in your account.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Savings Account with Highest Interest Rates 2023. There are a number of public sector as well as private sector banks in India. Although each bank, generally has a different range of interest rate for savings accounts, the rates usually range from 2.70% p.a. to 7.00% p.a.

Concept note-2: -How much interest can you earn on $1, 000? If you’re able to put away a bigger chunk of money, you’ll earn more interest. Save $1, 000 for a year at 0.01% APY, and you’ll end up with $1, 000.10. If you put the same $1, 000 in a high-yield savings account, you could earn about $5 after a year.
