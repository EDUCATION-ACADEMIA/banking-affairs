Under Akbar, the Mir Bakshi was required to look after

(A) ** military affairs

(B) the state treasury

(C) the royal household

(D) the land revenue system

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The head of the military was called the Mir Bakshi, appointed from among the leading nobles of the court. The Mir Bakshi was in charge of intelligence gathering, and also made recommendations to the emperor for military appointments and promotions.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The head of the military department under the reorganized central machinery of administration during Akbar’s reign was Mir Bakshi.

Concept note-3: -The Mir Bakshi post was started under the rule of the Khilji Sultanate of Delhi when Alauddin Khilji introduced it.
