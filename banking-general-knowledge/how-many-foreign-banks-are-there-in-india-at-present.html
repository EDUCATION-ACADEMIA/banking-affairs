How many foreign banks are there in India at present?

(A) 42

(B) 43

(C) 44

(D) ** 45

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), there are now 46 Foreign banks in India as of 2022.

Concept note-2: -As of the year 2022, 46 international banks have established operations in India, as reported by the country’s central bank. A total of 45 international banks operate in India, with most of their roughly 300 branches concentrated in the country’s largest cities.

Concept note-3: -Standard Chartered is the largest foreign bank in India. There were around 46 international banks in India in the year 2020. Other well-known foreign banks having a large presence in India are Citibank, Deutsche Bank, and Barclays Bank. The headquarter is located in Hong Kong.

Concept note-4: -Foreign banks too started to appear, particularly in Calcutta, in the 1860s. Grindlays Bank opened its first branch in Calcutta in 1864. The Comptoir d’Escompte de Paris opened a branch in Calcutta in 1860, and another in Bombay in 1862; branches followed in Madras and Pondicherry, then a French possession.

Concept note-5: -At present, there are 12 public sector banks in India.
