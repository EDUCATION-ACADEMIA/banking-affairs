What can be the minimum initial contribution to the paid-up equity capital of small finance banks?

(A) 25%

(B) ** 40%

(C) 75%

(D) 50%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Basel II norms will be generally applicable to the small finance banks, unless stipulated otherwise. The promoters shall hold a minimum of 40 per cent of the paid-up voting equity capital of the bank, which shall be locked-in for a period of five years from the date of commencement of business of the bank.
