(A) Regulation of currency

(B) Control of credit

(C) Banker to the government, bankerâ€™s bank and lender of the last resort.

(D) ** Accepting deposits and making loans and advances to public

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advancing loans to the public and accepting deposits from the public is not the function of the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-2: -Which entities can legally accept deposits from public? Banks, including co-operative banks, can accept deposits. Non-bank finance companies, which have been issued Certificate of Registration by RBI with a specific licence to accept deposits, are entitled to accept public deposit.

Concept note-3: -Facilitating import of goods is not a primary function of a bank.
