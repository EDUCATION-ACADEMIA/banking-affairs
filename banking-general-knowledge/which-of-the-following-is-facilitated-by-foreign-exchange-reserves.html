Which of the following is facilitated by Foreign Exchange Reserves?

(A) External trade

(B) Orderly development

(C) Maintenance of foreign exchange market

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Foreign exchange reserves facilitate external trade and payment and promote orderly development and maintenance of foreign exchange market in India.

Concept note-2: -Silver holding of the RBI is not included in the foreign exchange reserves of India.
