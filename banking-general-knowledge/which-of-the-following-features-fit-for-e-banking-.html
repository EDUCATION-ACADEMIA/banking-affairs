Which of the following features fit for E-Banking-?

(A) Providing Ease in banking operations

(B) Stress on branchless banking

(C) Time Saving

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -E-banking means electronic banking (also known as online banking or virtual banking). Through e-banking, a customer can perform banking transactions such as transferring money from one account to another, checking account balance, making payments and applying for loans.

Concept note-2: -Internet banking provides personal and corporate banking services offering features such as viewing account balances, obtaining statements, checking recent transactions, transferring money between accounts, and making payments.
