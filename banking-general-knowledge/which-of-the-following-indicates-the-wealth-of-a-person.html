Which of the following indicates the wealth of a person?

(A) Bank balance

(B) Capital

(C) ** Net worth

(D) Assets

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your net worth is the value of all of your assets, minus the total of all of your liabilities. Put another way, it is what you own minus what you owe. If you owe more than you own, you have a negative net worth. If you own more than you owe you will have a positive net worth.

Concept note-2: -Net worth is the most common measure of wealth, determined by taking the total market value of all physical and intangible assets owned, then subtracting all debts.

Concept note-3: -Your net worth represents how much wealth you have, measured by assets like a house, cars, 401(k), jewelry or cash in the bank, minus the debt obligations you have, or what you owe.

Concept note-5: -Net worth is assets minus liabilities. Or, you can think of net worth as everything you own less all that you owe.
