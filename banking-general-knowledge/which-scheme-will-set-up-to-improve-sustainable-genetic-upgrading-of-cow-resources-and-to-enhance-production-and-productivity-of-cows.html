Which scheme will set up to improve sustainable genetic upgrading of cow resources, and to enhance production and productivity of cows?

(A) Rashtriya Gaay Aayog

(B) Rashtriya Kamdhenu Ghar

(C) Bhartiya Kamdhenu Aayog

(D) ** Rashtriya Kamdhenu Aayog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To help the central government to develop appropriate schemes, programs for sustainable development of cows in the country and to suggest measures for optimum economic utilization of cow wealth.

Concept note-2: -It has been constituted to organize animal husbandry on modern and scientific lines and to take steps for preserving and improving breeds, and prohibiting the slaughter of cows and calves and other milch and draught cattle. There are 50 well-defined breeds of cattle and 17 breeds of buffaloes in the country.

Concept note-3: -About Rashtriya Kamdhenu Aayog: Rashtriya Kamdhenu Aayog is an apex advisory body to address the issues related to conservation, protection and development of cows and their progeny . It was created by a government resolution in February 2019 and will function as an integral part of Rashtriya Gokul Mission.

Concept note-4: -Aayog Chairman Dr. Vallabhbhai Kathiria appealed all the Vice Chancellors across India and College Heads for starting Kamdhenu Chair in every University and College.
