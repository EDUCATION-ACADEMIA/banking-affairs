What is the full form of NSDL?

(A) ** National Securities Depository Limited

(B) National Securities Demanding Liability

(C) National Sample Depository Limited

(D) National Sample Driven Land

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The full form of NSDL is National Securities Depository Limited. NSDL is a depository of Indian securities that stores digitally securities such as investor bonds, shares and debentures.

Concept note-2: -The National Securities Depository Limited is a financial entity set up to hold securities in the form of tangible or non-physical certificates. Itâ€™s like a bank account system for securities like bonds and shares, in the form of either tangible or intangible certificates.

Concept note-3: -Simply put, the NSDL allows traders and investors to open accounts, so their shares and securities can be held electronically. It is comparable to a bank, where customers open accounts to store their funds.
