Which of the following is the Central Bank of USA?

(A) Federation of Banks, USA

(B) Citigroup, USA

(C) Bank of America

(D) ** Federal Reserve

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Federal Reserve, the central bank of the United States, provides the nation with a safe, flexible, and stable monetary and financial system.

Concept note-2: -Instead of a single central bank, the framers of the Federal Reserve Act provided for a central banking “system” with three main features: (1) a central governing Board, (2) a decentralized operating structure of regional reserve banks, and (3) a combination of public and private characteristics.

Concept note-3: -The Federal Reserve System is the central bank of the United States.

Concept note-4: -The Reserve Banks are decentralized by design and are located in Boston, New York, Philadelphia, Cleveland, Richmond, Atlanta, Chicago, St. Louis, Minneapolis, Kansas City, Dallas, and San Francisco.
