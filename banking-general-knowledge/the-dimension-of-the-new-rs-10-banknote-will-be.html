The dimension of the new Rs 10 banknote will be

(A) 56 mm x 136 mm

(B) ** 63 mm x 123 mm

(C) 71 mm x 142 mm

(D) 67 mm x 166 mm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The note has motif of ‘Sun Temple, Konark’ on the reverse, depicting the country’s cultural heritage. The base colour of the note is chocolate brown. The note has other designs, geometric patterns aligning with the overall colour scheme, both at the observe and reverse. The size of the new note is 63mm x 123mm.

Concept note-2: -The size of the new note is 66mm x 150mm. The colour of the notes is stone grey and the predominant new theme is Indian heritage site-Red Fort.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 150mm × 66 mm.

Concept note-4: -* The major difference is that the new Rs 10 note will be in chocolate brown in colour instead of the previous combination of orange and pink. * The new Rs 10 notes will be in same height of 63 mm but the width will be 123 mm-slightly lesser than the existing width of 137 mm.
