Which of these products does NOT allow people a way to save money and earn interest?

(A) certificate of deposit

(B) ** home mortgage

(C) money market account

(D) savings account

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Current Account.

Concept note-2: -A money market account is an interest-bearing savings product available at most banks and credit unions. You can usually write checks from it and may get a debit card.

Concept note-3: -A savings account is a basic type of financial product that allows you to deposit your money and typically earn a modest amount of interest. These accounts are federally insured up to $250, 000 per account owner and offer a safe place to put your money while earning interest.
