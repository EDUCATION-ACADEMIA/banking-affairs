The Kirloskar group had announced that it has received license from the Reserve Bank to launch a NBFC. Who will be heading this NBFC?

(A) ** Vimal Bhandari

(B) Renu Satti

(C) Satish Kumar Gupta

(D) Sandeep Bakhshi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Systemically Important Core Investment Company (CIC-ND-SI): CIC-ND-SI is an NBFC carrying on the business of acquisition of shares and securities.

Concept note-2: -NBFCs Which Need Not be Registered With RBI Core Investment Companies – (assets are less than 100 crore or public funds not taken) Merchant Banking Companies. Companies that are engaged in the business of stock-broking. Housing Finance Companies.

Concept note-3: -Every company seeking registration as NBFC-Factor shall have a minimum Net Owned Fund (NOF) of ₹5 crore, or as specified by the Reserve Bank from time to time.
