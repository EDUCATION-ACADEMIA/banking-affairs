National Housing Bank (NHB), is an apex financial institution for housing, having headquarters in-?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bank is an officer oriented, professionally managed institution with headquarters in Delhi and offices in Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Kolkata, Chennai, Ahmedabad and Lucknow with plans to open offices at Patna and Bhopal over the next few months.

Concept note-2: -National Housing Bank (NHB) was set up by an Act of Parliament in 1987. NHB is an apex financial institution for housing.

Concept note-3: -It was set up in July 1988 under the National Housing Bank Act, 1987 as the apex bank in the field of housing finance. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-4: -NHB is wholly owned by Reserve Bank of India, which contributed the entire paid-up capital.
