What is the minimum and maximum period required for Online RD and TD?

(A) ** Minimum 12 months and 120 months

(B) Minimum 3 months and 60 months

(C) Minimum 6 months and 24 months

(D) Minimum 9 months and 72 months

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DURATION:-Minimum 6 Months and Maximum 120 Months, i.e. Term of RD can be for 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24………… 120 months .

Concept note-2: -The minimum period of a Recurring Deposit is six months at most of the banks. These deposits can be opened from 6 months to 10 years, depending on the depositor.

Concept note-3: -You can choose a suitable period of deposit with a maximum tenure stretching up to 10 years. The interest rate offered on RDs is always higher than the interest earned through a savings account.

Concept note-4: -Duration: For fixed deposits, the minimum duration is 7 days and for recurring deposit, the minimum duration is six months. The maximum duration for both schemes is 10 years.
