Which of the following facilities is not available in bob World Internet?

(A) Online Nomination facility

(B) Online Account Transfer Facility

(C) Online Purchase of SGB

(D) ** Cash on Mobile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apart from the Loans, Deposits and Cards, Bank of Baroda offers other services that add value to our banking system, such as capital market services, collection services, ECS, etc. Browse through all the other services that we offer to our customers.

Concept note-2: -bob World has been crafted intuitively for a seamless, delightful, contactless and effortless experience with 240+ services, all that you have to do is to sit back and experience the joy of banking for your 360° banking needs.

Concept note-3: -You can also access the same under Login > Services > Mobile Banking (bob World) > Registration. Select your customer ID, confirm that the mobile number shown is your registered mobile number, and click on Continue. Confirm Details, then enter remarks and Transaction password. Click on Submit button.

Concept note-4: -The bob World mobile banking app can help you perform a lot of banking ancillary actions such as changing your login password, changing your BoB mPIN, updating your registered email-ID, referring a friend to join the BoB family and providing feedback on our services.
