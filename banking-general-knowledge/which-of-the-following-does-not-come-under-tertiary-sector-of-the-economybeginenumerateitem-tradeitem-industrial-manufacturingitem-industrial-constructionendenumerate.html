Which of the following does not come under tertiary sector of the economy?

1. :

Trade

2. :

Industrial Manufacturing

3. :

Industrial Construction

(A) Only 2

(B) Only 3

(C) ** 2 and 3

(D) Only 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Electricity. Tertiary sector also known as service sector is one among the three type of economic sectors in a developed economy, the other two being Primary (extraction of raw materials) and secondary (manufacturing) sectors.

Concept note-2: -Electricity sectors does not come under tertiary sector.

Concept note-3: -The tertiary sector covers a wide range of activities from commerce to administration, transport, financial and real estate activities, business and personal services, education, health and social work.

Concept note-4: -Activities that involve the provision of services are categorised under the tertiary sector. For example, transportation, insurance, and tourism are all service activities.
