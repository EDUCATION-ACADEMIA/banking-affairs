Chief of RBI is known as____

(A) President

(B) Chairman

(C) Managing director

(D) ** None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Shaktikanta Das, IAS Retd., former Secretary, Department of Revenue and Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India assumed charge as the 25th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India effective December 12, 2018.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is India’s central bank, also known as the banker’s bank.

Concept note-3: -Recently, Shaktikanta Das has been appointed the Governor of RBI for a period of three years. The Governor of the Reserve Bank of India is the Chief Executive Officer of the Central Bank of India and the Ex-officio Chairman of its Central Board of Directors.

Concept note-4: -In India, Reserve Bank Of India or RBI is known as the banker’s bank. It is so called because it acts as a bank for all the commercial banks in India.
