As we have noticed many banks of Indian origin are opening offices/branches in foreign countries. Why is this trend emerging at a very fast pace?

1. :

These Banks wish to provide banking facilities to foreigners as banking facilities are not plenty in many foreign countries. India wants to take an advantage of the situation.

2. :

These banks wish to help Indian firms to acquire funds at internationally competitive rates.

3. :

These banks wish to promote trade and investment between India and other countries.

(B) Only 2

(C) Only 3
