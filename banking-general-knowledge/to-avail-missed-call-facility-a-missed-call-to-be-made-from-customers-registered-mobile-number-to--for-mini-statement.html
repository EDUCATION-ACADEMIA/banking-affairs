To avail missed call facility, a missed call to be made from customer’s registered mobile number to ____ for Mini Statement.

(A) ** 8468 00 1122

(B) 8468 00 1111

(C) 8468 00 2222

(D) 8468 00 3333

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The procedure to register SBI Missed Call Banking (SBI Quick) is mentioned below: You must send an SMS to 09223488888 from the mobile number that is linked to your account. The format of the SMS must be ‘REG Account Number’. Once the SMS is sent, you will receive a confirmation message.

Concept note-2: -SBI Balance Enquiry Toll Free Number-FAQs The account-holders can check their SBI balance by calling the SBI balance inquiry toll free number 09223766666.

Concept note-3: -Mini Statement can be obtained by giving missed call or by sending SMS. Customer can get last 5 transactions details by giving missed call to 9223866666.
