NBFC is a company registered under ____

(A) ** The RBI Act

(B) The Indian Contract Act

(C) The Companies Act, 1956

(D) Co-operative society Act

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Though NBFCs come under the purview of the Companies Act, they are exempted from the above Rules since they come under RBI regulation under the Reserve Bank of India Act.

Concept note-2: -A Non–Banking Financial Corporation is a company incorporated under the Companies Act 2013 or 1956. According to section 45-I (c) of the RBI Act, a Non–Banking Company carrying on the business of a financial institution will be an NBFC.

Concept note-3: -LIC Housing Finance, Bajaj Finance, Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services, Shriram Transport, Tata Sons, L&T Finance, Indiabulls Housing Finance, Piramal Capital & Housing Finance, Cholamandalam Investment and Finance Co., Shanghvi Finance Pvt.

Concept note-4: -In terms of Section 45-IA of the RBI Act, 1934 it is mandatory for a company to obtain Certificate of Registration (CoR) from Reserve Bank of India (RBI) before commencing or to carry on business of a non-banking financial institution.
