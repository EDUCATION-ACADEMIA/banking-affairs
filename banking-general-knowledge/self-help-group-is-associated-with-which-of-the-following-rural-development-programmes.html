“Self Help Group” is associated with which of the following Rural Development Programmes?

(A) Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana

(B) ** Swarna Jayanti Gram Swarozgar Yojana

(C) TRYSEM

(D) Indira Aawas Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The objective of SGSY is to bring every assisted family above the poverty line within three years by providing them income generating assets through a mix of bank credit and Government subsidy.

Concept note-2: -SGSY was started on 1st April, 1999 after restructuring erstwhile IRDP, TRYSEM, SITRA GKY and MWS. The basic objective is to bring assisted poor families above the poverty line by providing income generating assets through mix of bank credit and subsidy.

Concept note-3: -The Swarna Jayanti Swarozgar Yojna (SGSY) has been renamed as National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM). With this the scheme will be made universal, more focussed and time bound for poverty alleviation by 2014.

Concept note-4: -SGSY provides loans at low subsidies; PMGY develops basic healthcare in rural areas. SGSY and PMGY provide employment in urban and rural areas, respectively.
