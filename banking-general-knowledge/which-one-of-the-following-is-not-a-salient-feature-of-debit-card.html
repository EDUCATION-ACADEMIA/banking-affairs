Which one of the following is not a salient feature of debit card?

(A) No bad debts to banks and no suits for recovery

(B) No interest earning for banks

(C) Works like a normal withdrawal slip

(D) ** 45 days credit is given to the card holder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Service of providing 45 days credit is given to the card holder is not a salient feature of debit card. It is a feature of credit card. In debit card, when a payment is made for a transaction the money comes directly from the user’s bank account.

Concept note-2: -It is simple to use and makes transactions fast, easy and convenient. Encourages Judicious Spending: In debit cards, there is no feature of the credit facility; thus, this encourages judicious spending. Acts like an Emergency Fund: A debit card can be used to withdraw money from an ATM, in case of an emergency.
