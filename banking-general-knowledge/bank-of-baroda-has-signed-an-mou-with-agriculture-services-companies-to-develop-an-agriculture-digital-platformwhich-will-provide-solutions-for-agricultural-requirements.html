(A) Baroda Khet

(B) Baroda Krishi

(C) ** Baroda Farmer

(D) Baroda Kisan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Public sector lender Bank of Baroda Tuesday said it will develop an agri-digital platform called ‘Baroda Kisan’ in order to provide solutions for all major agricultural requirements.

Concept note-2: -At Bank of Baroda, we seek to be the source that will help all our stakeholders realize their goals. To our customers, we seek to be a one-stop, reliable partner who will help them address different financial needs.

Concept note-3: -Moreover, the app is available in three languages at present i.e. English, Hindi, and Gujarati – making it accessible to a wider base of farmers.

Concept note-4: -We treat our customers in a polite manner, in exactly the same way we would like to be treated ourselves. We deliver customer service with honesty, responsiveness, transparency, respect, value, loyalty and with an aim to resolve any real or potential problems.
