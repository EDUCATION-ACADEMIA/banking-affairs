Dominic wants to verify his account balance from home. He has Internet access.

(A) savings account

(B) ** online banking

(C) safe deposit box

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can check your account balance online anytime-and much more. To get started, navigate to your bank’s website and access your account information. You can also use a mobile app, as described below.

Concept note-2: -Use your login ID and password only on the official login page of the bank, which should be a secure website. Look for ‘https://’ in the URL when logging in; it means that the website is secure. Check your account after making any transaction online. Verify whether the right amount has been deducted from your account.
