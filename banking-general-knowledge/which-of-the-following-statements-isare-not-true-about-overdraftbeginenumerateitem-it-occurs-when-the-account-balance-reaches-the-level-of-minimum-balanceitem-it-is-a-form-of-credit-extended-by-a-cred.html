Which of the following statement(s) is/are not true about overdraft?

1. :

It occurs when the account balance reaches the level of minimum balance.

2. :

It is a form of credit extended by a creditor when the account balance reaches zero.

3. :

Overdrafts allow bank clients to withdraw money even when there are no funds in the account.

(A) Only 1 and 2

(B) Only 2

(C) Only 1 and 3

(D) ** Only 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An overdraft occurs when there isnâ€™t enough money in an account to cover a transaction or withdrawal, but the bank allows the transaction anyway.

Concept note-2: -Overdrafts are where the bank account becomes negative and the businesses in effect have borrowed from the bank. This is shown in the cash book as a credit balance.

Concept note-3: -Yes, you can withdraw cash from your overdraft facility by using a cash machine. How much you can take out depends on what your daily limit is.

Concept note-4: -If you choose to opt in to debit card and ATM overdraft, you are usually allowed to make ATM withdrawals and debit card purchases even if you do not have enough funds at the time of the transaction. However, you will generally incur fees on transactions that settle against a negative balance later.
