____is the only dedicated Institution for education in Insurance and Actuarial Science in the World.

(A) IBAI

(B) IRDAI

(C) ** IIRM

(D) IIB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIRM is a joint initiative of the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) and the Government of Telangana to strengthen education and provide skilled human resources to the Financial Services Sector.

Concept note-2: -The Insitute of Insurance and Risk Management ranked 21st in India in the Government B School category according to the Business World B School ranking. India Today rankings placed IIRM Hyderabad in the 6th position in the South zone of the Govt. B school category. IIRM Hyderabad ranked 29th in India in the Govt.

Concept note-3: -IIRM is the pioneering Educational Institution offering programmes in Financial Services, Insurance, Actuarial Science, and allied verticals. It is unique in character as the Institute is promoted by IRDAI and the Government of Telangana with the sole aim of developing an employable workforce.

Concept note-4: -Yes, IIRM Hyderabad is a government college.
