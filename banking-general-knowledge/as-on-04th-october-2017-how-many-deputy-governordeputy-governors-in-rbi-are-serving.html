As on 04th October 2017, How many Deputy Governor/Deputy Governors in RBI are serving?

(A) Five

(B) Two

(C) ** Three

(D) Four

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Governor of Reserve Bank of India is the Chief Executive Officer of the Central Bank of India and the Ex-officio Chairman of its Central Board of Directors. At present there are 4 deputy governors of the RBI.

Concept note-2: -Besides the governor, the RBI has four deputy governors.

Concept note-3: -Today, we have the senior management of Reserve Bank, starting with Governor, Shri Shaktikanta Das – Deputy Governors, Shri M.K. Jain; Dr. Michael Patra; Shri M.

Concept note-4: -The Sub-committee is chaired by the Deputy Governor in charge of supervision and includes the three Deputy Governors and two Directors of the Central Board as Members.
