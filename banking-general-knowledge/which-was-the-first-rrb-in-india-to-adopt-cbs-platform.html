Which was the first RRB in India to adopt CBS platform?

(A) Prathama Bank

(B) Telangana Grameen Vikas Bank

(C) ** Rushikulya Gramya Bank

(D) Gaur Gramin Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which was the first RRB in India to adopt CBS platform? Notes: The Rushikulya Gramya Bank (RGB), which operates in south Orissa, was the first RRB to have placed all its branches on the Core Banking Solutions (CBS) platform.

Concept note-2: -In 2009 Bank sponsored Rewa Siddhi Gramin Bank becomes the first RRB in the country to achieve 100 % CBS connectivity.

Concept note-3: -Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) were set up under the terms of the 26 September 1975 ordinance and the RRB Act of 1976. First Regional Rural Bank was established on 2 October 1975. The Prathama Bank of Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh was the first RRB. Currently, there are 43 RRBs in India.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Prathama Bank.
