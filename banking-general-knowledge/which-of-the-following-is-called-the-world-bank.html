Which of the following is called the World Bank?

(A) International Monetary Fund

(B) Asian Development Bank

(C) World Trade Organisation

(D) ** International Bank for Reconstruction and Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The world’s largest development bank, IBRD provides financial products and policy advice to help countries reduce poverty and extend the benefits of sustainable growth to all of their people.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is World Bank. The International Bank of Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) is a development bank administered by the World Bank.

Concept note-3: -Together, IBRD and IDA form the World Bank, which provides financing, policy advice, and technical assistance to governments of developing countries. IDA focuses on the world’s poorest countries, while IBRD assists middle-income and creditworthy poorer countries.

Concept note-4: -The International Development Association (IDA) provides interest-free loans-called credits-and grants to governments of the poorest countries. Together, IBRD and IDA make up the World Bank.

Concept note-5: -India approached the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (IBRD), popularly known as World Bank and the International Monetary Fund (IMF), and received $7 billion as loan to manage the crisis.
