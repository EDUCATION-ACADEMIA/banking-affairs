Which of the following is the reason owing to which Government charges export duty on some export items?

(A) To get benefit from high international prices

(B) To improve the tax collection targets

(C) It is as per the international practices.

(D) ** To ensure the smooth availability of the items in India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per a finance ministry notification, exports of specified pig iron and steel products as well as iron ore pellets will attract ‘nil’ export duty.

Concept note-2: -In international trade: Tariffs. Similarly, an export duty, or export tax, is a tax imposed on commodities leaving a customs area. Finally, some countries provide export subsidies; import subsidies are rarely used.
