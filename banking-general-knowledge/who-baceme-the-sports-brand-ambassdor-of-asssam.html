EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -a) Assam state sports ambassador. i) M.S. Dhoni.

Concept note-2: -Hima Das became the first Sports Ambassador of Assam State in 2018. This was announced after she became the first Indian woman to win a gold medal at the IAAF World U-20 Athletics Championship. She secured top spot in the women’s 400 m finals, held in France.

Concept note-4: -Hima Das is an Indian sprinter from Assam. She holds the current Indian nationwide record in 400 meters with a timing of 50.79 s in Indonesia at the Asian Games, 2018. Sportswear Adidas has signed Hima Das as its brand ambassador for India.
