How much fund transfer limit of Corporate customer for Self-Linked A/c & Govt. Payments (in Lacs) in bob World Internet?

(A) 25

(B) 50

(C) 75

(D) ** Unlimited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Corporate customer can perform interbank transfer for multiple beneficiaries with single attempt. Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day. Customer can apply for Debit Card and manage other card activities like: Set daily debit card limit for ATM transactions.

Concept note-2: -If you are using Bank of Baroda NEFT services, you can transfer Rs. 10 lakhs by visiting the bank branch. The maximum amount that can be transferred through NEFT online is Rs. 5 lakhs.
