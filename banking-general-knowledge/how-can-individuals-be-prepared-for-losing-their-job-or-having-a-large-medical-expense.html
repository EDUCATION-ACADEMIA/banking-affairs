How can individuals be prepared for losing their job or having a large medical expense?

(A) Maintain a checking account with sufficient funds

(B) Pay credit card bills on time

(C) Prepare a budget

(D) ** Save at least three months of income

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fidelity suggests setting aside at least 3 to 6 months’ worth of essential expenses to protect yourself from the financial fallout of a potential job loss or the loss of other income. If you’re single but have family backup, you might be comfortable with 3 months of savings.
