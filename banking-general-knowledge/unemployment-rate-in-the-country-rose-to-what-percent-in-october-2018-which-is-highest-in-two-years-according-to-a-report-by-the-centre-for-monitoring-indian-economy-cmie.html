Unemployment rate in the country rose to what percent in October 2018 which is highest in two years, according to a report by the Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy (CMIE)?

(A) 5.60%

(B) ** 6.9%

(C) 6.30%

(D) 5.10%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India unemployment rate for 2018 was 5.33%, a 0.03% decline from 2017.

Concept note-2: -India’s unemployment rate improved from the 45-year high of 6.1% in 2017-18 to 5.8% in 2018-19, says the latest Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS), released by the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -Unemployment Rate In India State Wise 2022 According to the most recent data provided by the Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy (CMIE), India’s unemployment rate increased to 8.30% in December from 8.00% in November, which was the highest level in 16 months.

Concept note-4: -Unemployment Rate in India averaged 8.20 percent from 2018 until 2023, reaching an all time high of 23.50 percent in April of 2020 and a record low of 6.40 percent in September of 2022.
