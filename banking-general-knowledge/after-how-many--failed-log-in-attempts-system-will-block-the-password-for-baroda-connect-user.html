After how many ____ failed log in attempts, system will block the password for Baroda connect user?

(A) ** 5 times

(B) 6 times

(C) 3 Times

(D) 10 times

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After enter the correct Transaction password and Security answers/OTP the account will be Unlocked successfully. contact Toll free Helpline no-1800 22 33 44/1800 102 44 55 to get the Transaction password activated. Once Password is activated then repeat step 1 to 6.

Concept note-2: -Three Invalid Login Attempts, blocks your Internet Banking access and you shall get the message – ‘Invalid User Id or Password’, though you’re entering the correct combination of User Id and Password.

Concept note-3: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-4: -To ban or unblock your BOB ATM card, dial the toll-free number 1800-258-44-55 or 1800-102-44-55 from your registered mobile number 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
