In Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA) account how many times one can withdraw money?

(A) six

(B) two

Concept note-1: -In BSBDA, banks are required to provide free of charge minimum four withdrawals, including through ATM and other mode. Beyond four withdrawals, it is left to discretion of the banks to either offer free or charge for additional withdrawal/s.
