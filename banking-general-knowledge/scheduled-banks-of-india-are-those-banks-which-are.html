Scheduled Banks of India are those banks, which are?

(A) Included in First Schedule of RBI Act, 1934

(B) Not included in First Schedule of RBI Act, 1934

(C) ** Included in Second Schedule of RBI Act, 1934

(D) Not included in Second Schedule of RBI Act, 1934

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By definition, any bank which is listed in the 2nd schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 is considered a scheduled bank. The Schedule consists of those banks which satisfy various parameters, criteria under clause 42 of this act.
