(A) Thailand

(B) Japan

(C) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can use the bank transfer method or Paytm online checkout, in which debit card, credit card, UPI and net banking are available, to send the prepayment for purchases of Alibaba Cloud services.

Concept note-2: -Paytm leverages third-party cloud providers such as AWS, Azure, and Google Clouds and has managed to provide a seamless user experience to millions of customers on a daily basis.

Concept note-3: -China’s Alibaba Group has sold 3.3% stake in Paytm parent One97 Communications through the open market on Friday and mopped Rs 1, 378 crore. Alibaba.com Singapore E-Commerce Pvt Ltd sold 2, 14, 31, 822 shares at Rs 642.74 apiece. This was at a steep discount of 9% to Thursday’s close price of the stock.

Concept note-4: -Introducing Paytm Intelligence (Pi), the innovation and development line of business within Paytm Labs in Canada, focused on creating financial products using smart technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning.
