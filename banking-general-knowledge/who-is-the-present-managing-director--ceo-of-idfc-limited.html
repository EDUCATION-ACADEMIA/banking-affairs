Who is the present Managing Director & CEO of IDFC Limited?

(A) Vinod Rai

(B) ** Sunil Kakar

(C) Chintamani Bhagat

(D) Manish Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sunil Kakar is the Managing Director & CEO of IDFC Limited. He started his career at Bank of America where he worked for 18 years in various roles, covering Business Planning & Financial Control, Branch Administration and Operations, Market Risk Management, Project Management, and Internal Controls.

Concept note-2: -Mahendra N Shah will be Managing Director with effect from October 1, 2022, till September 30, 2023 subject to the approval of the shareholders of the Company and other applicable statutory/ regulatory approvals, IDFC Limited said. Shah is the Company Secretary & Compliance Officer of IDFC Limited since May 24, 2019.

Concept note-3: -IDFC Limited appoints Mahendra N Shah as its new managing director-BusinessToday.
