Name the person, who has been appointed as CEO and MD of Bank of Maharashtra?

(A) Ashok Kumar Pradhan

(B) SS Mallikarajuna Rao

(C) ** AS Rajeev

(D) S Harisankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Rajeev is a Fellow Member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and holds Diploma in Information System Audit from ICAI.

Concept note-2: -Shri A S Rajeev Shri. A.S. Rajeev, aged 56 years, assumed charge as Managing Director & CEO of Bank of Maharashtra on 2nd December 2018. Prior to this, he was Executive Director of Indian Bank from 22.01. 2016.

Concept note-3: -Vijayakumar A. has assumed charge as Executive Director of Bank of Maharashtra w.e.f. 10.03. 2021. Prior to assuming the position, he was Chief Vigilance Officer at Indian Overseas Bank since 01.04. 2020.

Concept note-4: -The Centre has extended the tenure of managing director (MD) and CEO of Bank of Baroda (BoB) Sanjiv Chadha for about five months till 30 June, 2023. The order comes ahead of his three-year tenure that ends on 19 January.
