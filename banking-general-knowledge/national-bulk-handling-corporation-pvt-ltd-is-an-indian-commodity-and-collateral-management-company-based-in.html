National Bulk Handling Corporation Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian commodity and collateral management company based in

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Kolkata

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Bulk Handling Corporation Pvt. (NBHC) is an Indian commodity and collateral management company based in Mumbai. NBHC is wholly owned by True North (erstwhile IVFA), a private equity major.

Concept note-2: -Average annual salary in National Bulk Handling Corporation is INR 5.8 lakhs .

Concept note-3: -NBHC Storage and Bulk Handling services has been enabling a diverse range of clients, including farmers, traders, processors, national and international corporates, commodity exchanges and banks, to meet their warehousing and logistics requirement with the great ease and at optimum costs.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Ramesh Doraiswami, Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer. Ramesh has over 34 years of leadership experience across industry verticals and diverse company cultures. He brings a strong blend of functional and general management skills to NBHC.
