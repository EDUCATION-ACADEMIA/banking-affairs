Which of the following apex body and regulator has asked banks to swap customer-related information so that the frauds and defaults may be prevented in future?

(A) Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)

(B) Indian Banks, Association (IBA)

(C) Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)

(D) ** Reserve Bank of India (RBI)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is NABARD. It is an apex regulatory body for the overall regulation and licensing of regional rural banks and apex cooperative banks in India.

Concept note-2: -Regulator and supervisor of the financial system: Prescribes broad parameters of banking operations within which the country’s banking and financial system functions. Objective: maintain public confidence in the system, protect depositors’ interest and provide cost-effective banking services to the public.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is the Reserve Bank of India. Reserve Bank of India is the apex banking institution of India that regulates the financial sector.
