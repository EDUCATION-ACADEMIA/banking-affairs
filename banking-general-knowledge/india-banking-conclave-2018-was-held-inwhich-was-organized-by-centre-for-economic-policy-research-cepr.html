India Banking Conclave 2018 was held in____which was organized by Centre for Economic Policy Research (CEPR).

(A) Mumbai

(B) Pune

(C) Chennai

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The banking system of India consists of the central bank (Reserve Bank of India-RBI), commercial banks, cooperative banks and development banks (development finance institutions). These institutions, which provide a meeting ground for the savers and the investors, form the core of India’s financial sector.

Concept note-2: -The effort was sup-ported by country’s official think tank, NITI Aayog as the knowledge partner.

Concept note-3: -Financial Security: Indian banking system provides financial security to the people by providing loans at competitive rates, paying reliable remittance services, etc. It helps people save their money and invest it in different financial instruments like Government securities, long-term bonds, etc.
