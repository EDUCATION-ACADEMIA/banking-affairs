India and____recently held their first ever Dialogue on Intellectual Property in Delhi to deepen bilateral strategic cooperation on IP policy.

(A) France

(B) Russia

(C) ** The USA

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian government approved its first Intellectual Property Rights Policy in May 2016.

Concept note-2: -On February 24, 2022, the Delhi High Court Intellectual Property Rights Division Rules, 2022 and the High Court of Delhi Rules Governing Patent Suits, 2022 were published in the Official Gazette. All IPR lawsuits, cases, procedures, or disputes before the IPD of the Delhi High Court are subject to the Rules.

Concept note-3: -The Union Cabinet in its meeting dated 19.02. 2020 gave the approval for signing the MoU with United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) in the field of IP Cooperation.

Concept note-4: -On 13 May 2016, the Union Cabinet approved India’s first Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) policy for awareness, creation, commercialization, protection and enforcement of Intellectual Property (IP) in India.
