What is the repayment period for the Home loan for a NRI?

(A) ** 30 Years

(B) 40 Years

(C) 60 Years

(D) 65 Years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Loans available for both salaried and self-employed individuals. Flexible repayment option of up to 30 years.

Concept note-2: -The answer is yes, you can obtain a loan for a home that is 30 years old. But it must be in good shape, in a suitable location, and you have a strong credit record. Houses are financed depending on the worth of the home, not only its age.

Concept note-3: -A home loan is usually repaid over a period of 10 to 30 years in EMIs (Equated Monthly Instalments) as per the amortisation schedule decided by the lender.
