____ pools money from investors and invests in stocks, bonds, shares etc.

(A) a bank

(B) a factor

(C) ** a mutual fund

(D) a company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A mutual fund is a pool of money managed by a professional Fund Manager. It is a trust that collects money from a number of investors who share a common investment objective and invests the same in equities, bonds, money market instruments and/or other securities.

Concept note-2: -Pooled funds are investment vehicles such as mutual funds, commingled funds, group trusts, real estate funds, limited partnership funds, and alternative investments. The distinguishing feature of a pooled fund is that a number of retirement boards or investors contribute money to the fund.

Concept note-3: -A mutual fund is a company that pools money from many investors and invests the money in securities such as stocks, bonds, and short-term debt. The combined holdings of the mutual fund are known as its portfolio.

Concept note-4: -ETFs-Another Way To Diversify Similar to mutual funds, ETFs allow investors to pool their money when investing in stocks, bonds or other assets. However, ETFs differ from mutual funds in that they are traded on the national stock exchange at market prices.

Concept note-5: -The investment pools aggregate, or pool, donations from many different Giving Accounts and invest those assets in an underlying investment(s).
