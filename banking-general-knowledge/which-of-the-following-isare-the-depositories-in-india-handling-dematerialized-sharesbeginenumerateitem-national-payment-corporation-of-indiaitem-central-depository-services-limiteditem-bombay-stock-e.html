Which of the following is/are the depositories in India handling dematerialized shares?

1. :

National payment corporation of India

2. :

Central depository services limited

3. :

Bombay stock exchange (BSE)

(A) ** Only 1 & 2

(B) Only 1

(C) Only 2

(D) Only 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, there are two depositories: National Securities Depositories Ltd (NSDL) and Central Securities Depositories Ltd (CDSL). Both the depositories hold your financial securities, like shares and bonds, in dematerialised form and facilitate trading in stock exchanges.
