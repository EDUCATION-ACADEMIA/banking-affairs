Ujjivan Small Finance Bank launched an overdraft (OD) facility for micro & small enterprise (MSE) customers. OD facility is offered to all MSEs with a turnover of Rs____lakh or more.

(A) Rs 20 lakh

(B) Rs 40 lakh

(C) Rs 10 lakh

(D) ** Rs 50 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Ujjivan Small Finance Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary of Ujjivan Financial Services Limited, has launched Overdraft (OD) facility for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSE) customers across all bank branches nationally.

Concept note-2: -CGTMSE Overdraft Facility Rate of interest (ROI Range ): 13%-15% p.a.

Concept note-3: -MSE Loans: Ujjivan SFB offers MSE loans from ₹ 3 lakhs to ₹ 1500 Lakhs for any small or medium enterprise business need.
