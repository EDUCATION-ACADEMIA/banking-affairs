What does N stands for in NUUP?

(A) Network

(B) Neutral

(C) Net

(D) ** National

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -[National Unified USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Services Data) Platform] The NUUP service is a USSD based Mobile Banking service from NPCI using which financial and non-financial transactions can be done using mobile phone without mobile internet connection. This service is popularized as *99# by NPCI.

Concept note-2: -Option C. Explanation: NUUP (National Unified USSD Platform) is a USSD based mobile banking service from NPCI that brings together all the Banks and Telecom Service Providers.

Concept note-3: -NUUP the acronym stands for National Unified USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) Platform introduced by National Payment Corporation of India for accessing Mobile Banking services by customers across various Telecom service providers. 2.

Concept note-4: -It is a USSD based mobile banking service offered on a short code *99#, is currently available with 41 banks & all the GSM service providers and can be accessed in 12 languages, including Hindi & English.
