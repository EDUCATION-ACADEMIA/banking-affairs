Which of the following is NOT associated with climate change and/or global warming?

(A) ** Basel norms

(B) Bali Meet 2007

(C) Copenhagen Conference

(D) Kyoto Protocol

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer: The character which is not associated with global warming is a. Decreasing sea levels. One of the major effects of global warming is the increase in sea level due to the melting of the polar ice caps.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Increased agricultural productivity worldwide.

Concept note-3: -The consequences of climate change now include, among others, intense droughts, water scarcity, severe fires, rising sea levels, flooding, melting polar ice, catastrophic storms and declining biodiversity.

Concept note-4: -Higher temperatures are worsening many types of disasters, including storms, heat waves, floods, and droughts. A warmer climate creates an atmosphere that can collect, retain, and unleash more water, changing weather patterns in such a way that wet areas become wetter and dry areas drier.
