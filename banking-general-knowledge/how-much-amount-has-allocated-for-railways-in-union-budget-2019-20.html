How much amount has allocated for Railways in union budget 2019-20?

(A) ** Rs 64, 587 crore

(B) Rs 1, 58, 658 crore

(C) Rs 17, 500 crore

(D) Rs 12, 340 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Now, Rs 2, 40, 000 crore has been provided to the railways in the financial year 2023-24. In 2019-20, UP had got an allocation of Rs 8576 crore and in 2020-21, Rs 12696 crore was provided to the state in 2021-22, and Rs 14761 crore in 2022-23.

Concept note-2: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on Wednesday allocated Rs 2.40 lakh crore for the Railways for 2023-24. The outlay is Rs 1 lakh crore more than the Rs 1.40 lakh crore allocated to the Railways last fiscal.

Concept note-3: -The net revenue expenditure of Railways is placed at Rs 2, 65, 000 crore in the Budget Estimate (BE) 2023-24 as against Rs 2, 42, 892.77 crore in Revised Estimate (RE) 2022-23.

Concept note-4: -Indian Railways Budget 2023: Govt outlines highest ever Rs 2.40 lakh crore boost for Indian Railways in Budget-The Economic Times.
