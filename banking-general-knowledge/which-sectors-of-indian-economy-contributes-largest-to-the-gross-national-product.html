Which sectors of Indian Economy contributes largest to the gross national product?

(A) Primary sector

(B) Secondary sector

(C) ** Tertiary sector

(D) Public sector

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The service sector, also called the tertiary sector contributes most of the GDP in India.

Concept note-2: -The services sector contributes the largest percentage towards the GNP. The service sector accounts for 53.66 % of the total of India’s GNP. Industry comes second with a share of 31.12 %. The agriculture sector contributes the lowest with the share of 15.11% of GNP.

Concept note-3: -The tertiary sector contributes more to India’s GDP (more than 50 %). As the tertiary sector provides services to all current firms and final consumers, the tertiary sector is often known as the service industry.
