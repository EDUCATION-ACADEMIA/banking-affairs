Customer can use missed call facility maximum ____ in a day, system will not respond thereafter.

(A) ** 3 times

(B) 5 times

(C) 7 times

(D) 10 times

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Leveraging technology, Bank of Baroda introduces missed call facility. All customers who have registered their mobile number can get the balance inquiry of accounts by just leaving us a missed call.

Concept note-2: -Send SMS “MODBAL” to “919220055222” to get available balance and MOD balance as response through SMS.

Concept note-3: -Yes, for your convenience, Cosmos offers you Missed call services to enable you to know your account balance instantly at any point in time. All you must do is ring up 90290 13793 from your registered mobile number. Know your Balance = 1 Missed Call!
