Which of the following countries has the largest Mutual Fund market in the World?

(A) ** USA

(B) Japan

(C) China

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which of the following countries has the largest Mutual Fund market in the World? Notes: The country with the largest mutual fund industry is United States. It has an amount of $18.9 trillion.

Concept note-2: -With a whopping USD 26.96 trillion in total net assets, the US mutual fund industry remained the largest in the world in 2021. The United States accounted for almost more than 40% of the global mutual fund market.

Concept note-3: -The United States has a very large mutual fund market. There are literally hundreds of different fund families and thousands of individual funds available, and all of them are vying for your investment dollars.
