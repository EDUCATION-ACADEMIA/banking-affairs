Who is the Ex-Officio chairmain of Niti Aayog?

(A) The President

(B) The Finance Minister

(C) ** The Prime Minister

(D) The Chief of CSO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr Parameswaran Iyer is the Chief Executive Officer of NITI Aayog.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Who is the ex-officio chairman of NITI Aayog in India? Notes: The Prime Minister of India serves as the ex-officio chairperson of the NITI (National Institution for Transforming India) Aayog.

Concept note-3: -Ex-Niti Aayog Vice Chairman Rajiv Kumar predicts 6% growth in 2023-24, decodes India’s economic trend.

Concept note-4: -The Prime Minister of India is ex officio Chairman of NITI Aayog. Other ex officio members of NITI Aayog are the Minister of Home Affairs, the Minister of Finance, the Minister of Railways, and the Minister of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare.

Concept note-5: -Prime Minister is the Ex-officio chairman of the Planning Commission of the India. Q. Who is the Chairman of the Planning Commission of India ?
