Which Bank’s headquarter is located in Mumbai?

(A) Allahabad Bank

(B) ** Union Bank of India

(C) United Bank of India

(D) Punjab and Sindh Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Bank of India is one of the leading public sector banks of the country. The Bank is a listed entity and the Government of India holds 83.49 percent in Bank’s total share capital. The Bank, having its headquarters at Mumbai (India), was registered on November 11, 1919 as a limited company.

Concept note-2: -Union Bank Of India from Union Bank Of India group, specialized as Banking and has 123 branches in Mumbai.
