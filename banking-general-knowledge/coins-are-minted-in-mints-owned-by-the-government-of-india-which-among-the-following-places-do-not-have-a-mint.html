Coins are minted in mints owned by the Government of India. Which among the following places do not have a mint?

(A) ** Salboni

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Noida

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among the given options, Bangalore is the place where there is no coin mint.
