India’s first banking robot Lakshmi launched by____in Chennai.

(A) Axis Bank

(B) South Indian Bank

(C) ICICI Bank

(D) ** City Union Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lakshmi is currently based at CUB’s T-Nagar branch in Chennai and is currently the only version of a humanoid banker that the bank has got. Depending on the performance, around 100 such robots could appear at the CUB’s branch over the next 9-12 months.

Concept note-2: -CUB Lakshmi-India’s first Robot response service in a Bank.

Concept note-3: -Tang Yu, the first executive robot in human history, was designated as the virtual CEO for the company’s principal subsidiary, Fujian NetDragon Websoft.

Concept note-4: -IRA, which stands for Intelligent Robotic Assistant, will help branch staff in servicing customers. With this launch, HDFC Bank becomes the first bank in the country to introduce a humanoid for customer service.
