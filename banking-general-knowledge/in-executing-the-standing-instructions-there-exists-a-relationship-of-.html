In executing the standing instructions, there exists a relationship of ____

(A) debtor and creditor

(B) Trustee and Beneficiary

(C) Bailee and Bailor

(D) ** Agent and Principal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The relationship between the banker and the customer is therefore that of a creditor and a debtor.

Concept note-2: -Debtor and Creditor Relationship-The relation of banker and customer is primarily that of debtor and creditor. But who is what at a particular moment depends on the balance of the account of the customer. If the account shows a credit balance, the banker will be a debtor and the customer a creditor.

Concept note-3: -b) Trustee and Beneficiary: When a cheque or a bill of exchange is deposited with a bank for collection, the bank becomes a trustee for the cheque or bill till it is collected.
