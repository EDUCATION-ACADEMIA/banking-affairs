Which of the following is one of the most effective and least expensive ways to market a business?

(A) In-theater advertising

(B) ** Effective customer service

(C) Glossy, colorful, professional brochures

(D) One-on-one sales calls

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The two surefire marketing strategies for small businesses – cooperation marketing and social proof – are designed to bring you more for less: maximum traffic and customers, at minimal spend. Cooperation marketing helps drive traffic to your website by partnering with other businesses.

Concept note-2: -Which of the following is something a company must NOT do in order to provide excellent customer service? Develop the necessary skills.

Concept note-3: -There are four key principles of good customer service: It’s personalized, competent, convenient, and proactive. These factors have the biggest influence on the customer experience.

Concept note-4: -What is the main reason that more businesses are not actually providing excellent customer service? They don’t care about serving customers. They don’t know how to get started. They don’t like people.
