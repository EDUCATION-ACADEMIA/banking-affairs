Financial services and products generally offered by banks and credit unions include:

(A) Real estate listings

(B) ** Savings and loans

(C) Legal services

(D) Business plans

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Banks and similar business entities, such as thrifts or credit unions, offer the most commonly recognized and frequently used financial services: checking and savings accounts, home mortgages, and other types of loans for retail and commercial customers.

Concept note-2: -The 4 most common types of financial institutions are commercial banks, brokerage firms, insurance companies, investment banks.

Concept note-3: -The financial services sector is comprised of banking, mortgages, credit cards, payment services, tax preparation and planning, accounting, and investing.
