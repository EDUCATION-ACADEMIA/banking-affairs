EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mandatory deductions: Federal and state income tax, FICA taxes, and wage garnishments. Post-tax deductions: Garnishments, Roth IRA retirement plans and charitable donations. Voluntary deductions: Life insurance, job-related expenses and retirement plans.

Concept note-2: -If you make ₹ 2, 500, 000 a year living in India, you will be taxed ₹ 885, 000. That means that your net pay will be ₹ 1, 615, 000 per year, or ₹ 134, 583 per month. Your average tax rate is 35.4% and your marginal tax rate is 43.2%.
