____ is the exercise between India and Thailand

(A) Nomadic Elephent

(B) ** Maitree

(C) Sampriti

(D) Yudh Abhyas

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exercise Maitree is a bilateral exercise between the Indian Army and the Royal Thai Army, started off in 2006. It is an annual event between the troops of the armies of both countries.

Concept note-2: -The Joint Military Training Exercise Bold Kurukshetra is a series of joint military exercises between India & Singapore. The four-day joint training emphasized interoperability and the execution of joint tactical operations in mechanized warfare.

Concept note-3: -Cobra Gold (Thai: ) refers to multi-national Indo-Pacific military exercises held in Thailand. They are among the largest annual exercises of their kind.

Concept note-4: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-5: -Mitra Shakti Exercise is a bilateral joint military exercise between the armies of India and Sri Lanka. It is also called the India-Sri Lanka Joint Training Exercise. It is one of the important joint military exercises that India conducts with other nations. UPSC & State PSC History Test Series.
