Which type of deposits gives highest rate of interest?

(A) Current deposit

(B) ** Fixed deposit

(C) Recurring deposit

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fixed Deposits (FD) offers the highest rate of interest, ranging from 4 to 7.25 percent. So, the correct answer is “Option C”. Note:Recurring Deposits has higher interest than Saving Deposits. Fixed Deposits has higher interest than Savings, Recurring and current deposits.

Concept note-2: -Fixed Deposits (FD) are investment instruments offered by banks and non-banking financial companies, where one can deposit money for a higher rate of interest than savings accounts. These deposits, hence, offer the highest rate of interest, ranging from 4 to 7.

Concept note-3: -Corporate and Other Fixed Deposits Offering High ROI If you choose a good Company FD scheme, you will generally earn more on your investment than bank FDs as these schemes offer the highest interest rate on FD.
