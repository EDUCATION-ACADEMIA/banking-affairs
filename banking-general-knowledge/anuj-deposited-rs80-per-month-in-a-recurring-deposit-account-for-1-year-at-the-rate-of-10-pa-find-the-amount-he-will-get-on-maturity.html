Anuj deposited Rs.80 per month in a recurring deposit account for 1 year at the rate of 10% p.a. Find the amount he will get on maturity.

(A) Rs. 1000

(B) ** Rs. 1012

(C) Rs. 2012

(D) Rs. 2000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rs. 150 for 8 months is 150×8=1200. Therefore, the maturity value Amit will have at the end of 8 months is Rs. 1236.

Concept note-2: -A Recurring Deposit Account of Rs 1, 200 per Month Has a Maturity Value of Rs 12, 440. If the Rate of Interest is 8% and the Interest is Calculated-Mathematics. A recurring deposit account of Rs 1, 200 per month has a maturity value of Rs 12, 440.
