Concept note-1: -It is a counter manned by a Teller where a Transaction Processing Device(TPD), similar to a PoS machine, is attached to the terminal. Customer swipes the Shopping cum Debit Card, selects a particular transaction and enters the amount and the PIN.

Concept note-2: -SBI has launched paperless banking system called ‘The Green Channel’ counter at all its branches across the city. Here, customers of the bank can transact without using any pay in-slip, withdrawal forms, cheque books or remittance forms.
