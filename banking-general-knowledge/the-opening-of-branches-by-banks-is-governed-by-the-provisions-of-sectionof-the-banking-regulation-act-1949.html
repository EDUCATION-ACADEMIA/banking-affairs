The opening of branches by banks is governed by the provisions of Section____of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949.

(A) Section 44

(B) Section 18

(C) Section 27

(D) ** Section 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The mandate and related documentation which forms the basis for effecting payments for such transactions carried out over the ATMs should be settled bilaterally between the bank and customers and the rights and obligations of each party should be clearly stated in the mandate and should be valid in the court of law.

Concept note-2: -In terms of these provisions, banks without the prior approval of the RBI, cannot open a new place of business in India or abroad or change otherwise than within the same city, town or village, the location of the existing place of business.
