What is full form of NBFC as used in financial sector?

(A) New Banking Finance Corporation

(B) ** Non Banking Finance Company

(C) New Business Formation Company

(D) Net Banking & Finance Cooperative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of nonbank financial institutions include insurance firms, venture capitalists, currency exchanges, some microloan organizations, and pawn shops.

Concept note-2: -What is a NBFC? A Non-Banking Financial Corporation is a company that is registered under the Companies Act, 1956 of the Companies Act, 2013 and is involved in the lending business, hire-purchase, leasing, insurance business, receiving deposits in some cases, chit funds, stocks, and shares acquisition, etc.
