State Bank of India is an Indian multinational, public sector banking and financial services statutory body. Who is present Chairperson of SBI?

(A) ** Rajnish Kumar

(B) Arundhati Bhattacharya

(C) Pratip Chaudhuri

(D) Om Prakash Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Arundhati Bhattacharya. Arundhati Bhattacharya is the first woman to be the Chairperson of the State Bank of India (7 October 2013 – 6 October 2017). She was succeeded by Rajnish Kumar.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Dinesh Kumar Khara.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Smt. Arundhati Bhattacharya.
