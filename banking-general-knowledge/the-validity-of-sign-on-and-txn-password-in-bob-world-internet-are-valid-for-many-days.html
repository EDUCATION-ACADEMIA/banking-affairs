The validity of Sign on and Txn password in bob World Internet are valid for many days?

(A) 360 days & 365 days

(B) ** 365 days & 360 days

(C) 360 days for both

(D) 365 days for both

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -4-digit Transaction PIN is used to authorize any transaction (both financial and non-financial) carried out in mobile banking app – bob World. Never share your PIN with anyone. Bank or its employees will never ask for your PIN. You can create your own transaction PIN during activation of mobile banking.

Concept note-2: -The system will prompt for setting the passwords (Sign-on or/and transaction password). 8. On successful registration, a success message would be displayed and the account details would be available after 24 hours. For Corporate customers, please contact your branch and submit request for internet banking.

Concept note-3: -1) Visit BOB Internet banking homepage www.bobibanking.com and select Retail Users. 3) The following screen will appear after clicking the link. 4) On entering the correct details, an OTP would be sent on the registered mobile number of the customer.
