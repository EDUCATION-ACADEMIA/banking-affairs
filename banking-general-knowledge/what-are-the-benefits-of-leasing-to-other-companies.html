What are the benefits of leasing to other companies?

(A) Interest Revenue

(B) High Residual Values

(C) Tax Incentives

(D) ** All of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One advantage of a financial lease is that: it has a shorter maturity than term loans. it never appears as a liability on the balance sheet. it eliminate the needs to make periodic payments.

Concept note-2: -By definition: “Financial leasing companies engage in financing the purchase of concrete assets. Though leasing company is the legal owner of the goods, the ownership and possession is effectively conveyed to the lessee, who earns all benefits, costs, and risks linked to ownership of the assets.”
