Baroda Paypoint allows which modes for payment:

(A) Card & Net banking only

(B) ** Card (Visa, Master, Rupay), Net Banking, UPI, NEFT/RTGS

(C) Card (Visa, Master, Rupay, Ma setro), Net Banking, UPI, NEFT/RTGS

(D) Card (Visa, Master, Rupay, Ma setro, Diner & Amex), Net Banking, UPI, NEFT/RTGS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Users can choose any method of payment according to their convenience and pay outstanding bills as per the due date. The online payment modes available to Bank of Baroda credit card holders can use the following offline payment options-Cheques, Auto Debits, Drafts or Cash payments at branches.

Concept note-2: -Bank of Baroda presents Bhim Baroda Pay-A Unified Payments Interface (UPI) application that lets you transfer funds from any bank account using a Virtual Payment Address (VPA). No need to remember beneficiary details like account number, IFSC, Mobile Number, MMID etc.

Concept note-3: -While making payment through NEFT, you are requested to Use IFSC Code as BARB0COLABA and 16 digit credit card number as Beneficiary a/c No. (Kindly mention name and registered mobile number in remark column).

Concept note-4: -Login to the bob World app using your mobile number and login PIN. On your mobile screen, look for the Fund transfer tab. Under that TAB Go to Within Bank in Self Link Account. Select the Debit account, in to option select the Loan account which will be auto fetched.
