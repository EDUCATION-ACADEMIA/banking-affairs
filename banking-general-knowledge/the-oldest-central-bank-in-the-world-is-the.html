(A) Reserve Bank of India

(B) ** Bank of England

(C) U S Federal Reserve

(D) State Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Riksbank is thus the world’s oldest central bank. Here you can read about the history of the Riksbank.

Concept note-2: -A family business since 1672 C. Hoare & Co. is the UK’s oldest privately-owned bank, owned continuously by the Hoare family for 12 generations.

Concept note-3: -1) Sweden (Sveriges Riksbank): Founded in 1668, the Bank of Sweden or “Sveriges Riksbank” is the oldest central bank in the world.

Concept note-4: -Berenberg bank is the second oldest in the world after Banca Monte Dei Paschi di Siena, and it’s also the world’s oldest merchant bank.

Concept note-5: -The Bank of England is the central bank of the United Kingdom. We’re different to a bank that you would come across in the high street. That means we don’t hold accounts or make loans to the public. We issue banknotes that you spend in shops.
