Consider the following statements:

1. :

Regional Rural Banks grant direct loans and advances to marginal farmers and rural artisans.

2. :

NABARD is responsible for laying down policies and to oversee the operations of the RRBs.

3. :

RRB is not responsible for laying down policies.

Which of the statements given above is/are correct?

(A) 1 only

(B) 2 only

(C) ** Both 1 and 2

(D) Neither 1 nor 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) and Land Development Banks (LDBs) provide direct credit assistance to rural households.

Concept note-2: -Medium-term Conversion. NABARD provides medium-term credit limits for conversion of short-term crop loans advanced for financing seasonal agricultural operations (SAO) to State Co-operative Banks and Regional Rural Banks for providing relief to the farmers whose crops have been damaged due to natural calamities.
