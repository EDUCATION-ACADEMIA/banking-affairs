Which of the following taxes is not levied by the Government of India?

(A) Income Tax

(B) ** Professional Tax

(C) Excise Duty

(D) Dividend Distribution Tax

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Excise Tax. Excise tax is not levied by the Union Government.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is B Land revenue Which of the following is not a Central Government Tax? Q.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Custom Duty. Custom Duty tax is not included in Goods and Services Tax (GST).

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Stamp duties. Stamp duties are levied by Union and collected and appropriated by States.

Concept note-5: -Professional tax is a direct tax that is deducted from your gross salary by your employer. This tax is levied by the state government and thus can vary depending on the state you live in. The maximum limit of which you can be charged is Rs 2500. The tax is calculated based on the slabs.
