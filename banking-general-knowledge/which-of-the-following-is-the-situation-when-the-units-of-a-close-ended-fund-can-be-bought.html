Which of the following is the situation when the units of a close-ended fund can be bought?

(A) ** At the launch

(B) At any time

(C) At redemption

(D) Depends upon the fund type.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During the launch or New Fund Offer (NFO) period, investors can invest in units of a close-ended mutual fund. After which, the units are listed on the stock exchange and can be traded like regular stocks. Once the NFO period closes, the units of a close-ended mutual fund can no longer be purchased or redeemed.
