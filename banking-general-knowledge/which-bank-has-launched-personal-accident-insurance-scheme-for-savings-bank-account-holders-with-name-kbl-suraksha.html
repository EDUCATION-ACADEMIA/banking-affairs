Which Bank has launched personal accident insurance scheme for savings bank account-holders with name KBL Suraksha?

(A) City Union Bank

(B) ** Karnataka Bank

(C) Kotak Mahindra Bank

(D) IDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A) Karnataka Bank is a Corporate Agent and also a Master Policy Holder of KBL Suraksha. B) The Underwriting of risk, premium dues and payment of claims is as per the policy terms & conditions of the insurer.

Concept note-2: -“KBL – Surkasha” is a unique Personal Accidednt Policy providing financial securities to the family members of our SB Account Customers. M/s Universal Sompo General Insurance Co Ltd, (M/s USGICL) is providing this insurance cover for accidental death.

Concept note-3: -RULES FOR THE PRADHAN MANTRI SURAKSHA BIMA YOJANA (With effect from 1.6.2022) DETAILS OF THE SCHEME: PMSBY is an Accident Insurance Scheme offering accidental death and disability cover for death or disability on account of an accident. It would be a one-year cover, renewable from year to year.

Concept note-4: -The Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana is a government-backed accident insurance scheme that covers accidental death, permanent disability, and partial disablement. Individuals between 18 years and 70 years are eligible to apply for this scheme.
