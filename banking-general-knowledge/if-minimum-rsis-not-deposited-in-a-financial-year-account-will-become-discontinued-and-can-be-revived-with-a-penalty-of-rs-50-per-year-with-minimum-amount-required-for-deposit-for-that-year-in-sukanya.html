If minimum Rs____is not deposited in a financial year, account will become discontinued and can be revived with a penalty of Rs 50/-per year with minimum amount required for deposit for that year in Sukanya Samriddhi Accounts?

(A) Rs 2000/

(B) ** Rs 1000/

(C) Rs 3000/

(D) Rs 5000/-

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the minimum annual deposit amount required for Sukanya Samriddhi Scheme? The minimum deposit amount required per annum is Rs.250.

Concept note-2: -Minimum deposit ₹ 250/-Maximum deposit ₹ 1.5 Lakh in a financial year.

Concept note-3: -You can make any number of deposits in a year. But, if you fail to make the minimum investment in a given year, your account will be discontinued. You can reactivate the account though by paying a penalty of ₹50 and submitting the minimum deposit amount.

Concept note-4: -Eligibility for Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana The Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana account can be opened in the name of a girl child, by her parents or legal guardians, any time before the girl child attains 10 years of age.
