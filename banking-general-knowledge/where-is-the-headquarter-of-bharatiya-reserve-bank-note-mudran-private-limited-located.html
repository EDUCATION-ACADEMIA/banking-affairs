Where is the headquarter of Bharatiya Reserve Bank Note Mudran Private Limited located?

(A) Chennai

(B) New Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Bangalore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No. 3 & 4, Ist Phase, Ist Stage, BTM Layout Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore – 560 029 Karnataka, India.

Concept note-2: -Two of the currency note printing presses are owned by the Government of India and two are owned by the Reserve Bank, through its wholly owned subsidiary, the Bharatiya Reserve Bank Note Mudran Ltd. (BRBNML). The government owned presses are at Nasik (Western India) and Dewas (Central India).

Concept note-3: -The BRBNMPL has been registered as a Private Limited Company under the Companies Act 1956 with its Registered and Corporate Office situated at Bengaluru. The company manages 2 Presses one at Mysore in Karnataka and the other at Salboni in West Bengal.

Concept note-4: -Dr. Chakrabarty is also Chairman of the Bharatiya Reserve Bank Note Mudran Pvt. Ltd.

Concept note-5: -Its Email address is cobangalorebrbnmpl.co.in and its registered address is NO 3 & 4, 1ST STAGE, 1ST PHASE, B.T.M. LAYOUT, BANNERGHATTA ROAD, BANGALORE Bangalore KA 560029 IN .
