Parking and visitor management is a special feature of

(A) ** Baroda Paypoint Colony World

(B) Baroda Paypoint Mobile eKart

(C) Baroda Paypoint Get-eKart

(D) Baroda e Gateway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda provides a facility, to receive payment online from any debit/credit card, UPI (VPA & QR code), wallet, net banking (through aggregator) of more than 34+ banks, called ‘Baroda e-Gateway’ (Internet Payment Gateway), which is a safe, secure, and trusted payment solution for e-Commerce/online business.

Concept note-2: -Users can choose any method of payment according to their convenience and pay outstanding bills as per the due date. The online payment modes available to Bank of Baroda credit card holders can use the following offline payment options-Cheques, Auto Debits, Drafts or Cash payments at branches.

Concept note-3: -The internet payment gateway is the infrastructure that allows an organizations to accept credit card/ Debit card/ e-Banking/ IMPS etc payments over their web portals/websites. A payment process in IPG is a transaction between the users and organizations, with transfer of funds in between following accounts.
