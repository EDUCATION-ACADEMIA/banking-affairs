What is the limit on the amount of compensation as specified in an Award?

(A) Rs. 15 lakh

(B) ** Rs. 20 lakh

(C) Rs. 18 lakh

(D) Rs. 10 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Is there any limit on the amount of compensation as specified in an Award? The compensation amount, if any, which can be awarded by the NBFC Ombudsman, for any loss suffered by the complainant, is limited to the amount arising directly out of the act or omission of the NBFC or rupees one million, whichever is lower.

Concept note-2: -The Banking Ombudsman may award compensation not exceeding ₹ 1 lakh (₹ One Hundred Thousand) to the complainant for mental agony and harassment.

Concept note-3: -However, for any consequential loss suffered by the complainant, the Ombudsman shall have the power to provide a compensation up to Rupees 20 lakh, in addition to, up to Rupees One lakh for the loss of the complainant’s time, expenses incurred and for harassment/mental anguish suffered by the complainant.

Concept note-4: -Compensation not exceeding rupees hundred thousand can also be awarded by the Banking Ombudsman to the complainant for loss of time, expenses incurred as also, harassment and mental anguish suffered by the complainant.
