Sending and receiving messages without using words, for example:facial expressions and posture is called:

(A) Communication Skills

(B) ** Non-Verbal Communication

(C) Verbal Communication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nonverbal communication (NVC) is the transmission of messages or signals through a nonverbal platform such as eye contact, facial expressions, gestures, posture, use of objects and body language.

Concept note-2: -Nonverbal communication refers to the ways in which beings convey information about their emotions, needs, intentions, attitudes, and thoughts without the use of verbal language.

Concept note-3: -Nonverbal communication is a message shared in an unspoken manner, such as through facial expressions, which allows for an individual to communicate a message through the use of their eyebrows, eyes, mouth, and facial muscles. Smiling, scowling, rolling eyes, and eye contact are a few examples of facial expressions.

Concept note-4: -Posture, facial expressions, and eye contact are examples of nonverbal messages. We all use these cues in daily conversation, even involuntarily. Nonverbal communication also involves the way we present ourselves to others.

Concept note-5: -not using words, or not relating to the use of words: Body language is a potent form of non-verbal communication.
