What is the total number of stock exchanges in India?

(A) 21

(B) 22

(C) ** 23

(D) 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are 23 stock exchanges in India. Among them, two are national-level stock exchanges namely Bombay Stock exchange (BSE) and National Stock Exchange (NSE). The rest 21 are Regional Stock Exchanges (RSEs).

Concept note-2: -In India, there are 23 stock exchanges. A stock exchange is a floor or platform that houses a market where buyers and sellers convene to trade stocks during specific hours of business days. Two of them are national stock exchanges, the National Stock Exchange (NSE) and the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).
