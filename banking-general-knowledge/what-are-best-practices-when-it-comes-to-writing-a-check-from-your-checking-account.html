What are best practices when it comes to writing a check from your checking account?

(A) ** Writing in ink

(B) ** Recording the check in your check register

(C) ** Neat handwriting so there is no confusion about the amount being paid

(D) Waiting to deposit money into your account until when you expect the check will be cashed

(E) Do not sign the check so that no one can copy your signature

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After you write the check, remember to write the date, check number, payee, and the amount in the check register located at the front of the checkbook.

Concept note-2: -The safest and easiest way to write a check to yourself is to put your name on the “Pay to the order of” line and fill out the date, amount, and signature line as usual. At your bank, endorse the back of the check in the endorsement area and show your ID to the teller.
