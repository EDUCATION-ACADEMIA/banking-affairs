Moody’s Investors Service estimated that India will grow____in the calendar year 2018 and____in 2019, amid signs of economic recovery from the impact of demonetisation and GST.

(A) 7.2% and 7.3%

(B) 7.4% and 7.5%

(C) 7.7% and 7.9%

(D) ** 7.6% and 7.5%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Moody’s Cuts India’s 2022 GDP Growth Forecast to 7%, Sees 2023 Growth at 4.8%

Concept note-2: -Moody’s on Friday slashed India’s GDP growth projection for 2022 to 7 per cent on expectation that global slowdown and high domestic interest rates would dampen economic momentum.

Concept note-3: -Moody’s Investors Service on Friday lowered India’s economic growth projection for 2022 to 7% from 7.7% earlier.

Concept note-4: -Moody’s had cut India’s growth forecast for 2018 to 7.3% in May from 7.5%. The graphic presentation of the growth estimates in the latest report still shows India’s growth at 7.3% for 2018 but the commentary pegs growth at around 7.5%.
