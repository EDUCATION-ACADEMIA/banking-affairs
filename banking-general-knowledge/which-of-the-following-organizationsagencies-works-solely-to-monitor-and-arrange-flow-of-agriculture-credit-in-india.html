Which of the following organizations/agencies works solely to monitor and arrange flow of agriculture credit in India?

(A) ** NABARD

(B) SIDBI

(C) RBI

(D) SEBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NABARD-National Bank For Agriculture And Rural Development.

Concept note-2: -The major functions of NABARD include promotion and development, refinancing, financing, planning, monitoring and supervision.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Facilitating credit flow for the promotion and development of agriculture. The full form of NABARD is National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development. NABARD was established on 12th July 1982.

Concept note-4: -Agricultural credit refers to one of several credit vehicles used to finance agricultural transactions such as a loan, note, bill of exchange, or a bankerâ€™s acceptance. Financing is specially adapted to the specific financial needs of farmers.
