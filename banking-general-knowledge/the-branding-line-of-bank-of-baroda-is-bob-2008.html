(A) International Bank of India

(B) ** India’s International Bank

(C) India’s Multinational Bank

(D) World’s local Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda is called as India’s International Bank because it has as many as 107 foreign branches (the second most after SBI) spread across 24 countries.

Concept note-2: -The extensive worldwide network of branches of Bank of Baroda offers international banking services. Select branches of the bank that are well equipped to handle the business of correspondent banking.
