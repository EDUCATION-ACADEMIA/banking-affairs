Which of the following institution is considered as ‘ soft loan window’?

(A) IMF

(B) IBRD

(C) ** IDA

(D) IFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The International Development Association (IDA) is known as the soft loan window of the World Bank.
