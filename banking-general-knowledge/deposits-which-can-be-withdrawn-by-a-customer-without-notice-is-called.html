Deposits which can be withdrawn by a customer without notice is called?

(A) Time Deposits

(B) ** Demand Deposits

(C) Variable Deposits

(D) Low cost Deposits

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Demand deposits are deposits which can be withdrawn by the depositor at any time by means of a cheque. No interest is paid on such deposits.

Concept note-2: -A demand deposit account (DDA) is a bank account from which deposited funds can be withdrawn at any time, without advance notice.

Concept note-3: -The deposits one can withdraw on demand are known as demand deposits.

Concept note-4: -A demand deposit is money deposited into a bank account with funds that can be withdrawn on-demand at any time. The depositor will typically use demand deposit funds to pay for everyday expenses. For funds in the account, the bank or financial institution may pay either a low or zero interest rate on the deposit.
