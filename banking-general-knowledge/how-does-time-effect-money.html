How does time effect money?

(A) The more money you save the more you will make

(B) Saving for a short period of time proves long term investments

(C) ** The longer you save your money the more you will make

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The time value of money means that a sum of money is worth more now than the same sum of money in the future. The principle of the time value of money means that it can grow only through investing so a delayed investment is a lost opportunity.

Concept note-2: -The time value of money (TVM) is an important concept to investors because a dollar on hand today is worth more than a dollar promised in the future. The dollar on hand today can be used to invest and earn interest or capital gains.
