Money Laundering refers to

(A) Conversion of assets into cash

(B) ** Conversion of money which is illegally obtained

(C) Conversion of cash into gold

(D) Conversion of gold into cash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is Money Laundering? Money laundering is the process of hiding the source of money obtained from illegal sources and converting it to a clean source, thereby avoiding prosecution, conviction, and confiscation of the criminal funds. It is an illegal exercise that converts black money into white money.

Concept note-2: -Money laundering is the illegal process of converting money earned from illegal activities into “clean” money – that is, money that can be freely used in legitimate business operations and does not have to be concealed from the authorities.

Concept note-3: -Briefly described, “money laundering” is the process by which proceeds from a criminal activity are disguised to conceal their illicit origin.

Concept note-4: -What Are Common Ways to Launder Money? The traditional forms of laundering money, including smurfing, using mules, and opening shell corporations. Other methods include buying and selling commodities, investing in various assets like real estate, gambling, and counterfeiting.
