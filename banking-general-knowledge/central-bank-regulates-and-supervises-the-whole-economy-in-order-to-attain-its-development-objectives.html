BANKING GENERAL KNOWLEDGE

[SOURCES]
Central Bank regulates and supervises the whole economy in order to attain its development objectives.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main objective of a central bank is to ensure financial stability. Depending on the country, central banks might have other objectives such as controlling inflation, unemployment, interest rates, or exchange rates. However, all these objectives are in line with the main objective of ensuring financial stability.

Concept note-2: -A central bank is a public institution that is responsible for implementing monetary policy, managing the currency of a country, or group of countries, and controlling the money supply.

Concept note-3: -Issuing of coins is not a function of the Central Bank.

Concept note-4: -The Central Bank aims to ensure that regulated firms are financially sound and safely managed. Regulation of financial institutions and markets is undertaken through risk-based supervision, which is underpinned by credible enforcement deterrents.
