(A) ** Which is included in the Second Schedule of the RBI Act 1934

(B) Which is included in the Banking Regulation Act, 1949

(C) Which is registered under NI Act 1981

(D) Can’t say

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All banks which are included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 are Scheduled Banks. These banks comprise Scheduled Commercial Banks and Scheduled Co-operative Banks.

Concept note-2: -Second schedules of RBI Act, 1934 Under this schedule, the banks should raise at least Rs 5 lakhs and capital. The banks added in the second schedule are known as scheduled banks, and these banks include scheduled cooperative banks and scheduled commercial banks.
