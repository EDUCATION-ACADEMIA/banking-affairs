Green Pin Facility available in Baroda Connect only for customers

(A) Retail

(B) Corporate

(C) Non-Individual

(D) ** Both Retail & Corporate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Green PIN OTP can be generated only on our Bank ATMs. The OTP validation can be done in our Bank as well as other Bank ATMs. Customer who has opted Green PIN need to validate OTP and change PIN in first use.

Concept note-2: -Green PIN-Debit Card PIN Generation through Bank of Baroda ATM. In a move towards green initiative and better customer service, Bank of Baroda has introduced yet another customer centric initiative whereby cardholder can generate the debit card PIN by using any Bank of Baroda ATM. It is easy and convenient.

Concept note-3: -Green PIN initiative offers a hassle free mechanism to generate Debit-cum-ATM PIN for existing as well as new Debit card customers. Customers can set their PIN for new debit cards instantly after receiving the debit card or make use of this functionality in case they forget their existing Debit-cum-ATM Card PIN.
