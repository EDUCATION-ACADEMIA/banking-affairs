What type of endorsement is the most secure?

(A) Blank Endorsement

(B) ** Restrictive Endorsement

(C) Endorsement in Full

(D) Full Endorsement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For Deposit Only-Restrictive Endorsement The most secure way to endorse a check is to: Write: For Deposit Only to Account Number XXXXXXXXXX. Sign your name below that, but still within the endorsement area of the check.

Concept note-2: -A restrictive endorsement is a conditional guaranty of a transfer of a negotiable instrument. That is, an endorsement that takes effect only on the occurrence or non-occurrence of another act or event.

Concept note-3: -Blank or General Endorsement. Full Endorsement or Special Endorsement. Conditional Endorsement. Restrictive Endorsement.

Concept note-4: -In a restrictive endorsement, the check is restricted to use by the payee named at the back of the check.
