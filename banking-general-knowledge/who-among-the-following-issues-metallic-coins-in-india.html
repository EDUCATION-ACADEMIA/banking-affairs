Who among the following issues metallic coins in India?

(A) RBI

(B) ** Government of India

(C) Banks and financial institutions

(D) Any of the above can issue it.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India is the issuing authority of coins and supplies coins to the Reserve Bank on demand. The Reserve Bank puts the coins into circulation on behalf of the Central Government.

Concept note-2: -(b) Coins are issued by the Government of India. Coins in India are issued by the Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Metallic money are the money which are issued by the central bank of a country in form of metals that acts as an unlimited legal tender in the economy.
