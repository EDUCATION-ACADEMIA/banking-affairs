Banking Ombudsman Scheme is applicable to the business of

(A) all scheduled commercial banks excluding RRBs

(B) ** all scheduled commercial banks including RRBs

(C) only public sector banks

(D) all banking companies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India, hereby directs that all commercial banks, regional rural banks and scheduled primary co-operative banks shall comply with the Banking Ombudsman Scheme, 2006 as amended hereby.

Concept note-2: -All Scheduled commercial banks, Regional Rural Banks, and Scheduled Primary Co-Operative banks are covered under the Banking Ombudsman scheme.

Concept note-3: -All the Regional Rural Banks, Scheduled Commercial Banks, and Scheduled Primary Co-Operative Banks are covered under the Banking Ombudsman Scheme.

Concept note-4: -The BOS was notified by RBI in 1995 under Section 35A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. As on date, Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs), Scheduled Primary Urban Co-operative Banks (UCBs), Regional Rural Banks (RRBs), Small Finance Banks (SFBs) and Payment Banks (PBs) are covered under the Scheme.
