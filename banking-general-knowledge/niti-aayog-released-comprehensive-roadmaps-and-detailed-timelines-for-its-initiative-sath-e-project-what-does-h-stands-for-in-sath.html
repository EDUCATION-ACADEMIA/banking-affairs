NITI Aayog released comprehensive roadmaps and detailed timelines for its initiative ‘SATH-E’ project. What does ‘H’ stands for in SATH?

(A) Health

(B) Hospitality

(C) Homage

(D) ** Human

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In this background, NITI Aayog’s initiative of Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital in Education (Project SATH-E) is a guiding star in achieving SDG 4 for India.

Concept note-2: -Niti Aayog has shortlisted Uttar Pradesh, Assam and Karnataka under its ‘Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital (SATH)’ initiative to support them and transform their health sector.

Concept note-3: -With a vision to initiate transformation in the education and health sectors and to address the need expressed by many states for technical support from NITI Ayog, SATH – a programme for ” Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital” with the state government was launched by NITI Ayog.
