Banks are ____

(A) Economic institutions

(B) Social institutions

(C) ** Financial institutions

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A bank is a financial institution licensed to receive deposits and provide other services such as mortgage loans and individual retirement accounts.

Concept note-2: -Banking institutions include commercial banks, savings and loan associations, and credit unions. Non-banking financial institutions include insurance companies, pension funds, and hedge funds.

Concept note-3: -All financial institutions can also be termed as banking institutions.
