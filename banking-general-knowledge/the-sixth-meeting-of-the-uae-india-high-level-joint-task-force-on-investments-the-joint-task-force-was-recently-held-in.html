The sixth meeting of the UAE-India High Level Joint Task Force on Investments (’the Joint Task Force’) was recently held in____

(A) Kerala

(B) Dubai

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Joint Task Force was established in 2013 to promote trade, investment and economic ties between the UAE and India.

Concept note-2: -UAE’s exports to India stood at US$ 43.04 billion while India’s exports to UAE stood at US$ 25.4 billion. In 2022 India & UAE signed a Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) with an aim of taking bilateral trade to US$ 100 billion within 5 years.

Concept note-3: -India and UAE have shared trade links through the centuries. The trade, which was dominated by traditional items such as dates, pearl and fishes, underwent a sharp change after the discovery of oil in UAE (oil exports begun from Abu Dhabi in 1962).

Concept note-4: -The UAE is India’s third largest trading partner and second largest export destination after the US.
