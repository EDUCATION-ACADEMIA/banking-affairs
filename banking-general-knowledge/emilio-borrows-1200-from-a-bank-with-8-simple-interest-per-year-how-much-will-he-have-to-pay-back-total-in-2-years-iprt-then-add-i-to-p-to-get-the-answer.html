Emilio borrows $1200 from a bank with 8% simple interest per year. How much will he have to pay back total in 2 years? (I=prt, then add I to p to get the answer)

(A) $150

(B) $192

(C) $1350

(D) ** $1392

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Interest formula for simple interest: I = Prt where I is the total amount of interest accrued; over t time periods at a simple interest rate, r, and where the original amount invested or borrowed is P.

Concept note-2: -A simple interest calculator is a utility tool that calculates the interest on loans or savings without compounding. You may calculate the simple interest on the principal amount on a daily, monthly, or yearly basis.

Concept note-3: -How much interest is that? The simple interest formula is I=Prt. The P represents the principal.
