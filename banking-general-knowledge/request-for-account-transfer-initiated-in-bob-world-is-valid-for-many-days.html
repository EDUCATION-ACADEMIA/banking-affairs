Request for account transfer initiated in BOB WORLD is valid for many days?

(A) 15 days

(B) 4 days

(C) ** 30 days

(D) 45 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How to check BOB Account Transfer status? To know whether your account successfully transferred to the new branch or not, you can track your account transfer status. Click on Services – Account Transfer in Utilities. Now click on status.

Concept note-2: -If your IMPS transaction times out or fails, check if your money has been debited from the account. If the money is not debited, you can wait for a few minutes and strike another transaction. In another case, if the money is debited from your account, check your account after two working days.

Concept note-3: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-4: -Open the bob World mobile banking app and go to the Menu. Under the Menu, click on ‘Fund Transfer’ option and select ‘other banks’. Click on ‘Beneficiary Registration’ button and select ‘Online Fund transfer (IMPS)’.
