Which one of the is PMJDY related to?

(A) ** Financial inclusion

(B) Bank

(C) Literacy

(D) Female education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is National Mission for Financial Inclusion to ensure access to financial services, namely, a basic savings & deposit accounts, remittance, credit, insurance, pension in an affordable manner.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 1. PMJDY is a flagship financial inclusion scheme launched by Ministry of Finance.

Concept note-3: -The six pillars-providing bank accounts, credit, insurance, pension, financial literacy, and increasing the penetration of banking agents-were designed to address the prior challenges and to accelerate financial inclusion in India.

Concept note-4: -PMJDY brings about the objective of financial inclusion for all by providing basic banking accounts with a debit card with inbuilt accident insurance. The main features of PMJDY include Rs. 5, 000 overdraft facility for Aadhar-linked accounts and a RuPay debit card with inbuilt Rs. 1 lakh accident insurance cover.
