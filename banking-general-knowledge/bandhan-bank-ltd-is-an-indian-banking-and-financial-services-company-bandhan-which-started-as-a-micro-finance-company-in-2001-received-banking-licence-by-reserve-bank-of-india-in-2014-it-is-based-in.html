Bandhan Bank Ltd. is an Indian banking and financial services company. Bandhan, which started as a micro-finance company in 2001, received banking licence by Reserve Bank of India in 2014. It is based in

(A) Mumbai

(B) Bengaluru

(C) Chennai

(D) ** Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -History. Bandhan was set up in 2001 as a not-for-profit entity with the objective of financial inclusion and women empowerment through sustainable livelihood creation. It started its microfinance operations from Bagnan, a small village, about 60 km from Kolkata.

Concept note-2: -2015. Bandhan Bank started operations as a universal bank on August 23. It became the first microfinance company in India to get a universal banking licence. On the day of the launch, the Bank started with 2, 523 banking outlets.
