Which company became the first Indian company to cross Rs. 8 trillion market cap?

(A) Wipro

(B) TCS

(C) ** RIL

(D) Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Industries Ltd (RIL) became the first Indian listed firm to have a market capitalisation of Rs. 19 trillion (US$ 250 billion).

Concept note-2: -Reliance became 1st Indian company to cross USD 100 bn annual revenue.

Concept note-3: -India’s Market Capitalization: % of GDP-Historical Data If you ask a stock market investor today, which Indian company would be the first to touch the $1 trillion market, the obvious answer would be either Reliance Industries or Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

Concept note-4: -Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries Ltd’s (RIL) became the first Indian listed company to touch the Rs 19-trillion market capitalisation-mark after its shares scaled a record high.

Concept note-5: -This list was updated on 7th Feb, 2023. With a market cap of ₹1, 563, 887 crores Reliance Industries leads the Indian Industries with the highest market cap.
