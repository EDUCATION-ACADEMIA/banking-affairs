As per the RuPay Insurance Programme for Premium Cards, Minimum one successful RuPay Card induced financial transaction within ____ Days is required.

(A) 90

(B) 60

(C) 45

(D) ** 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rupay classic contactless Card (NCMC) Usage limit of Rs.25000/-in ATM and Rs.25000/-in POS. Facility of contactless POS purchases up to Rs.5000/-.

Concept note-2: -What is the available sum insured under this insurance cover? Ans. Sum Insured of Rs 1 lakh for RuPay Classic card holders and Rs 2 Lakh for RuPay Platinum card holders.

Concept note-3: -NPCI has tied up with New India Assurance Co Ltd as Insurance partner for Insurance program on RuPay Credit Cards. 4. Banks need to report all such claims to the New India Assurance Co Ltd for intimation and subsequent assessment. submission of complete documentation set.

Concept note-4: -RuPay Premium Debit Card comes with a daily withdrawal limit of Rs 25, 000 and shopping limit at POS & merchant outlets of Rs 1.25 Lacs. When you pay your Utility Bills with the card, you will be getting a 5% CashBack. Up to Rs 50 per month can be accumulated as cashback under this feature.
