Concept note-1: -PIN (Personal Identification Number) is a 4-digit code unique to each ATM card that ensures that the card user or account holder is carrying out the transactions.

Concept note-2: -Authentication is provided by the customer entering a Personal Identification Number (PIN). Using an ATM, customers can access their bank accounts in order to make cash withdrawals and check their account balances.

Concept note-3: -A personal identification number (PIN) is a numerical code issued with a payment card that is required to be entered to complete various financial transactions.
