Which bank has launched the country’s first contact less debit and credit cards on 7 January 2015?

(A) HDFC

(B) SBI

(C) ** ICICI

(D) Yes Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI Bank has launched the country’s first contactless debit and credit cards on 7 January, 2015.

Concept note-2: -Industrial Credit and Investment Corporation of India (ICICI) Bank launched the country’s first-ever ‘contactless’ debit and credit cards. These cards will give the customers to make electronic payments by waving the cards near the merchant terminal instead of dipping or swiping.

Concept note-3: -The first ever hint of contactless was a bus card issued in Seoul back in 1995, but it didn’t really hit the big time until the first contactless Barclaycard appeared 12 years later in 2007.

Concept note-4: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.

Concept note-5: -Axis Bank launched the First Multi-Currency Contactless Card service in India-GKToday.
