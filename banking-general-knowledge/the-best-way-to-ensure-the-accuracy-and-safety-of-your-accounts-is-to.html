the best way to ensure the accuracy and safety of your accounts is to:

(A) ** monitor your online accounts regularly

(B) shred your paper account statements weekly

(C) call your customer service center daily

(D) balance your checkbook annually

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Never leave your computer unattended once you have signed in to online banking. After completing your transactions, ensure that you sign out of online banking, clear your cache, and close your browser. Often, it is easy to forget to sign out of an online banking session. Keep your password and card number safe.

Concept note-2: -You should monitor your checking account at least once or twice a week. The more activity and transactions you make, the more often you should check your account. You should check your balance and your transactions for accuracy. We make it easy to manage your account with online banking and our mobile app.
