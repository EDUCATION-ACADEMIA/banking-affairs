____ type of account is opened by business man

(A) ** Current account

(B) Savings Bank account

(C) Recurring deposit account

(D) Term or fixed deposit account

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Current bank account is opened by businessmen who have a higher number of regular transactions with the bank. It includes deposits, withdrawals, and contra transactions. It is also known as Demand Deposit Account. Current account can be opened in co-operative bank and commercial bank.

Concept note-2: -Current accounts are generally used by businessmen who have a large number of regular transactions with the bank.

Concept note-3: -A current account, also known as financial account is a type of deposit account maintained by individuals who carry out significantly higher number of transactions with banks on a regular basis. It is created by the bank on request of the applicant and is made available for frequent or immediate access.

Concept note-4: -A business current account is a great tool for keeping the finances of a company separate and for managing regular transactions such as paying employees and taxes. Concept note-5: -Current Accounts (C/As) can be opened by individuals, partnership firms, private and public limited companies, HUFs/ specified associations, societies, trusts etc. Formalities/procedures relating to introduction and opening of Current Accounts for individuals are same as those mentioned for Savings Bank Accounts.
