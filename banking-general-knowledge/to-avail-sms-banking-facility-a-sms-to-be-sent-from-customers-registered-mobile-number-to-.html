To avail SMS banking facility, a SMS to be sent from customer’s registered mobile number to ____

(A) 8422009955

(B) 8422009966

(C) 8422009977

(D) ** 8422009988

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customers of our Bank can avail the services by sending SMS from their registered mobile number on 8422009988 with message as under. Charges applicable for this service for each SMS sent by the customer.

Concept note-2: -In the Internet banking site, click the SMS Alerts link in the Requests tab. You are displayed a list of your accounts. Select the account for which you wish to enable SMS alerts and click OK. Select the events for which you wish to receive alerts.

Concept note-3: -Step 1: Download the mobile banking app from the app store. Step 2: Open the app and click on ‘Confirm’. Step 3: After clicking on “confirm, an SMS will be sent to your registered number for verification. Step 4: Click on ‘Register Now’.

Concept note-4: -Click on Subscription sub-menu 2. Details of various types of Alerts subscribed by the User are displayed with tick mark. 3. Select the Alerts, you wish to unsubscribe by removing the tick against the internal mail alerts and/or SMS Alerts 4.
