What is the capital of Suriname

(A) ** Paramaribo

(B) Havana

(C) Athenes

(D) Caracas

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Paramaribo, formerly Dutch Guiana, largest city, capital, and chief port of Suriname. It lies on the Suriname River 9 miles (15 km) from the Atlantic Ocean. Paramaribo is built on a shingle reef that stands 16 feet (5 metres) above the river at low tide.

Concept note-2: -In Paramaribo you can explore the colonial history, see colonial buildings and be amazed at the huge wooden Saint Peter and Paul Cathedral, that is the biggest wooden structure in the Western Hemisphere.

Concept note-3: -Paramaribo (/ˌpɑːrəˈmɑːrɪboʊ/) is the capital and largest city of Suriname, located on the banks of the Suriname River in the Paramaribo District.
