What is the Dimension (size) of New 10 rupees note?

(A) 58 mm × 143 mm

(B) 66 mm × 124 mm

(C) 42 mm × 166 mm

(D) ** 63 mm × 123 mm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The note has motif of ‘Sun Temple, Konark’ on the reverse, depicting the country’s cultural heritage. The base colour of the note is chocolate brown. The note has other designs, geometric patterns aligning with the overall colour scheme, both at the observe and reverse. The size of the new note is 63mm x 123mm.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is 150mm × 66 mm.

Concept note-3: -The new ₹ 10 notes will be in the Mahatma Gandhi (New) Series, and will bear the signature of RBI Governor Dr Urjit R Patel, the central bank said in an official statement. The new denomination notes will have motif of the Sun Temple and the Konark on the reverse side, depicting the country’s cultural heritage.

Concept note-4: -The new Rs 10 coin weighs 7.74 grams. It will have a diameter of 27mm and 100 serrations on its edge.
