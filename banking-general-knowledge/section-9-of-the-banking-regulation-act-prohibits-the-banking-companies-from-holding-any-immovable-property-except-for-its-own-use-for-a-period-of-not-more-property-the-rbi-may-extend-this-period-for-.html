Section 9 of the Banking Regulation Act prohibits the banking Companies from holding any immovable property except for its own use for a period of not more property. The RBI may extend this period for a further period of____:

(A) 2 years

(B) 4 years

(C) ** 5 years

(D) 6 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per the amended Section 9 of the BR Act, 1949-‘Disposal of non-banking assets’ mandates that no banking company shall hold any immovable property howsoever acquired, except such as is required for its own use, for any period exceeding seven years from the acquisition thereof.

Concept note-2: -Section 19 in BANKING REGULATION ACT, 1949.

Concept note-3: -The Banking Regulation Act, 1949 confers power on Reserve Bank of India under Section 47(A) to impose penalties if there has been any default or contravention.

Concept note-4: -Section 5 in BANKING REGULATION ACT, 1949.
