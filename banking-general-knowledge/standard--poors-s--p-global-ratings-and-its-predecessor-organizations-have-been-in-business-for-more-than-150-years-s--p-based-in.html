Standard & Poor’s (S & P) Global Ratings and its predecessor organizations have been in business for more than 150 years. (S & P) based in

(A) New York, USA

(B) London, UK

(C) Paris, France

(D) ** Geneva, Switzerland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The scale runs from AAA to D and intermediate ratings of (+) or (-) are offered at each level between AA and CCC (for example, BBB+, BBB and BBB-). S&P may also offer guidance (referred to as a credit watch) on whether it is likely to be upgraded (positive), downgraded (negative) or uncertain (neutral).

Concept note-2: -Standard & Poor’s-officially, S&P Global-is a public company in the financial information and analytics business.
