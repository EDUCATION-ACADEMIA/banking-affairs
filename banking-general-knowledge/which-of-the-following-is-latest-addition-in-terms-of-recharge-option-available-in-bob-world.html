EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"HAPPY NEW YEAR 2023 OFFER” shall means 2 additional benefits to users recharging with 2999 annual recharge. “23 Days Extra Validity Extension Voucher Additional 23 Day validity voucher with same daily benefits as 2999 recharge i.e. Unlimited National Voice, National SMS:100/Day and Data: 2.5GB/Day in Home and Roaming.

Concept note-2: -The purpose of the add-on packs is to provide you with additional high-speed data allocation. Jio also offers 4G data vouchers that provide additional high-speed data benefits along with voice calling minutes for non-Jio networks.
