The debenture holders of a company are its____

(A) Employees

(B) Debtors

(C) ** Creditors

(D) Partners

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Debenture holders are merely lenders to the company and are considered to be creditors. Shareholders actively participate in the decision making process of the company. Debenture holders cannot participate in the decision making process.

Concept note-2: -Debenture is as good as debt of the company which carries a certain percentage of interest. Such amount is payable to the debenture holders and therefore, debenture holders are considered as creditors of the company and having a preference to the shareholders.

Concept note-3: -Debenture holders are creditors.

Concept note-4: -Debentures are part of loan, so a debenture holder is only a creditor of the company.

Concept note-5: -A debenture holder is an unsecured creditor. Unsecured credit is viewed as a higher risk.
