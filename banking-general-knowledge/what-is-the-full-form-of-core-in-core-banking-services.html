What is the full form of ‘CORE’ in core banking services?

(A) Channel of Rupee Exchange.

(B) Customer Online Realtime Exchange.

(C) Centralized Online Rupee Exchange.

(D) ** Centralized Online Realtime Exchange.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In fact, CORE is an acronym for “Centralized Online Real-time Exchange", thus the bank’s branches can access applications from centralized data centers.

Concept note-2: -Core Banking Solution (CBS) is the networking of bank branches, which allows customers to manage their accounts, and use various banking facilities from any part of the world. In simple terms, there is no need to visit your own branch to do banking transactions.

Concept note-3: -Summary. The three main business segments for a bank are retail banking, wholesale banking, and wealth management. Retail banking or personal banking involves deposits, mortgages, loans, and credit cards.
