What is the minimum net owned fund requirement for NBFCs?

(A) ** Rs 200 lakh

(B) Rs 250 lakh

(C) Rs 25 lakh

(D) Rs 100 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In sum, henceforth, any NBFC making an application to Reserve Bank of India for a certificate of registration should have NOF of Rs. 200 lakhs. 3. A copy of the notification issued under the powers derived from the provisions of clause (b) of sub-section (1) of section 45-IA of the RBI Act is enclosed.

Concept note-2: -Procedure to Incorporate an NBFC A company should first be registered under the Companies Act 2013 or should already be registered under the Companies Act 1956 as either a Private Limited or a Public Limited Company. The minimum net owned funds of the Company should be Rs. 2 Crore.

Concept note-3: -Every company seeking registration as NBFC-Factor shall have a minimum Net Owned Fund (NOF) of ₹5 crore, or as specified by the Reserve Bank from time to time.
