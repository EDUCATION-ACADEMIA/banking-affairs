The CII signed an MoU with the UN Environment for coherent implementation of the environmental dimension of sustainable development. What does C stands for in CII?

(A) Competition

(B) ** Confederation

(C) Council

(D) Commission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CII Design initiatives are focused in the following areas: Competitiveness of Industry by Design-Branding/ Promotion of Design through Media, Design Education, Design for Culture, Society & Environment and Implementation of Design Policy.

Concept note-2: -“Development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of the future generations to meet their own needs”. The goal of which is to achieve balance/harmony between environment sustainability, economic sustainability and socio-political sustainability.

Concept note-3: -It stated that the principle of sustainable development means a kind of development where the essential needs of especially the poor are met in the present without compromising the future generation’s ability to meet their needs. The primary focus of sustainable development in this regard is environmental protection.
