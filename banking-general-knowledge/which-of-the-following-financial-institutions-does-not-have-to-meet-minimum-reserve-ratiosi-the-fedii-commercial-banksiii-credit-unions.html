Concept note-3: -National Bank National banks are required to be members of the Federal Reserve System and belong to the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.

Concept note-4: -The required reserve ratio can be calculated by simply dividing the amount of money a bank is required to hold in reserve by the amount of money it has on deposit.
