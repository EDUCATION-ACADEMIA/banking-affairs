In the 29th meeting, GST council approves cash incentives to promote Digital transactions. Finance Minister Piyush Goyal announces____Cashback upto Rs 100 for the Digital Transactions done via BHIM, Rupay or USSD mode.

(A) 10%

(B) 25%

(C) ** 20%

(D) 15%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman.

Concept note-2: -The Union Finance Minister as the chairperson of the GST Council. The Union Minister of State in charge of revenue (finance) from the centre. A minister from each state who is either in charge of finance or taxation or has been nominated by the state to serve as a member of the GST Council.

Concept note-3: -GST is not applicable on cash backs of credit card as it is neither fall under definition of goods nor services.

Concept note-4: -The current Finance Minister Nirmala Sitaraman is the current chairman of the GST Council.
