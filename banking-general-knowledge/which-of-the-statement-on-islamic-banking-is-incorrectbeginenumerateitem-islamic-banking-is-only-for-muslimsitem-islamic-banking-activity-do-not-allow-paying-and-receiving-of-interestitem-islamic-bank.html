Which of the statement on Islamic banking is incorrect?

1. :

Islamic banking is only for Muslims.

2. :

Islamic banking activity do not allow paying and receiving of interest.

3. :

Islamic banking promotes the principle of profit sharing between banking institution and depositor.

(A) ** Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) Only 3

(D) Both 2 and 3.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Islamic banking is only for Muslims is an incorrect statement.

Concept note-2: -Islamic banking, also referred to as Islamic finance or Shariah-compliant finance, refers to financial activities that adhere to Shariah (Islamic law). Two fundamental principles of Islamic banking are the sharing of profit and loss and the prohibition of the collection and payment of interest by lenders and investors.

Concept note-3: -Concept of banking based on pooling of excess funds of depositors and channeling them towards those who require it for investing activities is not only approved but encouraged by Islam. But the concept to lending and borrowing on the basis of interest in not allowed in Islam.

Concept note-4: -No Riba/interest: Islamic banks cannot involve in riba/interest related transactions. They cannot lend money to earn additional amount on it.
