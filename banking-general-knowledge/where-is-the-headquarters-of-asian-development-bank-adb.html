Where is the headquarters of Asian Development Bank (ADB)?

(A) New Delhi, India

(B) Beijing, China

(C) ** Manila, Philippines

(D) Tokyo, Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is a regional development bank established on 19 December 1966, which is headquartered in the Ortigas Center located in the city of Mandaluyong, Metro Manila, Philippines.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The headquarters of Asian Development Bank situated at Manila. The Asian Development Bank (ADB), founded on 19 December 1966, is a regional development bank. The organization also has 31 field offices worldwide to support social and economic growth in Asia.

Concept note-3: -1972–1990 ADB’s first headquarters was located at 2330 Roxas Boulevard, Pasay City, Philippines. 8 April 1986, Mandaluyong City, Philippines Philippine President Corazon Aquino lays the foundation stone of the new ADB headquarters.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is the Philippines. The headquarters of the Asian Development Bank are in the Philippines.
