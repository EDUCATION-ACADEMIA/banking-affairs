____is an app that lets you make simple, easy and quick payment transactions using UPI.

(A) ** BHIM

(B) YONO

(C) ISDN

(D) Best Credit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BHIM UCO UPI is a payment app that lets you make simple, easy and quick transactions using Unified Payments Interface (UPI). You can make direct bank payments to anyone on UPI using their UPI ID or scanning their QR with the BHIM app. You can also request money through the app from a UPI ID.

Concept note-2: -Paytm UPI has gained the preference of millions of users, earning it the award for Best UPI app at the recent Global Fintech Fest.

Concept note-3: -Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is an instant real-time payment system developed by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).
