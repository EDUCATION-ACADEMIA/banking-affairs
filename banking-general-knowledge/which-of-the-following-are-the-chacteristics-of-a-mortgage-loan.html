Which of the following are the chacteristics of a Mortgage Loan?

(A) It is secured against, immovable or fixed property.

(B) It is a long-term loan in nature.

(C) It is repaid in small monthly installments along with interest

(D) ** All of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Features of the Mortgage Loan The options include-floating rates, fixed interest rates, interest-only mortgage and Payment option ARMs. A mortgage loan is one of the easiest ways to avail a home loan. You can be the sole owner of the house once the loan is repaid. The LTV ratio for Mortgage Loans is typically 60%-70%.

Concept note-2: -Mortgages are further classified as 1) Conventional mortgages 2) Jumbo mortgages 3) Government-insured mortgages 4) Fixed-rate mortgages 5) Adjustable-rate mortgages. Now, based on these, there are further loan type. Types of Mortgages in our country: Simple Mortgage.

Concept note-3: -A. Security on immovable property for a loan given by a bank. Security on movable property for a loan given by a bank. Security on immovable property for a deposit received by a bank.
