CEPI stands for?

(A) Council of Environmental Pollution of India

(B) Centre for Environmental Pollution in India

(C) ** Comprehensive Environmental Pollution Index

(D) Centre for Environmental Pollution and Industries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Comprehensive Environmental Pollution Index (CEPI), which is a rational number to characterize the environmental quality at a given location following the algorithm of source, pathway and receptor have been developed.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The Comprehensive Environmental Pollution Index (CEPI) is a rational number used to characterise the quality of the environment at a specific area using the source, route, and receptor approach. A rise in the CEPI score indicates that the receiving environment is suffering.

Concept note-3: -CEPI can be calculated by applying the highest EPI value of the three in im and the remaining two EPIs in i2 and i3. If the final CEPI score is 70 or above, the region is critically polluted and if the score is between 70-60, the area is considered to be severely polluted region.

Concept note-4: -So, such industrial areas have to be assessed for improving the quality of the environment. With this objective CPCB developed Comprehensive Environmental Pollution Index (CEPI) to find out an Index value to characterise quality of the environment.

Concept note-5: -CEPI is an innovative global partnership between public, private, philanthropic, and civil society organisations launched in Davos in 2017 to develop vaccines to stop future epidemics.
