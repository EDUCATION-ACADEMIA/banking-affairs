The World Trade Organization (WTO) deals with the global rules of trade between nations. Who is the Director General of WTO?

(A) ** Roberto Azevedo

(B) Christine Legarde

(C) Raghuram Rajan

(D) Oliver Ralph Kahn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WTO | Director-General: Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala.

Concept note-2: -Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Director-General of the World Trade Organization (WTO)

Concept note-3: -Mr Roberto Azevêdo was Director-General of the WTO from 1 September 2013 to 31 August 2020.

Concept note-4: -The World Trade Organization (WTO) is the only global international organization dealing with the rules of trade between nations.

Concept note-5: -Mexico, on 21 December 2012, nominated Mr Herminio Blanco for the post of WTO Director-General to succeed the current Director-General, Mr Pascal Lamy, whose term of office expires on 31 August 2013.
