Our Bank has renewed Debit card transaction Insurance policy from which Company?

(A) Tata AIG GIC.

(B) IFFCO TOKYO GIC

(C) United Insurance Co. Ltd

(D) ** National Insurance co Ltd.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Go to the website of the Insurance Information Bureau (IIB) of India and check your policy status. Go to the Parivahan Sewa website and check the policy status. Checking car insurance status on VAHAN App. Visit your Regional Transport Office (RTO)’s website and know your policy status.

Concept note-2: -66 years later, after passing of General Insurance Business Nationalisation Act in 1972, it was merged along with 21 foreign and 11 Indian companies to form National insurance Company Limited, one of the four subsidiaries of the General Insurance Corporation of India, fully owned by Govt. of India.

Concept note-3: -Step 1: Visit the official website of National Insurance and click on ‘Customer Login’ option on the homepage. Step 2: Enter your customer ID and password to login. Step 3: Select the insurance policy for which you want the 80D tax certificate for.
