An Indian citizen can open a Savings Account in which of the following?

(A) SIDBI

(B) RBI

(C) NABARD

(D) ** A Private Sector Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customers cannot open an SB account for business purpose.

Concept note-2: -A savings account is most suitable for people who are salaried employees or have a monthly income, whereas, Current Accounts work best for traders and entrepreneurs who need to access their accounts frequently. Savings accounts earn interest at a rate of around 4%, while there is no such earning from a Current Account.
