A typical commercial banks provide ____

(A) Long term loans

(B) medium term loans

(C) ** short term loans

(D) all the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commercial loans are most often used for short-term funding needs.

Concept note-2: -Depending upon the repayment period this loan type is classified as under: Short term loan: Repayment period less than 1 year. Medium term loan: Repayment period between 1 to 3 years. Long term loan: Repayment period above 3 years.

Concept note-3: -Commercial banks provide basic banking services and products to the general public, both individual consumers and small to midsize businesses. These services include checking and savings accounts; loans and mortgages; basic investment services such as CDs; and other services such as safe deposit boxes.

Concept note-4: -A commercial bank provides long-term and short-term loans and creates credit.
