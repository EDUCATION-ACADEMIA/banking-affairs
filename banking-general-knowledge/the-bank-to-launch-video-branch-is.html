The bank to launch ‘video branch’ is?

(A) HSBC

(B) ICICI

(C) ** IndusInd

(D) Yes Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Want to experience Video Branch? IndusInd Bank VideoBranch facility is available exclusively for IndusInd Bank customers.

Concept note-2: -Video Conversations for Face-to-Face Banking Anytime, Anywhere! Video Branch is a service offered exclusively to all our customers. By using this service, you can seamlessly connect with your Branch Manager, Relationship Manager or with our centralized Video Branch Executive.

Concept note-3: -IndusInd Bank offers the facility of opening savings account through video KYC as it simplifies the on-boarding process for new customers. It also replaces the existing practice of physical authentication of documents.

Concept note-4: -Video banking is a term used for performing banking transactions or professional banking consultations via a remote video connection. Video banking can be performed via purpose built banking transaction machines (similar to an Automated teller machine), or via a videoconference enabled bank branch.
