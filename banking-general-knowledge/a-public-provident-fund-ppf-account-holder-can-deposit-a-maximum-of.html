A Public Provident Fund (PPF) account holder can deposit a maximum of

(A) ** 1.5 Lakhs

(B) 2.5 Lakhs

(C) 1.0 Lakh

(D) 3.5 Lakhs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You can make a deposit to a PPF account ranging from Rs.500 up to Rs.1.5 lakh per financial year. The deposit can be made in a lump sum or in instalments. There is no restriction on the number of instalments per financial year.

Concept note-2: -You cannot deposit more than Rs. 1.5 lakhs in the PPF Account in any given financial year. The deposit frequency, however, is not limited. Earlier, the PPF account max deposit was twelve times in one financial year.
