1. :

2. :

(A) Only 1

(B) 1 and 2

(C) Only 3

(D) ** All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The BSE and NSE will be open for trading today (October 25) but will be closed on Wednesday (October 26) on the occasion of ‘Diwali Balipratipada’.

Concept note-2: -NSE or National Stock Exchange is open on the weekdays from Monday to Friday and is closed on Saturday and Sunday, except for any special trading sessions that are announced.

Concept note-3: -Since New Year’s Day falls on a Sunday this year, Monday, January 2, 2023, will be observed as a stock market holiday.
