What is the name of the program initiated by the govt which is responsible for water supply in around 500 cities?

(A) PMMY Programme

(B) APY Programme

(C) ** AMRUT Programme

(D) PMGSY

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The components of the AMRUT consist of capacity building, reform implementation, water supply, sewerage and septage management, storm water drainage, urban transport and development of green spaces and parks.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation. Purpose: To ensure that every household has access to a tap with the assured supply of water and a sewerage connection.

Concept note-3: -The Atal Mission 2.0 will be implemented from 2021-22 to 2025-26 and strive for 100 tap water connection and rejuvenation of water bodies, development of gardens and green spaces. As part of Amrut 2.0, projects worth Rs 27, 700 crore will be executed.
