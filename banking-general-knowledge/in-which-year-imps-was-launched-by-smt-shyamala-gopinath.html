In which year IMPS was launched by Smt. Shyamala Gopinath?

(A) 2014

(B) 2006

Concept note-1: -Immediate Payment Service (IMPS) public launch happened on 22nd November 2010 by Smt. Shyamala Gopinath, DG RBI at Mumbai and this service is now available to the Indian public.
