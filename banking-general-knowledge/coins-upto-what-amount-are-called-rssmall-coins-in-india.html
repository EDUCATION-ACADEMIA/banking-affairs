Coins upto what amount are called RS.small coins’ in India?

(A) 25 paise

(B) ** 50 paise

(C) 1 rupee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coins up to 50 paise are called ‘small coins’ and coins of Rupee one and above are called ‘Rupee Coins’. Coins can be issued up to the denomination of ₹1000 under The Coinage Act, 2011. 2.
