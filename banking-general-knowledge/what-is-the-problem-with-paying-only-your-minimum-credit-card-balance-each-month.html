What is the problem with paying only your minimum credit card balance each month?

(A) It lowers your credit score

(B) ** You have to pay interest

(C) The bank will cancel your credit card

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While it’s important to make at least the minimum payment, it’s not ideal to carry a balance from month to month, because you’ll rack up interest charges (unless you’re benefiting from an intro 0% APR) and risk falling into debt.
