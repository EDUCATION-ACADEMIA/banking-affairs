Agriculture, industry and services sectors are expected to grow at the rate of____,____, and____respectively in 2017-18.

(A) ** 2.1%, 4.4%, and 8.3%

(B) 2.1%, 4.4%, and 8.7%

(C) 2.1%, 4.6%, and 8.3%

(D) 2.8%, 4.4%, and 8.3%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The agriculture and industry sectors are expected to grow at 2.1% and 4.4% respectively, while the service sector is estimated to grow at 8.3%. Inflation: The Consumer Price Index (CPI) based inflation was 3.3% in 2017-18 (April-December). The average food inflation was 1.2% for the same period.

Concept note-2: -Gross Domestic Product GDP at current prices for the year 2017-18 is estimated at ‘167.73 lakh crore, showing a growth rate of 10.0 percent over the First Revised Estimates of GDP for the year 2016-17 of ‘152.54 lakh crore.

Concept note-3: -Agricultural output has been volatile over the past 10 years, with annual growth ranging from 8.6% in 2010-11, to-0.2% in 2014-15 and 0.8% in 2015-16.

Concept note-4: -Share of Agriculture & allied sector has declined at 18.20% in 2013-14. Share of Services sector has improved to 57.03%. Share of Industry sector has also increased to 24.77%. According to CIA Fackbook, sector-wise Indian GDP composition in 2017 is as follows: Agriculture (15.4%), Industry (23%), and Services (61.5%).

Concept note-5: -The services sector accounts for 54 per cent of India’s Gross Value Added (GVA). Its growth rate moderated to 7.5 per cent in 2018-19 from 8.1 per cent in 2017-18.
