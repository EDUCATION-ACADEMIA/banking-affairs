Banks move money between:

(A) other banks

(B) banks and individual customers

(C) governments

(D) ** all of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A bank transfer is a transaction that moves money from one bank account to another, either electronically or via a check. A bank transfer can be internal, meaning the transfer occurs between accounts at the same bank, or external, with funds transferred between accounts at two different banks.

Concept note-2: -The three main types of transactions include checks, withdrawals and deposits.

Concept note-3: -Money Movement is an experimental API that lets you simulate transfers between Capital One linked accounts. This can allow users to easily make and edit simple transfers from within your app.

Concept note-4: -A bank transfer is when money is sent from one bank account to another.
