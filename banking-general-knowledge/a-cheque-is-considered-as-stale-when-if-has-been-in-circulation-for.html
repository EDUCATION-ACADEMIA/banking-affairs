A cheque is considered as stale when if has been in circulation for

(A) More than forty eight hours

(B) More than one year

(C) More than six months unless otherwise specified

(D) ** More than three months

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Checks that remain outstanding for long periods of time cannot be cashed as they become void. Some checks become stale if dated after 60 or 90 days, while others become void after six months. Outstanding checks that remain so for a long period of time are known as stale checks.
