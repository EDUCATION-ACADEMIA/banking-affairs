How many Aadhaar Enrolment and Update Centres in total are there in India?

(A) 17000

(B) ** 13000

(C) 11000

(D) 60000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These include dedicated centres in all metro cities, all state capitals and Union Territories. The ASK will run along with over 35, 000 Aadhaar Kendra already being run by banks, post offices, BSNL and state governments.

Concept note-2: -Tembhli is a village in Nandurbar district of Maharashtra state in India, from where the ambitious Aadhaar Scheme was launched on 29 September 2010. Ranjana Sonawane became the first person to be given an Aadhaar identity number.

Concept note-3: -After checking the official site of UIDAI.GOV.IN for your query the maximum days for updation in aadhar card is 90 days. In general it should take 5-7 days and at most 90 days. If there is no response after 15 days I suggest to please make a call or write a mail on the official e-mail ID of UIDAI.
