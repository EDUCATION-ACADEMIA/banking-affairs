Which was the first bank to be wholly owned and run by Indians?

(A) Allahabad Bank

(B) Bank of Baroda

(C) Bank of India

(D) ** Central Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Established in 1911, Central Bank of India was the first Indian commercial bank which was wholly owned and managed by Indians.

Concept note-2: -The first bank fully managed by Indians was Punjab National Bank, established in 1895 in Lahore.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India is the central bank of the country.
