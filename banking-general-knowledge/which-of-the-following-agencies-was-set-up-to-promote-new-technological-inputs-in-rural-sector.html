Which of the following agencies was set up to promote new technological inputs in rural sector?

(A) ** CAPART

(B) HUDCO

(C) SIDBI

(D) IDBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CAPART is an autonomous society under the Ministry of Rural Development, established in 1986 to promote voluntary action towards implementation of projects for the enhancement of rural prosperity and to act as a catayst for development of technologies appropriate for the rural areas.

Concept note-2: -Under the Public Cooperation Scheme (PC), CAPART supports integrated rural development projects which are innovative and replicable.

Concept note-3: -Government of India in September 1986 set up the CAPART by merging two autonomous bodies: People’s Action for Development of India (PADI). Council for Advancement of Rural Technology (CART).

Concept note-4: -Council for Advancement of People’s Action and Rural Technology (CAPART) is an autonomous organisation under the Ministry of Agriculture. 2. Primary objective of the CAPART is to promote voluntary action to propagate appropriate rural technologies for the benefit of rural masses.

Concept note-5: -PC(CAPART) | Ministry of Rural Development | Government of India.
