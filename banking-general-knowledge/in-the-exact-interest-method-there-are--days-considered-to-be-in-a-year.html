In the exact interest method there are ____ days considered to be in a year.

(A) 12

(B) 360

(C) ** 365

(D) 52

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the other hand, Exact simple interest is a simple interest that uses exact number of days in a year which is 365 (or 366 for leap year). These two kinds of simple interest are only applicable if the unit of time used is in days.

Concept note-2: -This is the simplest way a lender calculates interest. Because there are 365 days in a normal year, and 365 cannot conveniently be divided by 12 months into a whole number, the lender simply assumes that there are 12 equal, 30-day months (or a 360-day year).

Concept note-3: -The interest year has 360 days.
