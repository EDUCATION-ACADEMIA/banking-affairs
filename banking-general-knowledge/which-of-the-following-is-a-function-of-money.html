Which of the following is a function of money?

(A) acceptability

(B) ** medium of exchange

(C) portability

(D) relatively scarce

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Money facilitates transactions of goods and services as a medium of exchange. Producers sell their goods to wholesalers in exchange of money. Wholesalers, in turn, sell their goods to the retailers and the retailers sell these goods to the consumers in exchange for money.

Concept note-2: -A medium of exchange is a portable instrument that is used as an intermediary to facilitate the sale and purchase of goods between parties. In modern economies, the medium of exchange is currency.

Concept note-3: -To summarize, money has taken many forms through the ages, but money consistently has three functions: store of value, unit of account, and medium of exchange.
