Which among the following Banks is going to develop as many as 100 digital villages in various States of the country as part of its initiative to promote digital banking among the rural population?

(A) UCO Bank

(B) ** Vijaya Bank

(C) Corporation Bank

(D) Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian bank will set up 100 digital villages across the country? Notes: The Vijaya Bank will set up 100 digital villages in various States of the country as part of its initiative to promote digital banking among the rural population.

Concept note-2: -Two private sector banks – ICICI Bank and HDFC – have announced they will set up digital banking units in India.

Concept note-3: -The Digital Banking definition is banking done through the digital platform, doing away with all the paperwork like cheques, pay-in slips, Demand Drafts, and so on. It means availability of all banking activities online.
