The biggest international financial centre in the world:

(A) Frankfurt

(B) Geneva

(C) ** London

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -London is responsible for $3.8tn in daily forex-this is more than one third of worldwide trades. That is more than the next three largest centres-New York, Singapore, and Hong Kong-combined.

Concept note-2: -In terms of assets, London is the largest and most established such centre, followed by New York, the difference being that the proportion of international to domestic business is much greater in the former. Examples cited by the IMF included New York City, London, and Tokyo. Regional Financial Center (RFC).

Concept note-3: -Which are the top financial centres in the world in 2021? The top financial centre in 2022 is New York, and in the top 5 are New York, London, Singapore, Hong Kong & San Francisco. The top 5 financial centres in Asia-Pacific are Singapore, Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen.

Concept note-4: -The U.K. is the highest net exporter of financial services and London, with its convenient time zone, use of English and feather-light regulations, is the world’s financial capital. Various cities, including Venice and Amsterdam, have held and lost the title throughout history.

Concept note-5: -New York ranked first as the most attractive financial centers worldwide as of March 2022. The ranking is based on an index for financial center rating, in which a multitude of factors are integrated. New York had a rating of with 759 points during that time, followed by London, with 726 points.
