Which of the following are the ways to repay the personal loan?

(A) Equated Monthly Installments via Cheques

(B) Electronic Clearing Services

(C) Cash

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The principal refers to the original sum of money borrowed in a loan. Interest is the charge for the privilege of borrowing money; a borrower must pay interest for the ability to use the funds released to them through the loan.

Concept note-2: -There are three different methods for repaying a housing loan: equal payments, equal instalments and fixed equal payments. The choice of the repayment method depends on many things, such as whether you want to pay the same amount every month or whether you prefer to pay off the loan within a specific time period.
