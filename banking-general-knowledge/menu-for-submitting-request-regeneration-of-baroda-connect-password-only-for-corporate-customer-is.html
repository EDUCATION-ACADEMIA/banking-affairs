Menu for submitting request regeneration of Baroda Connect password only for corporate customer is?

(A) HDCR

(B) ACUID

(C) ** REGPW

(D) MRCR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New user: Fresh Internet banking facility needs to be applied through HDCR menu in Finacle and activation of the same to be done through PWDDEL option in HDCR after delivering the password to the Customer.

Concept note-2: -Step 1: Enter the User id and Click on option a) You wish reset your Answers Click here. Step 2: Enter Mobile number to receive OTP. Step 3: Enter OTP received on registered mobile number. Step 4: Enter Transaction password.

Concept note-3: -Kindly, request for Regeneration of Password, by submitting your request, either to your branch OR fax the same to 91-22-27573288. We shall generate a fresh Login and/or Transaction Password, as requested and the same shall be dispatched at your Communication Address recorded with us.
