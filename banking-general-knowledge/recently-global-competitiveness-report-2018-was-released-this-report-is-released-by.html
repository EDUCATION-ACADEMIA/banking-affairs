Recently Global Competitiveness Report 2018 was released. This report is released by____

(A) World Bank

(B) ** World Economic Forum

(C) IMF

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Economic Forum has ranked India as the 58th most competitive economy in its Global Competitiveness Report 2018. China has slipped down in the rankings to 28, while US has been declared as the most competitive of the 140 economies in the world.

Concept note-2: -The Global Competitiveness Report (GCR) is a yearly report published by the World Economic Forum.

Concept note-3: -With a score of 85.6 out of 100, the United States tops the 2018 rankings of the GCI 4.0, confirming its status of most competitive economy in the world. Canada ranks 12 overall with a score of 79.9, behind three Scandinavian countries: Sweden (9), Denmark (10) and Finland (11).

Concept note-4: -During these years, India’s competitive ranking stayed the same at 43. In 2018, it was 44, the data shows. With the improvement in the ranking, India’s economic performance has improved from 37th in 2021 to 28th position in 2022.
