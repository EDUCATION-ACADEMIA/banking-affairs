Concept note-1: -Most options and futures contracts are cash-settled. However, an exception is listed equity options contracts, which are often settled by delivery of the actual underlying shares of stock.

Concept note-2: -In a physical settlement, the seller has to physically deliver the stocks to the buyer at the end of the expiration date. In a physical settlement, the following transactions take place: Taking Delivery: As a buyer, you take the delivery of the stocks after the expiration date.
