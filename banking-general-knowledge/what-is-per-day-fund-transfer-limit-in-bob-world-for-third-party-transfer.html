What is Per Day fund transfer limit in bob World for Third party Transfer?

(A) 200000

(B) ** 500000

(C) 1500000

(D) 50000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day. Customer can apply for Debit Card and manage other card activities like: Set daily debit card limit for ATM transactions. Set daily debit card limits for PoS and Ecommerce transactions.

Concept note-2: -(a) Inward transactions – Free, no charge to be levied. (b) Outward transactions 2 lakh to 5 lakh not exceeding 30 per transaction; Above 5 lakh – not exceeding 55 per transaction.

Concept note-3: -Within the banking industry, a third party transfer is a type of transaction that involves making out and depositing a payment into the account of a party other than the individual or entity who received the payment.
