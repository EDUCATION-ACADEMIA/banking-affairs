(A) 1990

(B) 1989

(C) ** 1999

Concept note-2: -The Government of India, Ministry of Finance, vide Notification No. GSR(371)(E) dated 1st May 2000 has notified that the Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (42 of 1999) shall come into force on the 1st day of June 2000.
