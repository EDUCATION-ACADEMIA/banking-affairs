You can replace a lost unused gift card by requesting a replacement card from the same business.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Contact the retailer immediately upon noticing the card missing to cancel the card and find out the process for getting a new one. If you don’t have the original card receipt, you may still be able to be reimbursed if you have the gift card number or if you registered the card at the issuer’s site when you received it.

Concept note-2: -Reach out to the retailer or post a notice on an online community board, such as Craigslist or NextDoor, to try to reconnect the gift card to its owner. (In some states, such as Minnesota, a citizen is even required by law to return lost property.)
