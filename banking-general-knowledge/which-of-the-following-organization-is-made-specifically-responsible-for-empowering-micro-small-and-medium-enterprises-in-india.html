Which of the following organization is made specifically responsible for empowering Micro, Small and Medium enterprises in India?

(A) NABARD

(B) RBI

(C) ** SIDBI

(D) ECGC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Board for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (NBMSME) was established by the Government under the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006 and Rules made thereunder.

Concept note-2: -Role of SIDBI SIDBI is mandated to serve as the Principal Financial Institution for executing the triple agenda of promotion, financing and development of the MSME sector and coordination of the functions of the various Institutions engaged in similar activities.

Concept note-3: -Small Industries Development Bank of India(SIDBI) | Department of Financial Services | Ministry of Finance | Government of India.

Concept note-4: -In view of the fact that banks are the predominant source of finance in India, the Reserve Bank of India includes micro and small enterprises in the list of priority lending sectors.
