What is the minimum paid-up equity capital for small finance banks?

(A) Rs 10 crore

(B) Rs 500 crore

(C) ** Rs 100 crore

(D) Rs 200 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Initial Capital Requirement for Small Finance Banks is ₹200 Crore.

Concept note-2: -200 Cr. The minimum capital for Small Finance is prescribed at Rs. 200 crore .

Concept note-3: -The small finance bank will be required to use the words “Small Finance Bank” in its name in order to differentiate it from other banks. The minimum paid-up equity capital for small finance banks shall be Rs. 100 crore.

Concept note-4: -Small Finance Banks It is licensed under section 22 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The minimum paid-up capital required to set up a small finance bank is Rs. 200 crore and it will be required to maintain a capital adequacy ratio of 15 percent of its risk-weighted assets.
