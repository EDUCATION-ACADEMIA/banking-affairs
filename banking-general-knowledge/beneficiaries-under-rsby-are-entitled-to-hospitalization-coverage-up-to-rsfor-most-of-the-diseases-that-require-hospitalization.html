Beneficiaries under RSBY are entitled to hospitalization coverage up to Rs.____for most of the diseases that require hospitalization.

(A) ** Rs. 30, 000/

(B) Rs. 20, 000/

(C) Rs. 50, 000/

(D) Rs. 90, 000/-

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One can claim up to Rs. 30000 for various expenses that are covered under the policy.

Concept note-2: -Benefits of Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana Through this scheme, you will be provided health insurance of Rs. 30000. Under this plan, you will be provided free treatment. Not only this, workers from the unspecified sector can choose their health care options in public and private hospitals.

Concept note-3: -Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana Insurance Coverage The total insured amount under RSBY scheme ranges up to Rs 30, 000 on a family floater basis and up to five family members of a BPL household can be insured under the scheme.

Concept note-4: -Eligibility Criteria for RSBY You need to meet the following eligibility criteria to apply for RSBY: An applicant should be a citizen of India. The family members of the applicant should be listed in the Below Poverty Line (BPL) list. An applicant should be a registered member of the welfare boards.
