The Reserve Bank of India opened a branch in which city to make transactions of the Uttarakhand State government more convenient?

(A) Mussoorie

(B) Rishikesh

(C) Nainital

(D) ** Dehradun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Jurisdiction of this office is the State of Uttarakhand.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India was established on April 1, 1935 in accordance with the provisions of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. The Central Office of the Reserve Bank was initially established in Kolkata but was permanently moved to Mumbai in 1937.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank manages public debt on behalf of the Central and the State Governments. It involves issue of new rupee loans, payment of interest and repayment of these loans and other operational matters such as debt certificates and their registration.

Concept note-4: -RBI has 31 branches in India. Mostly all are in Capital cities, exceptions are the Nagpur Reserve Bank branch which is actually a Second capital of Maharashtra and the Ahmedabad Reserve Bank branch.
