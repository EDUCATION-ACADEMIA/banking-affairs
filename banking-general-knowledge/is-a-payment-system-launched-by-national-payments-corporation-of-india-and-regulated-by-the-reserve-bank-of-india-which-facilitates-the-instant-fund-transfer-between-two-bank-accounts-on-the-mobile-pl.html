____is a payment system launched by National Payments Corporation of India and regulated by the Reserve Bank of India which facilitates the instant fund transfer between two bank accounts on the mobile platform.

(A) NACH

(B) BHIM

(C) ** UPI

(D) CPI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is an instant real-time payment system developed by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). The interface facilitates inter-bank peer-to-peer (P2P) and person-to-merchant (P2M) transactions. It is used on mobile devices to instantly transfer funds between two bank accounts.

Concept note-2: -RTGS is a funds transfer systems where transfer of money takes place from one bank to another on a “real time” and on “gross” basis.

Concept note-3: -BHIM (Bharat Interface for Money) is an Indian mobile payment app developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).

Concept note-4: -IMPS is an emphatic service which allow transferring of funds instantly within banks across India which is not only safe but also economical. Currently on IMPS, 694 members are live which includes banks & PPIs. This facility is provided by NPCI through its existing NFS switch.

Concept note-5: -Ans RBI EFT is a Scheme introduced by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) to help banks offering their customers money transfer service from account to account of any bank branch to any other bank branch in places where EFT services are offered.
