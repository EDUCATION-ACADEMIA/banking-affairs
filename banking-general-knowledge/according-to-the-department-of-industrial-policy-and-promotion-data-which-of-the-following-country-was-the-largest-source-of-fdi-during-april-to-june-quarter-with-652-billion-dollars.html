(B) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sector-wise FDI Equity Inflows during April-September 2022 During the first half of this fiscal, Singapore emerged as the top investor. It was followed by Mauritius, the U.A.E., the U.S.A., the Netherlands and Japan.

Concept note-2: -Top recipients of FDI inflows worldwide in Q3 2022 were the United States (USD 86 billion), Ireland (USD 37 billion) and the United Kingdom (USD 36 billion).
