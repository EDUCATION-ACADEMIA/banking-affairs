(A) ** Invesment management

(B) ** Investment

(C) ** Financial services

(D) ** Social services

(E) Housing developer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A bank is a lawful organisation that accepts deposits that can be withdrawn on demand. Banks are institutions that help the public in the management of their finances, public deposit their savings in banks with the assurance to withdraw money from the deposits whenever required.

Concept note-2: -The 5 most important banking services are checking and savings accounts, loan and mortgage services, wealth management, providing Credit and Debit Cards, Overdraft services.
