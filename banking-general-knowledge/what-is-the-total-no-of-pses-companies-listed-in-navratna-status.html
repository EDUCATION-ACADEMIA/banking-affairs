What is the total No. of PSEs companies listed in Navratna status?

(A) 9

(B) 10

(C) ** 16

(D) 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -14 Navratna Companies – Details.

Concept note-2: -There are 14 Navratna Companies in India till 2022. The companies are SCI, HAL, RINL, EIL, NMDC, BHEL, NBCC, OIL, MTNL, CONCOR, NALCO, REC, NLCIL, and PFC. The government of India provides all these companies with greater financial autonomy.

Concept note-3: -There are 14 Navratna Companies and 12 Maharatna Companies in India as of December 2022.

Concept note-4: -There are 277 Central Public Sector Undertakings in India.

Concept note-5: -Ans. There are a total of 10 Maharatna Companies in India. These include NTPC, ONGC, SAIL, BHEL, IOCL, HPCL, CIL, GAIL, BPCL and Power Grid Corporation of India.
