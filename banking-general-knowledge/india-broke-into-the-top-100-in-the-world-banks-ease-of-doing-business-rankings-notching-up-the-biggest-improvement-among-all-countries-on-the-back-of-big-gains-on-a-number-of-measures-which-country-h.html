(A) Norway

(B) Singapore

(C) ** New Zealand

(D) Switzerland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the World Bank, India ranked 63rd in 2022 in ease of doing business across the world among 190 countries, improving its rank from 142 in 2014.

Concept note-2: -The country has risen significantly in the Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) rankings, making it one of the top performers on a global platform.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is the World Bank.
