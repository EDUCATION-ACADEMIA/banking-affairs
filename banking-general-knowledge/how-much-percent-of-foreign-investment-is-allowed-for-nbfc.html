How much percent of foreign investment is allowed for NBFC?

(A) 50%

(B) 25%

(C) ** 100%

(D) 0%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -8 of Annex B to Schedule 1 of the Principal Regulations permits foreign investment up to 100%, under the automatic route, in Non-Banking Finance Companies (NBFCs) engaged in the 18 activities listed therein.

Concept note-2: -The new norm states 100 % FDI through the Automatic route for NBFC, under the Section 47 of the FEMA Act. Investment in the automatic route was limited to the 18 specified NBFC activities.

Concept note-3: -Automatic upto 74%, Government route beyond that wherever it is likely to result in access to modern technology or for other reasons.
