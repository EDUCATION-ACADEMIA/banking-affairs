Where is the headquarters of North-East Small Finance Bank located in India?

(A) Dispur

(B) Gangtok

(C) ** Guwahati

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global headquarters for North East Small Finance Bank is located in Guwahati, Assam, India.

Concept note-2: -North East Small Finance Bank Limited is the wholly owned subsidiary of RGVN (North East) Microfinance Limited. The only institution from North-Eastern state of India to receive “In-principle approval” to set up Small Finance Bank from Reserve Bank of India on September 16, 2015.

Concept note-3: -North East Small Finance Bank Limited The only institution from North-Eastern state of India to receive this recognition to establish Small Finance Bank.

Concept note-4: -Managing Director & CEO. Ms. Kalita is an experienced development banker with an experience of over 33 years in the Banking and Financial Services sector. She has 20 years of experience in Retail Banking in Langpi Dehangi Rural Bank during her tenure as Senior Manager in the bank.
