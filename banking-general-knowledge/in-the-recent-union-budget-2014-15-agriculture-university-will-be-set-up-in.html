(A) Haryana & Punjab

(B) Uttar Pradesh & Bihar

(C) Gujarat & Maharashtra

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh & Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -8 lakh crore has been set for agriculture credit during 2014-15. Corpus of Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) raised by an additional Rs. 5000 crores from the target given in the Interim Budget to Rs. 25000 crores.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: Union Minister Prahlad Singh Patel on Wednesday said the centre has taken several steps for the growth of the farm sector and to boost farmers’ income and highlighted that nearly ₹ 6.22 lakh crore has been allocated in the Budget for agriculture sector during 2014-22.

Concept note-3: -The total expenditure as per Union Budget 2014-15 is Rs. 17.94892 lakh crores, which is higher by Rs. 31678 crore than the interim budget 2014-15 of Rs. 17.63214 lakh crores.

Concept note-4: -The revised budget for 2014-15 has reduced the education allocation to ₹ 70, 505 crore from ₹ 82, 777 crore as was pegged in the budget estimate.
