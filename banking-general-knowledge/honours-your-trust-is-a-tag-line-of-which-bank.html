“Honours your Trust” is a Tag line of which bank?

(A) Andhra Bank

(B) Vijaya Bank

(C) ** Dena Bank

(D) UCO Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Option 3 is correct, i.e. Honours Your Trust. Founded in 1943, UCO Bank is a commercial bank and a Government of India Undertaking.

Concept note-2: -“Pure Banking, Nothing Else” is the tagline of SBI Bank. State Bank of India (SBI) is a publicly traded Indian bank and provider of financial services worldwide.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution: The Name You Can Bank Upon is the tagline for Punjab National Bank.
