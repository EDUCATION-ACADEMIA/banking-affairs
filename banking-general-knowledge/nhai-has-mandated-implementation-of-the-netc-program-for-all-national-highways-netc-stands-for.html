NHAI has mandated implementation of the NETC program for all national highways. NETC stands for

(A) ** National Electronic Toll Collection

(B) National Electronic Toll Connector

(C) National Electronic Timing Collection

(D) National Electronic Transport Collection

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The system is inter-operable and the same FASTag can be used across all toll plazas under the National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) program.

Concept note-2: -National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) FASTag.

Concept note-3: -NETC-FASTag is a device that employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly while the vehicle is in motion.

Concept note-4: -FASTag is an electronic toll collection system in India, operated by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). It employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid or savings account linked to it or directly toll owner.
