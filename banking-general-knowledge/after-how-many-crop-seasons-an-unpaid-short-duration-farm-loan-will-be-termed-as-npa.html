After how many crop seasons, an unpaid short duration Farm loan will be termed as NPA?

(A) 1 crop season

(B) ** 2 crops seasons

(C) 2 years

(D) 4 crop seasons

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A loan granted for short duration crops will be treated as NPA, if the instalment of principal or interest thereon remains overdue for two crop seasons.

Concept note-2: -A loan granted for short duration crops will be treated as NPA if the instalment of the principal or interest thereon remains unpaid for two crop seasons beyond the due date.

Concept note-3: -(i) Short Term (Seasonal Agricultural Operations) Refinance is provided for production purposes at concessional rate of interest to State Co-operative Banks (StCBs) and Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) by way of sanction of credit limits.
