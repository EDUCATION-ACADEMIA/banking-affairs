Which of the following places in India does not have a stock Exchange?

(A) Kolkata

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** Udaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is a list of sovereign states without a stock exchange: Afghanistan. Andorra. Belize.

Concept note-2: -NSE and BSE are the two major stock exchanges in India. Most of the investors who start their journey in the stock market are aware of that. However, there are more than two stock exchanges in India such as CSE, MSE, etc. According to SEBI, there are total 7 stock exchanges in India.

Concept note-3: -The New York Stock Exchange is the largest stock exchange in the world, with an equity market capitalization of nearly 23 trillion U.S. dollars as of December 2022. The following three exchanges were the NASDAQ, the Shanghai Stock Exchange, and the Euronext. What is a stock exchange?

Concept note-4: -There are a total of 23 stock exchanges in India. A stock exchange is a platform where buyers and sellers come together for trading financial tools such as bonds, stocks and commodities during specific hours of business days.
