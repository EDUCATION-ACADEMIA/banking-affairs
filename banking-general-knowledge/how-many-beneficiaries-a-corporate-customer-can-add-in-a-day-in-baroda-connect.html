How many beneficiaries a Corporate Customer can add in a day in Baroda Connect?

(A) 20

(B) 15

(C) ** 50

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Online Transfer For security reasons, the bank allows you to transfer money up to INR 1, 00, 000/-only to the newly added beneficiary ( in full or parts ) during the first 24 hours from adding the new beneficiary.

Concept note-2: -Corporate customer can perform interbank transfer for multiple beneficiaries with single attempt. Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day. Customer can apply for Debit Card and manage other card activities like: Set daily debit card limit for ATM transactions.

Concept note-3: -It will take a few minutes/hours for the bank to approve the beneficiary. You can transfer money through different types of funds such as RTGS, NEFT, IMPS after the bank approves your beneficiary.
