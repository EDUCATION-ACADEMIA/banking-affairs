Which of the following is NOT a source of income for a bank?

(A) ** the interest earned by depositors

(B) investments

(C) loan income

Concept note-1: -The primary source of income for banks is the difference between the interest charged from the borrowers and the interest paid to the depositors. Banks usually collect higher interest from loans than the interest they provide for deposits.
