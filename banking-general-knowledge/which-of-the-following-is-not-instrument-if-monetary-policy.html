Which of the following is not instrument if monetary policy

(A) Bank rate

(B) Open market operation

(C) Selective credit control

(D) ** Government spending

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, the Call money rate is NOT an instrument of monetary policy in India.

Concept note-2: -Out of the given options, deficit financing is not a monetary tool.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 2, i.e., MSP. MSP is NOT an instrument of RBI’s Monetary Policy.

Concept note-4: -One of the most effective instruments of monetary policy is the bank rate. A bank rate is essentially the rate at which the RBI lends money to commercial banks without any security or collateral. It is also the standard rate at which the RBI will buy or discount bills of exchange and other such commercial instruments.

Concept note-5: -Repo Rate: The (fixed) interest rate at which the Reserve Bank provides overnight liquidity to banks against the collateral of government and other approved securities under the liquidity adjustment facility (LAF).
