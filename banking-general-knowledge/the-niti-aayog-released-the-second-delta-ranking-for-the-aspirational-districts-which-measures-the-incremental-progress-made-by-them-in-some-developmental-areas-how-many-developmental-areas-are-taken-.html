The NITI Aayog released the Second Delta ranking for the Aspirational Districts which measures the incremental progress made by them in some developmental areas. How many developmental areas are taken in count preparing the list?

(A) ** 6

(B) 10

(C) 8

(D) 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A total of 112 Aspirational Districts (AD) across 27 states have been identified by NITI AAYOG based upon composite indicators from Health and Nutrition, Education, Agriculture & Water Resources, Financial Inclusion & Skill development and Basic Infrastructure which have an impact on HDI.

Concept note-2: -Wayanad has been ranked first in the country in the Aspirational Districts Programme (ADP) of the NITI Aayog.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Chandauli.
