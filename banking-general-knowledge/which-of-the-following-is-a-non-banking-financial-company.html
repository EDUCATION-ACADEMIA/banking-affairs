Which of the following is a non-banking financial company?

(A) SBI

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) ** Muthoot Finance Limited

(D) NABARD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -promoted by the Muthoot Pappachan Group is a Non-Banking Finance Company (NBFC) registered with the Reserve Bank of India and its equity shares listed in Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and National Stock Exchange of India (NSE).

Concept note-2: -Muthoot Finance Limited is a gold financing company. The company is a non-banking financial company (NBFC) which is engaged in providing loan (financing) against collateral of gold jewellery.

Concept note-3: -Examples of nonbank financial institutions include insurance firms, venture capitalists, currency exchanges, some microloan organizations, and pawn shops.

Concept note-4: -Non-financial corporations include the following entities: legally constituted corporations, branches of non-resident enterprises, quasi-corporations, notional resident units owning land, and resident non-profit institutions that are market producers of goods or non-financial services.
