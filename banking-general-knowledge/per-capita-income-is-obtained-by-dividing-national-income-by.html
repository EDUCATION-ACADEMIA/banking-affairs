Per capita income is obtained by dividing National Income by

(A) ** total population of the country

(B) total working population

(C) area of the country

(D) volume of the capital used

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The average income is obtained by dividing the total income of a country by its population. Average income is also called per capita income. Per capita income refers to money or income earned per person in a region or state.

Concept note-2: -Per capita income for a nation is calculated by dividing the country’s national income by its population.

Concept note-3: -Per capita income is calculated by dividing the population by the national income.

Concept note-4: -To calculate per capita, take the number you are interested in (e.g. a country’s GDP) and divide it by the population being analyzed.

Concept note-5: -It is calculated by taking a measure of all sources of income in the aggregate (such as GDP or Gross national income) and dividing it by the total population.
