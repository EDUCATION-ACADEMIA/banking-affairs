The Global Wage Report reviews the main trends in wages in developed, emerging and developing countries. It is published by

(A) World Bank

(B) ** International Labour Organisation

(C) World Health Organization

(D) UNESCO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Wage Report 2022-2023-ILO Publications.

Concept note-2: -Global Wage Report 2022-23: The impact of inflation and COVID-19 on wages and purchasing power. This ILO flagship report examines the evolution of real wages, giving a unique picture of wage trends globally and by region.

Concept note-3: -Which organisation released a report “Monitor on the World of Work”? Notes: International Labour Organization (ILO) released the 9th edition of the ILO Monitor on the World of Work.

Concept note-4: -Minimum Wages in India is expected to reach 178.00 INR/Day by the end of 2022, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations.
