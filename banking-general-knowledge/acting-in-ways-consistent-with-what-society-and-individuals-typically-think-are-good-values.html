Acting in ways consistent with what society and individuals typically think are good values.

(A) ** Ethical Behavior

(B) Customer Service

(C) Referrals

(D) Customer Feedback

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acting in ways consistent with what society and individuals typically think are good values. Ethical behavior tends to be good for business and involves demonstrating respect for key moral principles that include honesty, fairness, equality, dignity, diversity and individual rights.

Concept note-2: -Ethical behaviour is characterized by honesty, fairness and equity in interpersonal, professional and academic relationships and in research and scholarly activities. Ethical behaviour respects the dignity, diversity and rights of individuals and groups of people.

Concept note-3: -We often use the word “integrity” to refer to people who act in ways that are consistent with their beliefs. Here consistency means that a person’s actions are in harmony with his or her inner values.

Concept note-4: -They affect how people make decisions and lead their lives. Ethics is concerned with what is good for individuals and society and is also described as moral philosophy. The term is derived from the Greek word ethos which can mean custom, habit, character or disposition.

Concept note-5: -The deontological theory states that people should adhere to their obligations and duties when analyzing an ethical dilemma. This means that a person will follow his or her obligations to another individual or society because upholding one’s duty is what is considered ethically correct (1, 2).
