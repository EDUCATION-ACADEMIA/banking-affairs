Concept note-1: -Anyonya Co-operative Bank Limited (ACBL) located in the city of Vadodara (formerly Baroda) in Gujarat, is the first co-operative bank in India.

Concept note-2: -Citing the latest data, Sitharaman said there are 34 state co-operative banks, 351 DCCBs and 1, 534 UCBs in the country.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank regulates the banking functions of StCBs/DCCBs/UCBs under the provisions of Sections 22 and 23 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Cooperative Societies (AACS).

Concept note-4: -Central cooperative banks are private banks established with the objective of providing loans to cooperatives, individuals and others.
