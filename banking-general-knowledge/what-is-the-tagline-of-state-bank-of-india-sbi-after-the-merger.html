What is the tagline of State Bank of India (SBI) after the merger?

(A) ** Banker to every Indian

(B) The Name you can Bank Upon

(C) A Faithful & Friendly Financial Partner

(D) Experience Next Generation Banking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Pure Banking, Nothing Else” is the tagline of SBI Bank. State Bank of India (SBI) is a publicly traded Indian bank and provider of financial services worldwide.

Concept note-2: -"Pure Banking, Nothing Else” is the tagline of SBI Bank.

Concept note-3: -1. State Bank of India (Group) tagline :-Pure banking nothing else; With you all the way; The Nation banks on us; A Bank to the common man; A banker to every Indian. 2. IDBI Bank tagline :-Banking for All; Aao Sochein Bada.

Concept note-4: -On 1st April 2017, SBI merged with 5 of its associate banks and one other bank. This brought into SBI the 50 largest banks in the world. These associate banks included State Bank of Jaipur, Bikaner, Mysore, Travancore, Hyderabad, and Patiala.

Concept note-5: -State Bank of India Banks on us”, “A Bank of the Common Man”. The Banker To Every Indian”.
