(A) ** Karnataka

(B) West Bengal

(C) Rajasthan

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Asian Development Bank. For the highways up-gradation project in Karnataka, the Asian Development Bank has approved a loan of $ 346 million.

Concept note-2: -The Asian Development Bank (ADB) and the Government of India today signed a $350 million loan to improve the connectivity of key economic areas in the state of Maharashtra.

Concept note-3: -The Asian Development Bank (ADB) and the Government of India today signed a $220 million loan to improve energy security, quality of supply, efficiency, and resilience of power sector in Tripura.

Concept note-4: -ADB assists its members, and partners, by providing loans, technical assistance, grants, and equity investments to promote social and economic development.

Concept note-5: -The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is a regional development bank established on 19 December 1966, which is headquartered in the Ortigas Center located in the city of Mandaluyong, Metro Manila, Philippines.
