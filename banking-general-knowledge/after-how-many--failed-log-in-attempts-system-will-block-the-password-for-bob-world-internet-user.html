After how many ____ failed log in attempts, system will block the password for bob World Internet user?

(A) ** 5 times

(B) 6 times

(C) 3 Times

(D) 10 times

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Visit “Unlock/forgot login PIN” page on login page of the app. Select the country code of your mobile number and enter your registered mobile number without country code in the first field. Enter your existing 4-digit transaction PIN in the second field and proceed to create a new login PIN.

Concept note-2: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-3: -To ban or unblock your BOB ATM card, dial the toll-free number 1800-258-44-55 or 1800-102-44-55 from your registered mobile number 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
