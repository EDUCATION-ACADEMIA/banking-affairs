Which among the following organisation to provide Rs 30, 000 crore credit to farmers for irrigation?

(A) ** NABARD

(B) RBI

(C) SBI

(D) PNB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The state-run National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development, NABARD has set a target of providing 30, 000 crore rupees as credit to farmers for irrigation over the next three years.

Concept note-2: -NABARD provides medium-term credit limits for conversion of short-term crop loans advanced for financing seasonal agricultural operations (SAO) to State Co-operative Banks and Regional Rural Banks for providing relief to the farmers whose crops have been damaged due to natural calamities.
