Which one of the following is not an electronic Banking delivery channel?

(A) Mobile Phone Banking

(B) Internet Banking

(C) ** Mobile Vans

(D) Tele Banking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank overdraft as per Pass Book. Cheques issued but not presented for payment. Cheques deposited with bank but not collected. Cheques entered in Cash Book but not banked.

Concept note-2: -The major types of E-banking are online internet banking, mobile banking, automated teller machine (ATM), and debit and credit cards.

Concept note-3: -Electronic banking can be defined as the use of electronic delivery channels for banking products and services, and is a subset of electronic finance. * The most important electronic delivery channels are the Internet, wireless communication networks, automatic teller machines (ATMs), and telephone banking.
