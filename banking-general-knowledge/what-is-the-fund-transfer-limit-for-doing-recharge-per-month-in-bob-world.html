What is the fund transfer limit for doing recharge per month in BOB WORLD?

(A) ** 250000

(B) 500000

(C) 1000000

(D) 100000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-2: -Amount up to Rs. 50, 000 may be paid in cash to a beneficiary in India. Any remittance exceeding Rs. 50, 000 shall be paid entirely by means of account payee cheque, demand draft, payment order, etc., or credited directly to the beneficiary’s bank account only.

Concept note-3: -The IMPS limit depends on the respective bank’s policy and can range anywhere between ₹10, 000 and ₹2 lakh.

Concept note-4: -What is the NEFT limit in Bank of Baroda? Bank of Baroda has a maximum limit of Rs. 5 lakhs for NEFT transfers done through mobile banking or internet banking. For NEFT transactions done through the bank branch, there is no limit.
