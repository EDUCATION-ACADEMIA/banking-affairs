POSCO is a company which manufactures

(A) Cement

(B) Pharma products

(C) Sugar

(D) ** Steel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -POSCO was launched on April 1, 1968 with a mission for national industrialization. As the first integrated steel mill in Korea, Posco has grown to produce 41 million tons of crude steel a year, and has grown to become a global business with production and sales in 53 countries in the world.

Concept note-2: -About POSCO integrated steel project POSCO, a South Korean multinational corporation and the world’s fourth largest steel producer, sought to establish an integrated steel venture in India’s eastern state of Odisha. POSCO, via its wholly-owned and operated subsidiary POSCO-India Pvt.

Concept note-3: -Apart from Odisha, POSCO India has sites in Karnataka and Maharashtra. So, the correct answer is Pohang Iron and Steel Company.

Concept note-4: -POSCO operates two integrated steel mills in South Korea and a joint venture facility with U.S. Steel, USS-POSCO Industries, located in California.

Concept note-5: -POSCO India Private Limited (commonly POSCO India or Posco-India) is an Indian subsidiary of Korean conglomerate POSCO.
