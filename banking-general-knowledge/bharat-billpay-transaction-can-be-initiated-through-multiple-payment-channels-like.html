Bharat BillPay transaction can be initiated through multiple payment channels like

(A) Internet Banking

(B) Mobile-Banking

(C) Point of Sale terminal

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Different payment channels and payment modes: Bharat BillPay transactions can be initiated through multiple payment channels, such as Netbanking, mobile banking, mobile wallets, kiosk, ATM, bank branch, agents and business correspondents.

Concept note-2: -The BBPS system, with its two components – the BBPCU and the BBPOUs – will function as an authorised payment system under the Payment and Settlement Systems Act, 2007.

Concept note-3: -What is the Bharat Bill Payments System? It is an integrated bill payment system or a platform which acts as a connect between various billers and users. The Bharat Bill Payment System offers customers the convenience of payment by cataloguing various utility providers under one platform.

Concept note-4: -It offers bill payment services to customers through network of agents or online, allowing multiple payment modes and provides instant confirmation. It will facilitate a cashless society through migration of bill payments from cash to electronic channel.

Concept note-5: -Bharat Bill Payment System is a Reserve Bank of India (RBI) conceptualised system driven by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) which offers integrated, accessible and interoperable recurring payment services to consumers across geographies with certainty, reliability and safety of transactions.
