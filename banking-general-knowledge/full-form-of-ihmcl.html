Full Form of IHMCL

(A) India Highway Management Company Limited

(B) India Highway company Limited

(C) ** Indian Highway Management Company Limited

(D) Indian Highway company limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IHMCL – Indian Highway Management Company Limited.

Concept note-2: -CEO IHMCL & Chief General Manager (T) in National Highways Authority of India. Akhilesh Srivastava is B. Tech., MBA, FIE, FIV, LLB.

Concept note-3: -Indian Highways Management Company Limited (IHMCL) is a company jointly promoted by National Highways Authority of India jointly with its Concessionaires and Financial Institutions to carry out Electronic Tolling and other ancillary projects of NHAI.

Concept note-4: -FASTag is a device that employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid account linked to it. It is affixed on the windscreen of your vehicle and enables you to drive through toll plazas, without stopping for cash transactions.

Concept note-5: -Key Takeaways. FASTag is a Radio Frequency Identification tag developed by the NETC. FASTag allows you to make cashless toll payments while the vehicle is in motion.
