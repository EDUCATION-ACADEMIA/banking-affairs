The main function of commercial bank can be segregated into

1. :

payment system.

2. :

financial intermediation.

3. :

financial services.

Select the correct answer using the codes given below

(A) ** 1, 2 and 3

(B) 1 and 3

(C) 1 and 2

(D) 2 and 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main purpose of commercial banks is to provide financial services to the general public and also provide loan facilities to the business which helps in ensuring economic stability and growth of the economy. Therefore, we can say that credit creation is the most important purpose of commercial banks.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Accepting deposits, providing loans, mortgages, cash, borrowing, overdraft, and bill discounting are all primary responsibilities.
