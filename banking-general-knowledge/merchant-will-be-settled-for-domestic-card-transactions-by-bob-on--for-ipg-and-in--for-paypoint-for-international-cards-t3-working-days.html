Merchant will be settled for domestic card transactions by BOB on ____ for IPG and in ____ for Paypoint. For international cards T+3 (working days).

(A) T+2 (working days), T+2 (working day)

(B) ** T+1 (working days), T+2 (working day)

(C) T+1 (working days), T+3 (working day)

(D) T+2 (working days), T+1 (working day)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda provides a facility, to receive payment online from any debit/credit card, UPI (VPA & QR code), wallet, net banking (through aggregator) of more than 40+ banks, called ‘Baroda e-Gateway’ (Internet Payment Gateway), which is a safe, secure, and trusted payment solution for e-commerce/online business.

Concept note-2: -Internet Payment Gateway (IPG) is an e-commerce application service that authorizes payments for e-businesses e.g online payments (fee/fines/deposits)

Concept note-3: -Once a transaction has been approved, settlement is the second and final step. This is when the issuing bank transfers the funds from the cardholder’s account to the payment processor, who then transfers the money to the acquiring bank.
