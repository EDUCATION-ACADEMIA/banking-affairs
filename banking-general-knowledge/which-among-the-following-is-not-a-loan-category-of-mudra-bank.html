Which among the following is not a loan category of MUDRA bank?

(A) Shishu

(B) ** Arun

(C) Kishor

(D) Tarun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUDRA loans are available in three categories. For small business, loans upto 50000/-/-is available under the ‘Shishu’ category and beyond 50, 000 and up to 5 lakh under the ‘Kishor’ category. It also offers loans beyond 5 lakh and up to 10 lakh under the Tarun category.

Concept note-2: -There are three types of mudra loan schemes, namely Tarun, Kishor, and Shishu.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is (d) Payday lender.

Concept note-4: -It has no provision for concessional loans. This ‘refinance’ banking model is to be implemented through the public and private sector banks, NBFCs, MFLs, RRBs, District Banks, etc. Was this answer helpful?
