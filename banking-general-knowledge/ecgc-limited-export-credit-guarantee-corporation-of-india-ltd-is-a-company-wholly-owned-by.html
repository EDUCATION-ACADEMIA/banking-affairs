ECGC Limited (Export Credit Guarantee Corporation of India Ltd) is a company wholly owned by?

(A) RBI

(B) ** Government of India

(C) IRDAI

(D) NABARD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ECGC Ltd. (formerly known as Export Credit Guarantee Corporation of India Ltd.) wholly owned by government of India, was set up in 1957 with the objective of promoting exports from the country by providing credit risk insurance and related services for exports.

Concept note-2: -ECGC Limited (Formerly Export Credit Guarantee Corporation of India Limited) is a government owned export credit agency of India. It is under the ownership of Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India, and is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Concept note-3: -About ECGC The Company functions under the administrative control of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry.

Concept note-4: -ECGC is a government-owned export credit provider that comes under the ownership of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The details advertisement for ECGC PO 2023 will be released on the official website https://www.ecgc.in/.
