(A) Public Sector Bank

(B) ** Private Sector Bank

(C) Co-operative Bank

(D) Foreign Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Axis Bank is the third largest private sector bank in India. The Bank offers the entire spectrum of financial services to customer segments covering Large and Mid-Corporates, MSME, Agriculture and Retail Businesses.

Concept note-2: -Axis Bank established in 1993 was the first of the new private banks to have begun operations in 1994 after the Government of India allowed new private banks to be established. Axis Bank Ltd. has been promoted by the largest and the best Financial Institution of the country, UTI.

Concept note-3: -HDFC is the number one private bank in India.
