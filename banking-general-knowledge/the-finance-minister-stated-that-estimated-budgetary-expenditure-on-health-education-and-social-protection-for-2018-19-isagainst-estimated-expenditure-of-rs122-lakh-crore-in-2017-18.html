The Finance Minister stated that estimated budgetary expenditure on health, education and social protection for 2018-19 is____against estimated expenditure of Rs.1.22 lakh crore in 2017-18.

(A) Rs.1.52 lakh crore

(B) ** Rs.1.38 lakh crore

(C) Rs.1.27 lakh crore

(D) Rs.1.63 lakh crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Finance Minister said that estimated budgetary expenditure on health, education and social protection for 2018-19 is Rs. 1.38 lakh crore against estimated expenditure of Rs. 1.22 lakh crore in 2017-18 .

Concept note-2: -At the end of fiscal year 2018, the budgeted estimate of revenue expenditure on non-developmental expenses of India’s central and state governments amounted to more than 22 trillion Indian rupees and developmental expenses amounted to approximately 19 trillion rupees.

Concept note-3: -As indicated in Figure 1, the allocation to the Department of Health and Family Welfare has increased from Rs 11, 366 crore in 2006-07 to Rs 52, 800 crore in 2018-19.

Concept note-4: -It was presented to Parliament on 1 February 2018 by Finance Minister Arun Jaitley.

Concept note-5: -Total budgeted expenditure for 2018-19 is set at Rs 2, 442, 213 crore (US$ 383.93 billion). Central Government’s debt to GDP ratio will be brought down to 40 per cent, as per the recommendations of the Fiscal Reform and Budget Management Committee.
