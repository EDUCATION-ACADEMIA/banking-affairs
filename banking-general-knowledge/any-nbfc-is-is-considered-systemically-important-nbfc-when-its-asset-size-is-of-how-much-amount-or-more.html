Any NBFC is is considered systemically important NBFC when its asset size is of how much amount or more?

(A) Rs 100 Crore

(B) ** Rs 500 Crore

(C) Rs 250 Crore

(D) Rs 750 Crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All NBFCs – ND with an asset size of Rs. 500 crore and more as per the last audited balance sheet will be considered as a systemically important NBFC–ND (NBFC-ND-SI).

Concept note-2: -What are systemically important NBFCs? NBFCs whose asset size is of ₹ 500 cr or more as per last audited balance sheet are considered as systemically important NBFCs.

Concept note-3: -"Standard asset” means an asset in respect of which, no default in repayment of principal or payment of interest is perceived and which does not disclose any signs of impairment nor carry more than normal risk attached to the business.

Concept note-4: -Based on consolidation of assets of all the NBFCs in the Group, the consolidated asset size of the Group becomes ₹1320 crore (higher than the asset size threshold of ₹1000 core for classification in Middle Layer). As such, NBFC-ICC and NBFC-MFI will be classified in the Middle Layer.

Concept note-5: -As per AS 26, Financial Asset includes all deposits and investments in Shares, Debentures, etc.
