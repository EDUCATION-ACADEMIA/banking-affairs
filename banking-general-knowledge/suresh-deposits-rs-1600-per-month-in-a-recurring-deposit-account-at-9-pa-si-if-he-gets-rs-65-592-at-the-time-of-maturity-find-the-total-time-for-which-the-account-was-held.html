Suresh deposits Rs. 1600 per month in a recurring deposit account at 9% p.a. S.I. If he gets Rs. 65, 592 at the time of maturity, find the total time for which the account was held.

(A) 2 years

(B) 1 year

(C) ** 3 years

(D) 4 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maturity Value= Total Deposited Amount + Total Simple Interest.

Concept note-2: -Hence Matured value =Rs. 40440.

Concept note-3: -Rs. 150 for 8 months is 150×8=1200. Therefore, the maturity value Amit will have at the end of 8 months is Rs. 1236.

Concept note-4: -The correct option is B Mrs. Goswami deposits Rs 1000 every month in a recurring deposit account for 3 years at 8% interest per annum.
