Who is appointed as the new Director-general of Boarder security Force

(A) ** Rajni Kant Mishra

(B) KK Sharma

(C) Rajeesh kumar

(D) K Sivan

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government on Thursday appointed Rajni Kant Mishra as the Director-General of Border Security Force (BSF) in the place of K.K. Sharma, who is superannuating on September 30.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Pankaj Kumar Singh. Pankaj Kumar Singh, a 1988-batch Indian Police Service officer, was appointed as Director-General of the Border Security Force (BSF) on 25 August 2021. Singh is presently serving as the Special Director General of the BSF.

Concept note-3: -First ever woman chief of SSB retires; Rajni Kant Mishra takes charge. A post-graduate in science, Rajni Kant Mishra served in different capacities in both Uttar Pradesh and central governments.

Concept note-4: -ITBP Director General Anis Dayal gets addition charge of SSB Chief.

Concept note-5: -Sh. Anish Dayal Singh, IPS, DG, SSB Visited 41st Bn SSB, Ranidanga, West Bengal. Sh. Anish Dayal Singh, IPS took over as the Director General of Sashastra Seema Bal on 02 Oct, 2022.
