For Reissuance, Re-PIN, Link, De-Link and De-blocking of Debit Card which menu is used

(A) HDCR

(B) HBDTM

(C) ** DCISS

(D) HACM

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You need to login to the respective bank’s internet banking portal. Once you log in, go to the section marked as ‘Debit Card’. There will be instructions on how to generate an ATM PIN for your Debit Card. Generate an ATM PIN by following those instructions and your Debit Card will be activated online.

Concept note-2: -If you have blocked the card due to suspicious activity or due to your negligence and now would like to unblock it, you will have to place a request at the nearest bank branch. You may have to provide your identity proof along with your account number so that the bank can unblock the card.

Concept note-3: -Calling our 24-hour Customer Contact Centre at 1860 266 2666, or. Logging in to net banking, or. SMS DCBLOCK XXXX (last 4 digits your Kotak Debit Card number), or. By visiting your nearest Kotak Mahindra Bank branch.
