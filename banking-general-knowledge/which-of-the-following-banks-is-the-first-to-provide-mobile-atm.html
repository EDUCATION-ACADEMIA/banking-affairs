Which of the following banks is the first to provide Mobile ATM?

(A) HSBC

(B) ** ICICI BANK

(C) SBI

(D) Axis Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s largest private sector bank, ICICI launched the “ATM on Wheels” country first Mobile ATM, in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -ICICI Bank was the first bank to introduce internet banking facility in the country in order to enable individuals and corporates to transact online without visiting the branch. It was also the first bank to start mobile banking in the country.

Concept note-3: -In the year 2008, ICICI Bank was the first bank in India to launch mobile banking. Almost every bank has since followed suit.
