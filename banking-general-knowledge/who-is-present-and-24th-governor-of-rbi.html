Who is present and 24th governor of RBI?

(A) ** Urjit Patel

(B) Ajay Tyagi

(C) BP Kanungo

(D) Raghuram Rajan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Urjit R. Patel assumed charge as the twenty-fourth Governor of the Reserve Bank of India effective September 4, 2016 after serving as Deputy Governor since January 2013. He was re-appointed as Deputy Governor on January 11, 2016 after completion of his first three-year term of office.

Concept note-2: -Shri Shaktikanta Das, IAS Retd., former Secretary, Department of Revenue and Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India assumed charge as the 25th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India effective December 12, 2018.

Concept note-3: -Shri Shaktikanta Das, Governor, Reserve Bank of India, held a meeting with select FinTech entities (including AgriTechs) and some of their Associations on December 14, 2022.
