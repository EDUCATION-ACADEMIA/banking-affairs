IFCI was the first Development Financial Institution of India set up to propel economic growth through the development of infrastructure and industry. IFCI established in

(A) 1964

(B) 1956

(C) 1952

(D) ** 1948

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Against this backdrop and to bridge the demand supply gap for capital needs of the economy, the Government of India established The Industrial Finance Corporation of India (IFCI) on July 1, 1948 by enacting the IFC Act 1948.

Concept note-2: -IFCI Ltd (IFCI) was set up as a Statutory Corporation (“The Industrial Finance Corporation of India”) in 1948 for providing medium and long term finance to industry.. In 1993, after repeal of the Industrial Finance Corporation Act, IFCI became a Public Limited Company, registered under the Companies Act, 1956.

Concept note-3: -IFCI (industrial financial corporation of india) was first development bank of india in 1948.
