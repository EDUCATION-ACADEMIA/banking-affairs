What is maximum per transaction withdrawal limit at ATM The bob world “Yoddha” Debit card

(A) ** Rs.150000

(B) Rs. 100000

(C) Rs. 500000

(D) Rs. 50000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Un-limited Free ATM usage at all Bank’s ATMs. Max. per day limit at POS/Ecom. is Rs. 1, 00, 000/-.

Concept note-2: -Daily cash withdrawal Rs. 1, 50, 000 from ATM. Daily POS/E-commerce transactions Up to Rs 5, 00, 000.

Concept note-3: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day. Customer can apply for Debit Card and manage other card activities like: Set daily debit card limit for ATM transactions.

Concept note-4: -Rupay classic contactless Card (NCMC) Usage limit of Rs.25000/-in ATM and Rs.25000/-in POS. Facility of contactless POS purchases up to Rs.5000/-.

Concept note-5: -Log in to Bank of Baroda Mobile Banking application and tap on Request Services. In the Debit Card section, tap and open Set Debit Card Limit. Next screen tap on SET LIMIT. Here you can view your current BOB ATM Cash withdrawal and Online/POS transaction limit.
