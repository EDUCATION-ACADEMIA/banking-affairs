The Defence Acquisition Council (DAC) accorded approval for the acquisition of defence equipment worth nearly Rs____crore which includes BrahMos Missiles, and Armoured Recovery Vehicles (ARVs) for Battle Tank Arjun.

(A) Rs 4000 crore

(B) Rs 1000 crore

(C) Rs 7000 crore

(D) ** Rs 3000 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The DAC accorded AoN for procurement of HELINA Anti-Tank Guided Missiles, launchers and associated support equipment which will be integrated to the Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH). This missile is an essential part of weaponisation of ALH for countering enemy threat.

Concept note-2: -DAC is the highest decision-making body in the Defence Ministry for deciding on new policies and capital acquisitions for the three services – Army, Navy and Air Force, and the Indian Coast Guard. The Defence Acquisition Council is headed by the Defence Minister.

Concept note-3: -The Defence Minister is the chairman of the DAC.

Concept note-4: -A Rs 1, 700-crore agreement for the supply of additional BrahMos supersonic cruise missiles was signed on Thursday between the Defence ministry’s acquisitions wing and the M/s BrahMos Aerospace Pvt Ltd.
