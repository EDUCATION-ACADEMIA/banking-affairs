Indian Banks’ Association, formed on 26 September 1946 as a representative body of management of banking in India operating in India-an association of Indian banks and financial institutions based in Mumbai. Who is present Chairman of IBA?

(A) Jatinder Bir Singh

(B) Dinabandhu Mohapatra

(C) ** Sunil Mehta

(D) Rajnish Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Banks’ Association reelected A K Goel, managing director and CEO of Punjab National Bank as its new chairman, who will serve the industry body for the financial year 2022-23. The reelection was one of the rare occasions in history when an incumbent IBA chairman obtained a second consecutive term.

Concept note-2: -IBA: Indian Banks’ Association.

Concept note-3: -The banks which are members of the managing committee of the IBA include: Citibank. Indian Bank. Indian Overseas Bank.
