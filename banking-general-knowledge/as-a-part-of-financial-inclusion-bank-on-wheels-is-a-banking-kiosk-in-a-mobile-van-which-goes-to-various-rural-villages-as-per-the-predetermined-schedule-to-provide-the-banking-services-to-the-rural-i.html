(A) ** General Packet Radio Service

(B) General Packet Running Service

(C) Gross Packet Radio Service

(D) Gain Packet Radio Service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -by developing a culture of savings among large segment of rural population. Further, financial inclusion protects their financial wealth and other resources in exigent circumstances by bringing low income groups within the perimeter of formal banking sector.

Concept note-2: -Financial inclusion means that individuals and businesses have access to useful and affordable financial products and services such as transactions, payments, savings, credit and insurance that meet their needs and are delivered responsibly and sustainably.

Concept note-3: -Partnering with various stakeholders such as microfinance institutions, small finance banks, and commercial banks and taking the digital route will broaden the scope of financial inclusion and credit offtake.

Concept note-4: -Financial inclusion aims to bring in digital financial solutions for the economically underprivileged people of the nation. It also intends to bring in mobile banking or financial services in order to reach the poorest people living in extremely remote areas of the country.
