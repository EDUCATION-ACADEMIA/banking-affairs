Concept note-1: -Indian Banks’ Association (IBA), formed on (26 September 1946) as a representative body of management of banking in India operating in India-an association of Indian banks and financial institutions based in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -Indian Banks’ Association reelected A K Goel, managing director and CEO of Punjab National Bank as its new chairman, who will serve the industry body for the financial year 2022-23.
