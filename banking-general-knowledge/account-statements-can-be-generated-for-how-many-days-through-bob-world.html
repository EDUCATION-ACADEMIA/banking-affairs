Account Statements can be generated for how many days through bob World?

(A) ** 90 days or 3 months

(B) 180 days or 6 months

(C) 12 months

(D) 200 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Customers can opt for the Bank of Baroda account statement online through the net banking portal provided by the bank. After a successful login, select the time period and download the BOB statement PDF.

Concept note-2: -Through the bank’s offered net banking interface, customers can choose to request their Bank of Baroda account statement online. All one needs to do is select the time period and get the BOB statement PDF after logging in successfully. From your registered phone number, you must dial 8468001122 in a missed call.

Concept note-3: -You can get a copy of your mini statement by calling 8468001122 or texting ‘MINIspace>last 4-digits of A/C No. ‘ to 8422009988. You can also log in to the BOB Connect Net Banking Portal or download and use the Baroda M-Connect Plus mobile application. Mini statements are also available at any BOB or other bank’s ATM.

Concept note-4: -Bank of Baroda 1-Year Account Statement After login, click on the Accounts tab. Here you can see the Account statement more than 6 months, click on it. And the next screen select the account statement format. Select PDF or excel format and click on the statement to download the file.
