Which of the following is/are NOT the functions of a bank?

1. :

Accepting deposits from the public

2. :

Grant of loans and advances

3. :

Providing finance to all those who cannot earn enough to survive

(A) Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) ** Only 3

(D) All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Facilitating import of goods is not a primary function of a bank.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The incorrect statement is To accept deposits on interest from the public.

Concept note-3: -Loan is advanced by Commercial Bank in the form of Cash Credit, Loan & Advances & Overdraft. To supply input to farmers is not a function of a Bank.
