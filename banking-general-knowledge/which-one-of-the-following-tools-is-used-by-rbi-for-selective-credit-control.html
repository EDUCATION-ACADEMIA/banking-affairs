Which one of the following tools is used by RBI for selective credit control?

(A) It advises banks to lend against certain commodities

(B) ** It advises banks to recall the loans for advances against certain commodities

(C) It advises banks to charge higher rate of interest for advance against certain commodities.

(D) It discourages certain kinds of lending by assigning higher risk weights to loans it deems undesirable.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank rate is the selective credit control measure used by the central bank of the country.

Concept note-2: -Credit rationing is a selective qualitative credit control method.

Concept note-3: -Rationing of credit is a selective method adopted by the central bank for controlling and regulating the purpose for which credit is granted or allocated by commercial banks.

Concept note-4: -The important qualitative or selective methods of credit control are; (a) Marginal Requirements, (b) Regulation of Consumer Credit, (c) Credit Rationing, (d) Moral Suasion and (e) Direct Action. Quantitative or General Methods: 1. Bank Rate: It is the also known as the discount rate.
