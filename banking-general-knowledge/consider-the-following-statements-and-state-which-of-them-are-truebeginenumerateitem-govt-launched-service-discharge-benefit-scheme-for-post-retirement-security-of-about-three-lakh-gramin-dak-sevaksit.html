Consider the following statements and state which of them are true?

1. :

Govt. launched service discharge benefit scheme for post retirement security of about three lakh Gramin Dak Sevaks.

2. :

Govt. will deposit two hundred rupees per month in accounts of Gramin Dak Sevaks working in far flung areas.

3. :

Scheme is being functionized by utilizing funds from PFRDA.

4. :

Government will provide over 70 crore Rs. annually for this scheme.

(A) 2 and 4

(B) 1, 3 and 4

(C) 1 and 3

(D) ** All are true

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gramin Dak Sevaks are also referred to as extradepartmental agents in the postal department. As per Central Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1972, the minimum service period in regular employment in the said Department for being entitled to pension is 10 years.

Concept note-2: -A global distribution system (GDS) is a computerised network system owned or operated by a company that enables transactions between travel industry service providers, mainly airlines, hotels, car rental companies, and travel agencies.

Concept note-3: -The Full form of GDS is Gramin Dak Sevak. Under GDS Recruitment, the Department of Post, India offers two vacancies i.e BPM and ABPM. The Full form of BPM is Branch Post Master and ABPM Full Form is Assistant Branch Post Master.
