Change 6.75% to a decimal.

(A) 67.5

(B) .675

(C) 675

(D) ** .0675

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How do You Write a 6.75% as a Decimal? You write the percentage as 0.0675 in decimal notation. What is 6.75 Percent in Decimal Form? In decimal form, 6.75 percent equals 0.0675.

Concept note-2: -Solution: 6.75 as a fraction is 27/4.

Concept note-3: -6.75 as a percentage of certain number x can be calculated by dividing 6.75 by x, and multiplying the result by 100. For example, 6.75 as a percentage of 10 = (6.75 / 10) x 100% = 67.5%. Feel free to use our decimals to percents calculator below.

Concept note-4: -To convert a decimal to a percentage, multiply by 100 (just move the decimal point 2 places to the right). For example, 0.065 = 6.5% and 3.75 = 375%.

Concept note-5: -Noah’s son’s age as a decimal is 6.75 years old. And we decided that we could write this as a mixed number as six and 75 hundredths.
