What is the minimum start up capital fixed for Local Area Capital Banks?

(A) Rs 1 crore

(B) Rs 2 crore

(C) ** Rs 5 crore

(D) Rs 10 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Local Area Banks have jurisdiction over a maximum of three contiguous districts, and their basic function is to mobilise funds in rural and semi-urban areas. The minimum start-up capital of a LAB was fixed at Rs. 5 crore and the promoters were asked to bring entire minimum share capital up-front.

Concept note-2: -The minimum net worth of such small finance banks shall be Rs. 100 crore from the date of commencement of business.
