Which of the following defines ‘At sight’ under section 21 of the Negotiable Instrument Act, 1881?

(A) ** on presentation

(B) on demand

(C) on coming into vision

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -21. The expression “after sight” means, in a promissory note, after presentment for sight, and, in a bill of exchange, after acceptance, or noting for non-acceptance, or protest for non-acceptance.

Concept note-2: -Section 13 in The Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881.

Concept note-3: -14. Negotiation.-When a promissory note, bill of exchange or cheque is transferred to any person, so as to constitute the person the holder thereof, the instrument is said to be negotiated.
