Consider the following statements:

1. :

Allahabad Bank was the first bank to be established exclusively by Indians.

2. :

Seven banks forming subsidiary of State Bank of India was nationalized in 1960.

3. :

SBI was formed in 1949.

Which of the statements given above is/are correct?

(A) Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) ** Both 1 and 2

(D) Both 2 and 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oudh Commercial Bank or Awadh Commercial Bank was an Indian bank established in 1881 in Faizabad and operated until 1958 when it failed. It was the first commercial bank in India having limited liability and an entirely Indian board of directors.

Concept note-2: -Allahabad Bank was an Indian nationalised bank with its headquarters in Kolkata, India. Founded in Allahabad in 1865 and nationalized by the government of India in 1969, the bank provided banking and financial services for 155 years until it was merged with Indian Bank in 2020.

Concept note-3: -The first bank of India was the “Bank of Hindustan”, established in 1770 and located in the then Indian capital, Calcutta.
