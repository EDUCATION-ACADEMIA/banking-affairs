What is the minimum period for which an NRE (Non Resident External) term deposit account can be opened?

(A) ** 12 months

(B) 36 months

(C) 60 months

(D) 52 months

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With effect from April 29, 2003, the minimum maturity period for NRE deposits has been raised from six months to one year, making the range of the maturity period for fresh NRE term deposits from one to three years, in line with FCNR(B) deposits.

Concept note-2: -If the deposit is closed before 12 months from the date of deposit, no interest is payable, and thus no penalty will be charged. This is because the minimum duration of an NRE Fixed Deposit is 1 year.

Concept note-3: -For NRE Term Deposits, minimum period is 1 Year and maximum up to 10 Years.

Concept note-4: -What is the minimum and maximum period for NRE deposits? The minimum is one year and the maximum is currently 10 years.

Concept note-5: -Period of fixed deposit is from a Minimum 15 days to Maximum of 10 years.
