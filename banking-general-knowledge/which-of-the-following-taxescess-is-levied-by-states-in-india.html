Which of the following taxes/cess is levied by States in India?

(A) Tax on motor vehicles

(B) Educational cess

(C) Tax on hotels

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professional tax, VAT, and motor vehicle tax are some of the taxes that are levied and collected by the state.

Concept note-2: -The taxation system in India empowers the state governments to levy income tax on agricultural income, professional tax, value added tax (VAT), state excise duty, land revenue and stamp duty.

Concept note-3: -The Central Government levies both cess and surcharge. Both are collected and taken into the Consolidated Fund of India. State Governments cannot share any of these.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Taxes on lands and buildings. Taxes levied, collected and retained by the states: Taxes on mineral resources.

Concept note-5: -Which among the following taxes is levied by state government only? Notes: Entertainment tax is levied by state government only. Now it has been subsumed under Goods and Service Tax by 101st Amendment to the Indian Constitution.
