NBHC is an Indian commodity and collateral management company. What is the full form of NBHC?

(A) ** National Bulk Handling Corporation

(B) National Bulk Handling Company

(C) National Banking Handling Corporation

(D) National Branding Handling Corporation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Bulk Handling Corporation Pvt. Ltd. (NBHC)-Adding Value to Commodities :: An ISO 9001:2000 and ISO 22000:2005 Certified Company.

Concept note-2: -National Bulk Handling Corporation Pvt. It has 45+ functional QA labs and 200 + mobile labs for quality testing of around 170 agri-commodities, as well as pest management services across 16 states. NBHC also works closely with Government Organizations such as Food Corporation of India.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Ramesh Doraiswami, Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer. Ramesh has over 34 years of leadership experience across industry verticals and diverse company cultures. He brings a strong blend of functional and general management skills to NBHC.

Concept note-4: -What is the Operating Revenue of NATIONAL BULK HANDLING CORPORATION PRIVATE LIMITED? The operating revenue of NATIONAL BULK HANDLING CORPORATION PRIVATE LIMITED is in the range of INR 100 cr-500 cr for the financial year ending on 31 March, 2022.
