The main function of Central Statistical Organisation is____

(A) To determine the money supply

(B) To determine prices

(C) ** To publish national income estimates

(D) To collect employment details

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Central Statistics Office coordinates the statistical activities in the country and evolves statistical standards. It is headed by a Director General assisted by 5 Additional Director Generals.

Concept note-2: -The Central Statistical Organisation (CSO) which has the responsibility of preparing national income estimates has divided the economy into 13 sectors, grouped under five main headings. It prepares the estimate of net domestic product.

Concept note-3: -Central Statistical Organisation (CSO).

Concept note-4: -NSSO and CSO Merger The central government, on 23 May 2019, approved this merger making it official. NSSO was the largest organization in India that conducted surveys whereas CSO was responsible for ensuring that all the standards for statistical operations in the country were met.
