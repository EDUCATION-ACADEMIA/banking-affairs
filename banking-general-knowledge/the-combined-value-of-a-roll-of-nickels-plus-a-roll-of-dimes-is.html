The combined value of a roll of nickels plus a roll of dimes is

(A) $12.

(B) ** $ 7.

(C) $20.

(D) $10.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Likewise, the valueof x nickels is 5x cents. If the money box in a drink machine contains x nickels, 3x dimes, and (30x-1) quarters, express their total value in cents as an algebraic expression.

Concept note-2: -If you figured out the bonus question above, you know that there are: 40 quarters per roll. 50 dimes per roll. 40 nickels per roll.

Concept note-3: -Conversion of Nickels into Dollars As 100 cents form a dollar, and 1 nickel equals five cents, we can say that 20 nickels make up a dollar.

Concept note-4: -Different Ways to Make a Dollar Can you make a dollar using only one type of coin? Answer: 100 pennies, 20 nickels, 10 dimes, or 4 quarters; each = 1 dollar.
