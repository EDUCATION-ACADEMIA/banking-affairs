EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 13th Ministerial Conference of the World Trade Organization will be held in Abu Dhabi in the first quarter of 2024.

Concept note-2: -The United Arab Emirates (UAE) will host the 13th World Trade Organisation (WTO) Ministerial meeting in February 2024.

Concept note-3: -WTO members successfully concluded the 12th Ministerial Conference (MC12) in Geneva on 17 June, securing multilaterally negotiated outcomes on a series of key trade initiatives.

Concept note-4: -The Eleventh Ministerial Conference (MC11) took place from 10 to 13 December 2017 in Buenos Aires, Argentina. It was chaired by Minister Susana Malcorra of Argentina.
