Committee related with the merger of regional rural banks with their sponsor banks is____

(A) ** Khusro Committee

(B) Khan Committee

(C) Malegam Committee

(D) Hilton Young Committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which committee recommended the merger of Regional Rural Banks with their respective Sponsor Banks? Explanation: The Agricultural Credit Review Committee, Khusro Committee, 1989 recommended the merger of Regional Rural Banks with their respective Sponsor Banks.

Concept note-2: -The function of Khusro committee under the chairmanship of Prof. A.M. Khusro was to examine the problems of agricultural and rural credits, measure the structural and operational deficiencies and recommend major systematic improvements.

Concept note-3: -Notes: The Agricultural Credit Review Committee (Khusro Committee), 1989 recommended the merger of Regional Rural Banks with their respective Sponsor Banks. It highlighted the weakness of RRBs and their non-viability.
