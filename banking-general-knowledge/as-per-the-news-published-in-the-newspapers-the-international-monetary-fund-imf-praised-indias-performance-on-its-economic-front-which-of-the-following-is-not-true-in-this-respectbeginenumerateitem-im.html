1. :

IMF favoured the cuts in fuel subsidies as part of expenditure measures to create space for spending on priority sectors.

2. :

IMF was not in favour of huge foreign fund flow to India as it has created an imbalance at regional level.

3. :

IMF wants some more structural reforms to be implemented quickly.

Select the correct answer using the codes given below

(A) Only 1

(B) ** Only 2

(C) Only 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) works to achieve sustainable growth and prosperity for all of its 190 member countries. It does so by supporting economic policies that promote financial stability and monetary cooperation, which are essential to increase productivity, job creation, and economic well-being.

Concept note-2: -It is true that on becoming its member, countries get finance as temporary balance-of-payment needs. IMF works to ensure global market cooperation and financial stability of the global economyy.

Concept note-4: -Some studies find that IMF loan arrangements contribute to increased poverty. Pastor (1987) and Vreeland (2002), for example, show that participation in IMF programmes worsens income distributions, especially for the poor and the labour class, which can increase rates of poverty.
