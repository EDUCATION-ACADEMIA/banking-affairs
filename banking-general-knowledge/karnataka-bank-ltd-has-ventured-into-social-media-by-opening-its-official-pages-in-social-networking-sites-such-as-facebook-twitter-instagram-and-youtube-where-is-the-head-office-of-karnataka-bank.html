(B) Thiruvananthapuram

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Mangaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commenting on the achievement, Mahabaleshwara M.S, MD & CEO, said ‘"KBL VIKAAS’ a wholistic transformation journey, launched by the Bank in the year 2017, has enabled the Bank in implementation of digital capabilities.
