For which of the following reasons RBI has decided to undertake mid-quarter policy reviews? [Allahabad Bank 2011]

1. :

To re-align its policies

2. :

To take steps and ensure smooth flow of credit

3. :

To provide guidance to the economy

(A) ** Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) Only 3

(D) All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An increase in the Reverse Repo Rate provides an incentive to the banks to park their surplus funds with the central bank on a short-term basis, thereby reducing liquidity in the banking system and overall economy.

Concept note-2: -“…the primary objective of monetary policy is to maintain price stability while keeping in mind the objective of growth.”

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is price stability. Key Points Monetary Policy: Monetary policy refers to the policy of the central bank with regard to the use of monetary instruments under its control to achieve the goals specified in the Act.
