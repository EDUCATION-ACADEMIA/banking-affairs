For Debit Card Blocking we need to send ____ message from registered mobile number.

(A) BLOCK<space>B<space> Last Four Digit of Card Number orBLOCK<space>B<space>Last Four Digit of AccountNumber

(B) BLOCK<space>B<space> Last Four Digit of Card Number orBLOCK<space>A<space>Last Four Digit of AccountNumber

(C) BLOCK<space>C<space> Last Four Digit of Card Number orBLOCK<space>B<space>Last Four Digit of AccountNumber

(D) ** BLOCK<space>C<space> Last Four Digit of Card Number or BLOCK<space>A<space>Last Four Digit of AccountNumber

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Send BLOCK XXXX, where XXXX are the final 4 digits of the debit card number, to 567676 to disable your ATM card. The cellphone number associated with the bank account must be used to send the SMS.

Concept note-2: -If you have blocked the card due to suspicious activity or due to your negligence and now would like to unblock it, you will have to place a request at the nearest bank branch. You may have to provide your identity proof along with your account number so that the bank can unblock the card.

Concept note-3: -From your registered mobile phone number, you must dial either 1800 11 2211 or 1800 425 3800. Press card block or 0 once the call has been connected.
