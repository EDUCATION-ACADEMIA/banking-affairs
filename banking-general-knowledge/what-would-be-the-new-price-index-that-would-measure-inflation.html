Concept note-2: -Over the last 12 months, the Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) increased 1.5 percent before seasonal adjustment. The rate of increase in the CPI slowed in 2010, as the December-to-December increase fell from 2.7 percent in 2009 to 1.5 percent in 2010.

Concept note-3: -Consumer Price Index or CPI is used to measure inflation in India because it is more holistic than other indices.. Q.

Concept note-4: -The inflation rate in 2010 was 1.64%.

Concept note-5: -The Consumer Price Index (CPI) is an index that is often used to measure inflation by tracking the changes over time in the prices paid by consumers for a basket of goods and services.
