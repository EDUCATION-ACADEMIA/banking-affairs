Security Paper Mill (SPM) located at

(A) Mumbai

(B) Kolkata

(C) Bengaluru

(D) ** Hoshangabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SPMCIL comprises four units of India Government Mint located in the cities of Mumbai, Kolkata, Hyderabad and Noida. These mints produce circulation coins, commemorative coins, medallions and bullion, as required by the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -Security Printing & Minting Corporation of India Limited – Department of Economic Affairs.

Concept note-3: -SPMCIL is a wholly-owned Schedule ‘A’ Company of the govt. It is engaged in the manufacture of security paper, minting of coins, the printing of currency and banknotes, non-judicial stamp papers, postage stamps, etc.

Concept note-4: -The nine production units are: four India Government Mints, two Currency Note Presses, two Security Printing Presses and one Security Paper Mill located in Mumbai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Noida, Nashik, Dewas and Narmadapuram.
