As we all know that Reserve Bank of India manages the circulation of currency. In this context which of the following decides the currency denomination value?

(A) Planning Commission

(B) Reserve Bank of India Itself

(C) ** Government of India

(D) Finance Commission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ensuring smooth distribution of banknotes and coins throughout the country and retrieval of unfit notes and uncurrent coins from circulation, ensuring the integrity of bank notes, administering the RBI (Note Refund) Rules, reviewing/rationalising the work systems/procedures at the issue offices on an ongoing basis and.

Concept note-2: -The RBI follows the minimum reserve system, wherein it has to maintain a minimum quantity of gold bullion as reserves to issue currency.
