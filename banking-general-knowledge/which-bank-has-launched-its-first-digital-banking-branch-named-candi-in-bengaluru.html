Which Bank has launched its first ‘Digital Banking Branch’ named ‘CANDI’ in Bengaluru?

(A) Union Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) ** Canara Bank

(D) Indian Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Canara Bank. Canara Bank made the inaugural event of the first digital branch named ‘CANDI’ in Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -Canara Bank launches first digital branch in Bengaluru.

Concept note-3: -Canara Bank was incorporated on July 1 1906 with the name Canara Hindu Permanent Fund Ltd. In the year 1910 the name of the Bank was changed from Canara Hindu Permanent Fund Ltd to Canara Bank Ltd.

Concept note-4: -ICICI Bank says it is the first lender in the country to introduce a fully digital and paperless procedure for opening a PPF account.

Concept note-5: -At  Rs. 15.2 tn., the Canara Bank, merged with Syndicate Bank, would become the 4th largest in India. Andhra Bank and Corporation Bank are merged with Union Bank of India.
