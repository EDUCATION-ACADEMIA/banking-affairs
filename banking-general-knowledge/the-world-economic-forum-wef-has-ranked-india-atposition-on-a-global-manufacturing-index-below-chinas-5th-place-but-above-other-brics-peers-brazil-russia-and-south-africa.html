(A) ** 30th

(B) 32nd

(C) 34th

(D) 36th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Economic Forum (WEF) has ranked India at 30th position on a global manufacturing index-below China’s 5th place but above other BRICS peers, Brazil, Russia and South Africa) Q.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 30. The rank of India in the latest World Economic global manufacturing index is 30.

Concept note-3: -In the WEF 2022 report on global gender gap, India ranked 135, falling even behind Sri Lanka, Bangladesh and Nepal.

Concept note-4: -India ranks third in the list of most-suitable locations for global manufacturing among 48 countries in terms of cost competitiveness and operating conditions.

Concept note-5: -China retained its first position in the Global Manufacturing Risk Index, 2021.
