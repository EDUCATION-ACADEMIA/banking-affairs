National Income Estimate is prepared by

(A) Department of Economics and affairs

(B) ** CSO (Central Statistical Organization)

(C) RBI

(D) Finance ministry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Income Estimates in India are prepared by Central Statistical Office (CSO). The Central Statistics Office (CSO) comes under the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is the Central Statistical Office.

Concept note-3: -In India the estimates of national income are prepared by central statistical organization (C.S.O).

Concept note-4: -The Central Statistics Office (CSO) in the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoS & PI) is responsible for the compilation of National Accounts Statistics (NAS).

Concept note-5: -Central Statistical Office (CSO) estimates the National Income of India. It is a government agency under the Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation.
