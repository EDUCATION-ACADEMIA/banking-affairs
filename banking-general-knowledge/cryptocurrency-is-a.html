Cryptocurrency is a____

(A) Plastic Money.

(B) ** Digital Medium of Exchange

(C) Digital payment

(D) Both A & B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A cryptocurrency is a medium of exchange, such as the US dollar, but is digital and uses cryptographic techniques and itâ€™s protocol to verify the transfer of funds and control the creation of monetary units.

Concept note-2: -Cryptocurrencies are digital tokens. They are a type of digital currency that allows people to make payments directly to each other through an online system. Cryptocurrencies have no legislated or intrinsic value; they are simply worth what people are willing to pay for them in the market.

Concept note-3: -Digital currency is a form of currency that just exists in the digital form but on the other hand, cryptocurrency is also a digital currency but in the form of decentralized digital currency. It requires cryptography and no central authority to manage its balances and ledgers.

Concept note-4: -Cryptocurrencies are a digital means of exchange which use cryptography as a means of security.

Concept note-5: -Cryptocurrency is an exciting technology that offers users a secure way to transfer money without the need for intermediaries. This means that users can access financial services without financial institutions and at a fraction of the cost and time.
