(A) ** RBI Governor

(B) RBI Deputy Governor

(C) Chief Financial Officer

(D) Person appointed by Union Government

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The BPSS is empowered for authorising, prescribing policies and setting standards for regulating and supervising all the payment and settlement systems in the country. The Department of Payment and Settlement Systems of the Reserve Bank of India serves as the Secretariat to the Board and executes its directions.
