What is the full form of IPO in IPO issue manager?

(A) Interactive Persuasive Offering

(B) ** Initial Public Offering

(C) Immediate Public Offering

(D) Noneof the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What Is an Initial Public Offering (IPO)? An initial public offering (IPO) refers to the process of offering shares of a private corporation to the public in a new stock issuance for the first time.

Concept note-2: -The price at which the shares are sold is called the issue price. The issue price is important because it will determine how much money the company can raise from the IPO. The company will want to set the price high enough to raise enough money for its needs but not so high that no one buys the shares.

Concept note-3: -Share. Initial public offering definition. An initial public offering (IPO) refers to the first time a company sells shares publicly. It is a form of equity financing. An initial public offering (IPO) takes place when a company offers itself up for public ownership by listing and selling its shares on a stock exchange.

Concept note-4: -IPO full form is Initial Public Offering. IPO is the process of offering shares for a corporate world in a new stock issuance. It permits the company to put up revenue from the public investors.
