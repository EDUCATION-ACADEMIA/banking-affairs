Who amongst the following Padma Bhushan awardees is a famous banker?

(A) Azim H Premji

(B) Kapila Vatsyayan

(C) ** Chanda Kochar

(D) Waheeda Rehman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chanda Kochhar was awarded with ASSOCHAM Ladies League Mumbai Women of the Decade Achievers Award on 2 January 2014. In 2011, Kochhar was awarded the third highest civilian award the Padma Bhushan and the “ABLF Woman of Power Award (India)” at the Asian Business Leadership Forum Awards.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Sudarshan Sahoo. Sudarshan Sahoo is an Indian sculpture artist from Puri in Odisha. He was awarded the Padma Vibhushan in 2021.

Concept note-3: -Padma Bhushan The award criteria include “service in any field including service rendered by Government servants” including doctors and scientists, but exclude those working with the public sector undertakings.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The corrrect answer is Shri Ghulam Nabi Azad.

Concept note-5: -The list comprises 6 Padma Vibhushan, 9 Padma Bhushan and 91 Padma Shri Awards. 19 of the awardees are women and the list also includes 2 persons from the category of Foreigners/NRI/PIO/OCI and 7 Posthumous awardees.
