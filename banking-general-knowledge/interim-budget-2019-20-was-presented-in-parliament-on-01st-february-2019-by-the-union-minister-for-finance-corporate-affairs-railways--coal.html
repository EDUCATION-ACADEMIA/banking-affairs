Interim Budget 2019-20 was presented in Parliament on 01st February 2019 by the Union Minister for Finance, Corporate Affairs, Railways & Coal

(A) Suresh Prabhu

(B) ** Piyush Goyal

(C) Arun Jaitley

(D) Giriraj Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Interim Budget 2019 was presented by Finance Minister Piyush Goyal on 1st February 2019.

Concept note-2: -2019 Budget Speech: Union finance minister Piyush Goyal presented the interim Budget for 2019-20 in the Lok Sabha on Friday.

Concept note-3: -Piyush Goyal, Minister of Finance, presented the Indian Budget 2019 (Interim Budget 2019-20) on February 1, 2019.

Concept note-4: -Expenditure: The government proposes to spend Rs 27, 86, 349 crore in 2019-20, which is 13.4% above the revised estimate of 2018-19. Receipts: The receipts (other than net borrowings) are expected to increase by 14.2% to Rs 20, 82, 589 crore, owing to higher estimated revenue from corporation tax and dividends.
