The Syndicate bank logo is “Faithful Friendly” with the symbol of which animal?

(A) Cow

(B) ** Dog

(C) Cat

(D) Hen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As far as we know, there has always been a German Shepherd Dog in the logo, and through the years, the dog has become more prominent. The canine underscores the bank’s motto: “Faithful and Friendly, ” and to draw a correlation between a dog as man’s best friend and their bank as a reliable, faithful friend.

Concept note-2: -Syndicate Bank. The symbol of dog implies that Bank is trustworthy and a friend.
