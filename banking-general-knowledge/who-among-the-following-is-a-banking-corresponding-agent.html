Who among the following is a banking corresponding agent?

(A) ** An authorized representative of bank who offer banking services in unbanked areas

(B) Any person who offer banking services in unbanked areas

(C) An authorized representative of bank who makes profits for banks

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Business Correspondent is an extended arm of the Bank Branch who is providing Financial and Banking services to the customers in unbanked and underbanked areas.

Concept note-2: -Agent banks act on behalf of other parties in financial matters, and can work in various capacities to meet the needs of the individual, business, or another bank. They offer a variety of services that can help with conducting business both domestically and internationally.

Concept note-3: -Unbanked (without brick and mortar branch of a bank)

Concept note-4: -Commercial banks provide certain services to their customers in return for some commission, these are called agency functions. Some of the agency functions of a commercial bank are listed below. Collection of cheques, bills, and drafts. Payment of interest, installments of loans, insurance premium, etc.

Concept note-5: -Since the RBI launched the banking Correspondent’s model in 2006 to expand banking services to the underserved, BCs have become essential to Indian banking’s last-mile service delivery.
