CGTMSE stands for

(A) Central Government Fund Trust Medium and Small Enterprises

(B) Credit Guarantee Fund for Medium Enterprises

(C) Central Government Transfer Fund Multispecialty Micro Enterprises

(D) ** Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) is jointly set up by Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India and Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) to catalyse flow of institutional credit to Micro & Small Enterprises (MSEs).

Concept note-2: -The Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGMSE) was launched by the Government of India to make available collateral-free credit to the micro and small enterprise sector. Both the existing and the new enterprises are eligible to be covered under the scheme.
