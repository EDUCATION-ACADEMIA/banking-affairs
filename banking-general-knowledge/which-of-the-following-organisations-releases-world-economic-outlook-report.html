Which of the following organisations releases “World Economic Outlook” Report?

(A) Asian Development Bank

(B) World Bank

(C) ** International Monetary Fund

(D) World Trade Organisation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Economic Outlook is basically a survey conducted and published by the International Monetary Fund.

Concept note-2: -A Survey by the IMF staff usually published twice a year. It presents IMF staff economists’ analyses of global economic developments during the near and medium term.

Concept note-3: -As part of its World Economic and Financial Surveys, the IMF publishes flagship reports on multilateral surveillance twice a year: World Economic Outlook (WEO), Global Financial Stability Report (GFSR), and Fiscal Monitor (FM).

Concept note-4: -The IMF projects global growth to fall from 3.4% in 2022 to 2.9% in 2023, and then rise to 3.1% in 2024. Inflation is peaking amid low growth.
