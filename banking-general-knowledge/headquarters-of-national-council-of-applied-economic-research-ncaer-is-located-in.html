Headquarters of National Council of Applied Economic Research (NCAER) is located in____

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poonam Gupta is the Director General of NCAER, and a member of the Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister. She specialises in Macroeconomics, and issues related to the Emerging Market Economies.

Concept note-2: -The National Council of Applied Economic Research (NCAER) was founded in 1956 as an independent, board-run body to give support to both the government and the private sector in empirical economic research.

Concept note-3: -NCAER’s current Governing Body is headed by Mr Nandan M Nilekani, ex-Chairman of the Unique Identification Authority of India and former CEO of Infosys Ltd. At present, Dr Poonam Gupta is the Director General of NCAER, the first woman to occupy this position and Dr Anil K Sharma is the Secretary of NCAER.

Concept note-4: -What’s the nearest metro station to National Council of Applied Economic Research (Ncaer) in Delhi? The ITO station is the nearest one to National Council of Applied Economic Research (Ncaer) in Delhi.
