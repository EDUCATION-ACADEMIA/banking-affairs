Any bank having branches more than ____ need to make internal ombudsman

(A) ** 10

(B) 20

(C) 50

(D) 100

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has asked all scheduled commercial banks having more than 10 banking outlets to appoint internal ombudsman (IO) in the bank.

Concept note-2: -The Banking Ombudsman may award compensation not exceeding ₹ 1 lakh (₹ One Hundred Thousand) to the complainant for mental agony and harassment.

Concept note-3: -There are 22 regional offices of Banking Ombudsmen in India. The latest offices are opened in Jammu, Raipur, Mumbai-II & New Delhi-III.

Concept note-4: -Desposit-taking NBFCs with 10 or more branches and non-deposit taking NBFCs with assets size of ₹5, 000 crore must appoint an Internal Ombudsman (IO). The RBI has given six months time to follow the directions.
