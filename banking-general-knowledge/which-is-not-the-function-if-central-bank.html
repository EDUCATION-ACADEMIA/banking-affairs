Which is not the function if central bank

(A) Banker to the government

(B) Controller of the money supply

(C) Bankers bank

(D) ** Overdraft facility

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Issuing of coins is not a function of the Central Bank.

Concept note-2: -The central bank only deals with commercial banks, and not directly with the public.

Concept note-3: -1 Answer. B. Banking facilities to public is not the function of the Central Bank.

Concept note-4: -Features of Bank Overdraft 1. Banks offer overdraft facilities on a predetermined limit which differs from borrower to borrower. 2. In an overdraft account the withdrawal or deposit of an amount can be done anytime upto the specified limit.
