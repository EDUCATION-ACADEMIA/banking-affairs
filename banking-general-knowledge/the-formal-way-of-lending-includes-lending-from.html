The formal way of lending includes lending from?

(A) Private Sector Banks

(B) Co-operative Banks

(C) Public Sector Banks

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In simple terms, formal lending takes place between two parties who have a business relationship and always involves a written contract. It can be an unsecured personal loan or a loan for which you have to provide collateral.

Concept note-2: -A loan is a sum of money that an individual or company borrows from a lender. It can be classified into three main categories, namely, unsecured and secured, conventional, and open-end and closed-end loans.

Concept note-3: -Lending can be broadly broken down into two categories: personal (or “consumer") lending and business lending. Some types of loans are available in both personal and business lending, though they are handled differently.
