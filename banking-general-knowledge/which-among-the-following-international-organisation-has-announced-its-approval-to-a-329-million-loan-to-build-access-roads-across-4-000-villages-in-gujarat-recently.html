Which among the following international organisation has announced its approval to a$ 329 million loan to build access roads across 4, 000 villages in Gujarat recently?

(A) OPEC

(B) IMF

(C) ** AIIB

(D) ADB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is World Bank.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Gujarat. The World Bank and Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) will provide a loan of Rs 7, 500 crore for the Gujarat government’s Mission School of Excellence project.

Concept note-3: -The Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) is a new international development bank that provides financing for infrastructure projects in Asia. It began operations in January 2016.
