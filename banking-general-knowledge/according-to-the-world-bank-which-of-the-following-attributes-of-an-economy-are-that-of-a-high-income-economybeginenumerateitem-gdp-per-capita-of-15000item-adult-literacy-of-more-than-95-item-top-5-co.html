According to the World Bank, which of the following attributes of an economy are that of a ‘high income’ economy?

1. :

GDP per capita of$ 15000.

2. :

Adult literacy of more than 95% .

3. :

Top 5 countries of the World GDP wise.

Select the correct answer using the codes given below

(A) ** Only 1

(B) Only 3

(C) 1 and 2

(D) 1 and 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of the 2023 fiscal year, high-income economies are those that had a GNI per capita of $13, 205 or more in 2021.

Concept note-2: -The High Income designation is defined as all countries with a gross national income per capita exceeding $12, 055. Overall these countries account for 39% of Global GDP and 25% of global GDP growth in the past 10 years (2012-2022).
