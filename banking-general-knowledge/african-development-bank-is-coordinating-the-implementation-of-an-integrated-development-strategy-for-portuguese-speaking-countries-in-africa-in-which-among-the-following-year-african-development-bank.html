African Development Bank is coordinating the implementation of an integrated development strategy for Portuguese-speaking countries in Africa. In which among the following year African Development Bank was founded?

(A) 1984

(B) 1977

(C) 1972

(D) ** 1963

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The AfDB was founded following an agreement signed by member states on August 14, 1963, in Khartoum, Sudan, which became effective on September 10, 1964. The AfDB comprises three entities: the African Development Bank (ADB), the African Development Fund (ADF) and the Nigeria Trust Fund (NTF).

Concept note-2: -The Board of Directors of the African Development Bank Group has approved a new strategy for addressing fragility and building resilience in Africa for the period 2022-2026. The strategy offers a roadmap for building more resilient institutions, economies, and societies across the continent over the next five years.

Concept note-3: -The overarching objective of the African Development Bank (AfDB) Group is to spur sustainable economic development and social progress in its regional member countries (RMCs), thus contributing to poverty reduction.
