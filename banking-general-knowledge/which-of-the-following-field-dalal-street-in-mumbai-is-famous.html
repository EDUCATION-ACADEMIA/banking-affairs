Which of the following field Dalal Street in Mumbai is famous?

(A) NABARD

(B) ** Stock Exchange

(C) RBI

(D) SEBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bombay Stock Exchange is the best known and most important exchange located on Dalal Street.

Concept note-2: -BSE Limited, also known as the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), is an Indian stock exchange which is located on Dalal Street in Mumbai. Established in 1875 by cotton merchant Premchand Roychand, a Jain businessman, it is the oldest stock exchange in Asia, and also the tenth oldest in the world.

Concept note-3: -BSE (formerly Bombay Stock Exchange)

Concept note-4: -Dalal Street (Hindi: dalāl path, Marathi: dalāl gallī) in Financial District of Fort in Mumbai, is the address of the Bombay Stock Exchange and several financial institutions of the world.
