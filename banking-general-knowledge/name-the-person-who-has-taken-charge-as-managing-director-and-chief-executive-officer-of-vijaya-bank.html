Name the person, who has taken charge as Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of Vijaya Bank?

(A) ** R A Sankara Narayanan

(B) Puneet Chhatwal

(C) Mr Rajiv Gauba

(D) RK Raghavan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -R. A. Sankara Narayanan, assumed charge as MD & CEO of Vijaya Bank on 1st September 2017. He has taken the reigns of the Bank from Dr Kishore Sansi, who had attained Superannuation on 31st August 2017.

Concept note-2: -The Correct Answer is Option 1 i.e Bangalore. Vijaya Bank was founded on 23rd October 1931 by late Shri A.B. Shetty and other enterprising farmers in Mangaluru, Karnataka.

Concept note-3: -South Indian Bank. Mr. Salim Gangadharan is the Chairman and Non-Executive Independent Director of the Board of our Bank.

Concept note-4: -The function was inaugurated by paying rich tributes to founding father of Vijaya Bank, Late Shri Attavar Balakrishna Shetty and the architect of modern Vijaya Bank, Shri Mulki Sunder Ram Shetty.
