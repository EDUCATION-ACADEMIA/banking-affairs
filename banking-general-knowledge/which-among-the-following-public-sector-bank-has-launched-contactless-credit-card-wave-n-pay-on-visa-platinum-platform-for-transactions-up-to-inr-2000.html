Which among the following Public sector Bank has launched Contactless Credit Card-Wave N Pay (on Visa Platinum platform), for transactions up to INR 2000?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) Union Bank of India

(C) State Bank of India

(D) ** Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Punjab National Bank launched the PNB Wave & Pay Credit Card as a credit card with contactless technology that lets you use your card without touching the card machine.

Concept note-2: -ICICI Bank has launched the country’s first contactless debit and credit cards on 7 January, 2015.

Concept note-3: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.

Concept note-4: -1995. The world’s first contactless payment card for commuters, the UPass is launched by the Seoul Bus Transport Association in South Korea.
