NABARD provides refinance to

(A) Scheduled commercial banks

(B) Co-operative banks

(C) Regional rural banks

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In case of RRBs, NABARD’s refinance is to the extent of 70% of eligible amount with sponsor bank’s share at 25% and RRB’s share at 5%.

Concept note-2: -Agri-clinic central schemes. Agribusiness central schemes. Capital investment subsidy scheme for commercial production units for biological inputs and organic inputs.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Facilitating credit flow for the promotion and development of agriculture. The full form of NABARD is National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development.
