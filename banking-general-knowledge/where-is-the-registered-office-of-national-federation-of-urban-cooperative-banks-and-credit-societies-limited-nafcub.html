Where is the registered office of National Federation of Urban Cooperative Banks and Credit Societies Limited (NAFCUB)?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Shimla

(C) Kolkata

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Address : B-14, 3rd Floor A-Block Shopping Complex, Naraina Vihar Ring Road New Delhi-110028(India).

Concept note-2: -The first urban cooperative credit society was registered in Canjeevaram (Kanjivaram) in the erstwhile Madras province in October, 1904.

Concept note-3: -The Registered Office of NAFCUB is at Delhi and the area of operation is whole of the country. We represent around 1600 Urban Cooperative Banks and more than 50, 000 Cooperative Credit Societies functioning all over India. Some of the personalities to mention are Former President of India Her Excellency Smt.

Concept note-4: -The Urban Banks Department of the Reserve Bank of India is vested with the responsibility of regulating and supervising primary (urban) cooperative banks, which are popularly known as Urban Cooperative Banks (UCBs).
