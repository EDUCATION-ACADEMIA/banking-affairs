Under the APY, guaranteed minimum pension of Rs. 1, 000/-or 2, 000/-or 3, 000/-or 4, 000 or 5, 000/-per month will be given at the age of 60 years depending on the contributions by the subscribers.

Concept note-3: - The Atal Pension Yojana (APY) was launched on 09.05.2015 to create a universal social security system for all Indians, especially the poor, the under-privileged and the workers in the unorganised sector. APY is administered by Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA).
