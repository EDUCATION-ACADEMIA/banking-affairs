Which of the following is not a prerequisite for a promissory note?

(A) ** Drawn on a specified banker

(B) It should be unconditional

(C) It should be in writing

(D) It should be made and signed by the debtor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acceptance is not an essential requirement of a valid promissory note.

Concept note-2: -A promissory note cannot be made payable to the bearer, no matter whether it is payable on demand or after a certain time.

Concept note-3: -These include the drawee, drawer and payee. Drawer: A drawer is a person who agrees to pay the drawee a certain amount of money on the maturity of the promissory note. He/she is also known as maker. Drawee: She/He is an individual, in whose favour the note is prepared.
