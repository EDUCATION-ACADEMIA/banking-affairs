The Banking Ombudsman Scheme was introduced under which of the following acts?

(A) Banking Regulation Act, 1935

(B) ** Banking Regulation Act, 1949

(C) Banking Regulation Act, 1985

(D) None of The Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Banking Ombudsman Scheme is introduced under Section 35 A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 by RBI with effect from 1995.

Concept note-2: -The Banking Ombudsman Scheme was first introduced in India in 1995, and was revised in 2002. The current scheme became operative from 1 January 2006, and replaced and superseded the banking Ombudsman Scheme 2002.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank had first introduced the Banking Ombudsman Scheme in 1995 to provide expeditious and inexpensive forum to bank customers for resolution of their complaints relating to deficiency in banking services.

Concept note-4: -The Banking Ombudsman Scheme was implemented by the RBI to redress the complaints of customers on certain types of banking services provided by banks and to facilitate the settlement of those complaints. The scheme was introduced under the Banking Regulation Act of 1949 by RBI with effect from 1995.
