term deposit scheme has flexibility of period from ____ days to ____ moths

(A) ** 7-120

(B) 180-120

(C) 180-84

(D) 1-120

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Domestic & NRO Rupee Term Deposits can be opened for minimum period of 7 days and maximum up to 10 Years. For NRE Term Deposits, minimum period is 1 Year and maximum up to 10 Years.

Concept note-2: -The tenure for a Flexi Deposit account ranges from a minimum of 15 days to 91 days.

Concept note-3: -Penalty for default in payment of minimum deposit will be Rs. 50/-per financial year. What is the minimum and maximum period of deposit ? The minimum period of deposit is 5 years and maximum is 7 years.

Concept note-4: -A short-term FD has a maturity tenure of a minimum of 7 days to a maximum of 12 months. The short-term FD interest rates would vary from bank to bank and could also be revised at any time. Investors can also withdraw their deposit prematurely.
