What is the financial limit Under MUDRA’s Tarun scheme?

(A) Rs. 40 lakh to Rs. 50 lakh

(B) Rs. 30 lakh to Rs. 40 lakh

(C) Rs. 20 lakh to Rs. 30 lakh

(D) ** Rs. 5 lakh to Rs. 10 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Above-Rs 50, 000 & Maximum up to Rs.10 lakhs can be sanctioned to meet need-based requirement. In case of Working Capital, assessment will be done by Turnover method.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) is a scheme launched by the Hon’ble Prime Minister on April 8, 2015 for providing loans upto 10 lakh to the non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises.

Concept note-3: -Given as a part of the Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, the Mudra Tarun loan can help you secure funding of anywhere between ₹5 to ₹10 lakhs. You can use this money to set up a new business, expand your operations or even meet heavy working capital requirements.
