Which of the following is not a fee-based financial service?

(A) Corporate counseling

(B) Profit management

(C) ** Lease financing

(D) Issue management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following is not a fee‐based financial service? Notes: Lease financing is one of the important sources of medium-and long-term financing where the owner of an asset gives another person, the right to use that asset against periodical payments. It is not a fee‐based financial service.

Concept note-2: -Capital issue management is a fee based service.

Concept note-3: -A lease is an agreement under which a company or a firm acquires a right to make use of a capital asset like machinery, on payment of a prescribed fee called „rental charges‟. Long-term.

Concept note-4: -Therefore, Issuance of Letters of Credit is not the fund based business of commercial banks.
