____in partnership with Visa launched India’s first co-branded e-commerce credit card

(A) Flipkart and ICICI Bank

(B) Snapdeal and ICICI Bank

(C) Flipkart and HDFC Bank

(D) ** Snapdeal and HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.

Concept note-2: -Other co-branded cards include Maruti Suzuki NEXA HDFC Bank AllMiles (car-related privileges), Platinum Times Card (discounts on entertainment), and Snapdeal HDFC Bank Card (online benefits).

Concept note-3: -IndiGo HDFC Co-Branded Credit Cards Review. IndiGo has recently launched co-branded travel credit cards with HDFC Bank labeled as Ka-ching, these cards are offered in two variants-Rewards (Basic) and Rewards XL (Premium).

Concept note-4: -Punjab & Sind Bank and SBI Card launch co-brand credit cards Public sector bank Punjab and Sind Bank (PSB) has partnered with SBI Card to launch co-brand credit cards for the bank’s customers. PSB has also entered the credit card market as a new product segment in its portfolio as a result of this collaboration.
