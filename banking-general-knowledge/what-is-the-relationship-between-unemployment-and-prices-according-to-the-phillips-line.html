What is the relationship between unemployment and prices according to the Phillips line?

(A) Straight

(B) ** Adverse

(C) Stable

(D) All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Phillips curve states that inflation and unemployment have an inverse relationship. Higher inflation is associated with lower unemployment and vice versa.

Concept note-2: -The thinking behind the Phillips curve goes … Lower unemployment is associated with higher inflation. Higher unemployment is associated with lower inflation.

Concept note-3: -The short-run Phillips curve indicates an inverse relationship between inflation and unemployment. According to the short-run Phillips curve, as unemployment goes up, inflation goes down, and as inflation goes up, unemployment goes down.
