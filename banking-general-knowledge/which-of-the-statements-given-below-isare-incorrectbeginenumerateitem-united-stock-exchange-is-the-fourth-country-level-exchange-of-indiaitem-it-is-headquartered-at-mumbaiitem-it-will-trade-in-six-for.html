Which of the statements given below is/are incorrect?

1. :

United Stock Exchange is the fourth country level exchange of India.

2. :

It is headquartered at Mumbai.

3. :

It will trade in six foreign currency derivatives.

(A) Only 1 and 2

(B) Only 2 and 3

(C) Only 1 and 3

(D) ** Only 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currency Derivatives segment of NSE provides trading in derivative instruments like Currency Futures on 4 currency pairs, Cross Currency Futures & Options on 3 currency pairs and Interest Rate Futures on 10 Y GS and 91D T-Bill.

Concept note-2: -Currency Derivatives are exchange-traded contracts deriving their value from their underlying asset, i.e., the currency. The investor buys or sells specific units of fixed currency on a pre-specified date and rate.
