[SOURCES]
Sarswati Samman for the year 2017 is given by kk birla foundation to Sitanshu Yashchandra he is a poet in ____ language

(A) ** Gujrati

(B) Marathi

(C) Maithili

(D) Punjabi

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By India Today Web Desk: The 27th Saraswati Samman was conferred upon eminent Gujarati poet Sitanshu Yashaschandra on Tuesday. Organised by the KK Birla Foundation, the award for 2017 was given to Yashaschandra for his poetry collection, ‘Vakhar, ’ published in 2009.

Concept note-2: -The Correct Answer is Harivansh Rai Bachchan. Harivansh Rai Bachchan was the first winner of the Saraswati Samman for his four-volume autobiography, Kya Bhooloon Kya Yaad Karoon, Basere Se Door, Needa Ka Nirman Phir and Dashdwar se Sopan Tak.

Concept note-3: -So far, 31 awardees have been honoured with Saraswati Samman for their work in different languages. Recently, Hindi writer “Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra” was honoured with saraswati samman for 2021 for his collection of poems ‘In Toh Here Hoon’ on April 7, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Saraswati Samman is the most prestigious award of the KK Birla Foundation. 2. The Samman is given annually to an outstanding literary work published in Hindi only during the last 10 years.
