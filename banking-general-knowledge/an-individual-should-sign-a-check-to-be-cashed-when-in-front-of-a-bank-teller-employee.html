BANKING GENERAL KNOWLEDGE

[SOURCES]
An individual should sign a check to be cashed when in front of a bank teller (employee).

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. A check must be endorsed on the back for it to be valid for deposit. So, always sign your name in the blank space next to the X just before you bring it to the Bank. Note: You can deposit at a Bank location, through our mobile app, or at an ATM.

Concept note-2: -Who Endorses a Check? The person receiving the check is the one that needs to endorse the check. For instance, if the front of the check on the “Pay to the Order of” line says, “Jane Doe” then Jane Doe is the one who should be endorsing the check.

Concept note-3: -Sign your name on the line at the bottom right-hand corner using the signature you used when you opened the checking account. This shows the bank that you agree that you are paying the stated amount and to the correct payee.
