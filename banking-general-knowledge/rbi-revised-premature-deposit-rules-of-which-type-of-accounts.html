RBI revised premature deposit rules of which type of accounts?

(A) Kiddy accounts

(B) Current accounts

(C) ** Large value fixed deposit accounts

(D) Dollar savings accounts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes Bank penalty on FD premature withdrawal A 0.50 percent penalty will be imposed if the FD was closed in fewer than 181 days. If your tenure is 182 days or more, you will face a 0.75 percent penalty.

Concept note-2: -The new FD rule is related to interest being paid on unclaimed or overdue FDs. As per the new rule, if a term deposit matures and proceeds are unpaid, the unclaimed amount will now earn a lesser interest rate.

Concept note-3: -A term deposit is a fixed-term investment that includes the deposit of money into an account at a financial institution.
