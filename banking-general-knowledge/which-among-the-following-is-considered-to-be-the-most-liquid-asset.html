Which among the following is considered to be the most liquid asset?

(A) Gold

(B) ** Money

(C) Land

(D) Treasury bonds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cash in hand is considered the most liquid asset in accounting.

Concept note-2: -Cash is the most liquid asset possible as it is already in the form of money.

Concept note-3: -Cash is universally considered the most liquid asset because it can most quickly and easily be converted into other assets.

Concept note-4: -Money is the most liquid of all assets. A liquid asset is cash on hand or an asset that can be easily converted to cash.
