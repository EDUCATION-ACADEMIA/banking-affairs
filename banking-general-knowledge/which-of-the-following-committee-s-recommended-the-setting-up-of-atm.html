Which of the following committee s recommended the setting up of ATM?

(A) ** Rangarajan committee

(B) Shere committee

(C) Saraf committee

(D) Narasimhan committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -C Rangarajan Committee was recommended the establishment of ATMs.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Reserve Bank of India (RBI) had set up a task force under Deputy Governor SS Mundra to oversee the recalibration of ATM machines after the government introduced new Rs 500 and Rs 2, 000 notes.

Concept note-3: -Rangarajan Report on Poverty. The Expert Group under the Chairmanship of Dr. C. Rangarajan to Review the Methodology for Measurement of Poverty in the country constituted by the Planning Commission in June 2012 has submitted its report on 30th June 2014.

Concept note-4: -The Rangarajan Committee: It was constituted by the government in 1993 for making recommendations in context with the disinvestment.

Concept note-5: -ATM full form is Automated Teller Machine which is a self-service banking outlet. You can withdraw money, check your balance, or even transfer funds.
