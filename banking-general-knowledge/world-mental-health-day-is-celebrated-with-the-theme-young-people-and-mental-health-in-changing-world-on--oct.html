World Mental Health Day is celebrated with the theme ‘young people and mental health in changing world on ____ oct

(A) 8

(B) 9

(C) ** 10

(D) 11

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 World Mental Health Day theme ‘Make Mental Health & Well-Being for All a Global Priority’ gives an opportunity to rejuvenate the efforts in making the world a better place.

Concept note-2: -World Mental Health Day is observed on 10 October every year, with the overall objective of raising awareness of mental health issues and mobilizing efforts in support of better mental health.

Concept note-3: -On the theme of ‘anxiety’, this year’s Mental Health Awareness Week is from 15 to 21 May. Anxiety is a normal emotion in us all, but sometimes it can get out of control and become a mental health problem.

Concept note-4: -Annually, World Mental Health Day on October 10th raises awareness and mobilizes efforts to support mental health.
