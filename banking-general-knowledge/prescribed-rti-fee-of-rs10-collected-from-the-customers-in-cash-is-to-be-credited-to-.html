prescribed RTI fee of RS.10/-collected from the customers in cash, is to be credited to ____

(A) exchange accounts

(B) commission acount

(C) ** P and T charges recovered account

(D) interest account

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The payment of fee of Rs. 10/-can also be made by way of cash to the public authority against a proper receipt. 2. If the applicant belongs to below poverty line (BPL) category, he is not required to pay any fee.

Concept note-2: -RTI FEE IS TO BE PAID IN FAVOUR OF ACCOUNT OFFICER OF CONCERNED OFFICE BY CASH/BANKERS CHEQUE/ DEMAND DRAFT/ INDIAN POSTAL ORDER. information.

Concept note-3: -In normal course, information to an applicant shall be supplied within 30 days from the receipt of application by the public authority. If information sought concerns the life or liberty of a person, it shall be supplied within 48 hours.
