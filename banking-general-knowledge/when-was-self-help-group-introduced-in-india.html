When was Self Help Group introduced in India?

(A) 1996

(B) 2000

(C) 1980

(D) ** 1992

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1991-92 NABARD started promoting self-help groups on a large scale. And it was the real take-off point for the ‘SHG movement’. In 1993, the Reserve Bank of India also allowed SHGs to open saving accounts in banks.

Concept note-2: -The origin of SHGs in India can be traced back to the establishment of the Self-Employed Women’s Association (SEWA) in 1972.

Concept note-3: -The Genesis of SHG in India can be traced to formation of Self-Employed Women’s Association (SEWA) in 1970.
