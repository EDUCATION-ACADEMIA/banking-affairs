How many digits are there in a Aadhaar Virtual ID?

(A) ** 16 digits

(B) 12 digits

(C) 14 digits

(D) 11 digits

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VID is a temporary, revocable 16-digit random number mapped with the Aadhaar number. VID can be used in lieu of Aadhaar number whenever authentication or e-KYC services are performed.

Concept note-2: -Aadhaar Virtual ID, a 16-digit number that is randomly generated, is used in place of Aadhaar number for authentification purposes. It is valid until a new VID is generated by the user. It is generated at the UIDAI portal. It provides limited KYC access to agencies.

Concept note-3: -The virtual ID is a temporary 16-digit code that will replace Aadhaar for authentication.

Concept note-4: -Aadhaar is a 12 digit individual identification number which serves as proof of identity and proof of address for residents of India.

Concept note-5: -Step 1: Visit online portal of UIDAI. Step 2: under ‘My Aadhaar’ select ‘Download Aadhaar’. Step 3: Select VID option and enter your VID. Step 4: Enter the captcha and click on ‘Send OTP’ .
