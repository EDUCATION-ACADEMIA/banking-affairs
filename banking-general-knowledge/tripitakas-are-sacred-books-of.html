Tripitakas are sacred books of

(A) ** Buddhists

(B) Hindus

(C) Jains

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tripitakas are Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka and Abhidhamma Pitaka . These are contained teachings of Gautama Buddha.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Tripitaka. Tripiṭaka is the traditional term for the Buddhist scriptures.

Concept note-3: -Buddhists have a sacred text called the tripitaka. The Hindus have texts named the Vedas and the Upanishads. The Vedas are the oldest sacred books of Hinduism.

Concept note-4: -The Tripitakas or the three-fold basket have three dividions namely Vinaya Pitaka, Sutta Pitaka and Abhidhamma Pitaka. These are the earliest written records of the principles of Buddhism.
