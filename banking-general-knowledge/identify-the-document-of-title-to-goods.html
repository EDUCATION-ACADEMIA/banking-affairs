(A) Demand Draft

(B) ** Bill of lading

(C) Cheque

(D) Pay order

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The bill of lading is a legally binding document that provides the carrier and the shipper with all of the necessary details to accurately process a shipment.1 It has three main functions: It is a document of title to the goods described in the bill of lading. It is a receipt for the shipped products.

Concept note-2: -Railway receipts are document of title of goods.
