CAN NRI apply for HPCL CARD

(A) YES

(B) ** NO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eligibility Criteria The salaried applicant needs to have an income of ₹30, 000 per month and self-employed needs to have an annual income of ₹6, 00, 000 or above.

Concept note-2: -To apply for the card, the NRI will have to provide below documents: Valid work visa or resident card. Overseas Address proof. Valid India or overseas passport.

Concept note-3: -Fees and charges for ICICI Bank HPCL Coral VISA Credit Card For the 1st year, there is no annual fee. From the 2nd year onwards, there is an annual fee of Rs. 199, which is waived if Rs 50, 000 or more was spent on the card, the previous year. There is no fee for a Supplementary Card.

Concept note-4: -With a nominal joining fee of Rs. 199, this card offers decent benefits on fuel as well as other categories, such as movie tickets and dining. You can avail this credit card for free by spending Rs. 50, 000 in a year which is feasible if you are a loyal customer of HPCL.
