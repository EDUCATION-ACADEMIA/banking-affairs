(A) Rs.90000 crore

(B) Rs.80000 crore

(C) Rs.50000 crore

(D) ** Rs.10000 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. Current NAV: The Current Net Asset Value of the BHARAT 22 ETF as of Feb 14, 2023 is Rs 58.14 for Growth option of its Regular plan. 2. Returns: Its trailing returns over different time periods are: 10.35% (1yr), 15.63% (3yr) and 7.05% (since launch).

Concept note-2: -Taxation for this ETF will be like that of equity shares or equity mutual funds. AS per the prevalent tax laws, capital gains arising from investments held up to one year will be classified as STCG and will be taxed at 15%, plus surcharge and cess as applicable. LTCG will be tax exempt.
