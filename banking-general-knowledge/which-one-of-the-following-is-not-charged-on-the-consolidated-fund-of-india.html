Which one of the following is not charged on the Consolidated Fund of India?

(A) Salary & allowances of the president of India

(B) Salary & allowances of the speaker of the Lok Sabha

(C) Salary & allowances of the Justices of the Supreme Court of India

(D) ** Salary & allowances of the Vice President of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The salary of judges of the High Court is charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State and not on the Consolidated Fund of India.

Concept note-2: -Emoluments and allowances of the President and other expenditure relating to his office. Salaries and allowances of the Chairman and the Deputy Chairman of the Rajya Sabha and the Speaker and the Deputy Speaker of the Lok Sabha. Salaries, allowances and pensions of the judges of the Supreme Court.

Concept note-3: -The vice president receives a salary in the capacity of the ex officio chairman of the Rajya Sabha, which is currently ₹400, 000 (US$5, 000) per month (revised from ₹125, 000 in 2018).
