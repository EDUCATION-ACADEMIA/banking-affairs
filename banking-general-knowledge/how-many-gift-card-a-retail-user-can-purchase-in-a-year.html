How many gift card a retail user can purchase in a year?

(A) 5

(B) ** 10

(C) 15

(D) 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Based on the U.S. government legal regulations concerning limitations on gift card purchasing, within a 24 hour period: The maximum amount that can be purchased by a single person across multiple gift cards is $10, 000. The maximum amount that can be purchased by a single person for a single brand is $2, 000.

Concept note-2: -Amazon.com Gift Cards are available in $15, $25, $50, and $100 denominations at participating grocery, drug, and convenience stores throughout the U.S. At select stores, you can also choose a variable denomination card, which can be loaded with any amount between $25 and $500.

Concept note-3: -At any given time, 10% to 19% of gift card balances remain unredeemed-and around 6% of gift cards are never even used.
