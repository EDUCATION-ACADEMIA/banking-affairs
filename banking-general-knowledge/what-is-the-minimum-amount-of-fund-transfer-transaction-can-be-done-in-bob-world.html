What is the minimum amount of fund transfer transaction can be done in bob World?

(A) Rs.10

(B) Rs.15

(C) ** Rs.5

(D) Rs.100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-2: -As per NPCI circular, the maximum amount that can transferred is Rs 2 Lakhs per transaction.

Concept note-3: -Open the bob World mobile banking app and go to the Menu. Under the Menu, click on ‘Fund Transfer’ option and select ‘other banks’. Click on ‘Beneficiary Registration’ button and select ‘Online Fund transfer (IMPS)’.
