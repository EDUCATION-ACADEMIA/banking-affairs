The 3 main types of banks in the United States are:

(A) Commercial banks

(B) Savings and loan associations

(C) Credit unions

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How many types of banking sectors are there? Commercial banks are of three types i.e., Public sector banks, Private sector banks and Foreign banks.

Concept note-2: -There are three major types of depository institutions in the United States. They are commercial banks, thrifts (which include savings and loan associations and savings banks) and credit unions.

Concept note-3: -The 4 different types of banks are Central Bank, Commercial Bank, Cooperative Banks, Regional Rural Banks.
