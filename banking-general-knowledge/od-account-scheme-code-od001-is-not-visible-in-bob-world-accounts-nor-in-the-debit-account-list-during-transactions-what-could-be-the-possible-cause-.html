OD account (scheme code OD001) is not visible in BOB WORLD Accounts nor in the Debit Account list during transactions. What could be the possible cause-

(A) This is a new account and customer has not Refreshed his account list

(B) The mode of operation field is blank in CBS

(C) ** OD001 scheme code is not eligible for Mobile Banking

(D) Both (B) and (C)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Retail customers having our bank’s active debit card, can register themselves for bob World Internet from his office/home without visiting branches. A link ‘Online Registration using Debit Card’ is available on the login page of bob World Internet. Login to the website using the User ID and password.

Concept note-2: -You can also access the same under Login > Services > Mobile Banking (bob World) > Registration. Select your customer ID, confirm that the mobile number shown is your registered mobile number, and click on Continue. Confirm Details, then enter remarks and Transaction password. Click on Submit button.

Concept note-3: -For e.g.: https://intl.bobibanking.com or add this website in your favorites. This will avoid the need for typing the URL every time, you log in and subsequent risk of scouting. Please ensure that https is used while logging in. ‘s’ indicates that this is secured site.

Concept note-4: -Open the bob World banking mobile application and click on the ‘Open a digital savings account’ option. You would then be offered different bank account types to choose from. You can click on the ‘Know more’ button under each account type and understand the benefits of each offering in detail.
