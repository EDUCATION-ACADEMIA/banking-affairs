What is the time period for a Short term corporate loan?

(A) 1 Year

(B) 18 Months

(C) ** Both A and B

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Financial institutions usually give short-term loans, generally for a period of 1-2 years.

Concept note-2: -Bank debt, other than revolving credit facilities, generally takes two forms: Term Loan A – This layer of debt is typically amortized evenly over 5 to 7 years. Term Loan B – This layer of debt usually involves nominal amortization (repayment) over 5 to 8 years, with a large bullet payment in the last year.

Concept note-3: -Short-term financing means taking out a loan to make a purchase, usually with a loan term of less than one year.

Concept note-4: -A short term borrowing means borrowing of tenor up to but less than one year.
