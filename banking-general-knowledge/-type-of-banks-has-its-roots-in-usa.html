____ type of banks has its roots in U.S.A

(A) Branch banks

(B) Chain banks

(C) ** Unit banks

(D) Group banks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are three major types of depository institutions in the United States. They are commercial banks, thrifts (which include savings and loan associations and savings banks) and credit unions.

Concept note-2: -The 4 different types of banks are Central Bank, Commercial Bank, Cooperative Banks, Regional Rural Banks.

Concept note-3: -Some of the more notable universal banks include Deutsche Bank, HSBC, and ING Bank; within the United States, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, and JPMorgan Chase qualify as universal banks.

Concept note-4: -The beginnings of the banking industry can be traced to 1780 when the Bank of Pennsylvania was founded to fund the American Revolutionary War. After merchants in the Thirteen Colonies needed a currency as a medium of exchange, the Bank of North America was opened to facilitate more advanced financial transactions.
