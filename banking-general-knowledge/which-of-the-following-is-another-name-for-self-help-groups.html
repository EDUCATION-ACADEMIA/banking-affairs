Which of the following is another name for Self help groups?

(A) mutual aid

(B) support groups

(C) groups of people

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Self-help groups-also called mutual help or mutual aid groups-are composed of peers who share a similar mental, emotional, or physical problem, or who are interested in a focal issue, such as education or parenting.

Concept note-2: -Other terms that may be used for “self-help groups” include: local disability groups, parents groups, social groups, peer groups, clubs or mutual aid groups.

Concept note-3: -Self-help Groups (SHGs) are informal associations of people who come together to find ways to improve their living conditions. They are generally self-governed and peer-controlled.
