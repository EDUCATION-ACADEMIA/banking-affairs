Which of the following is NOT a Government of India undertaking? [Allahabad Bank 2010]

(A) Oil India Ltd.

(B) Sutlej Jal Vidyut Nigam Ltd.

(C) ** ICICI Bank

(D) National Thermal Power Corporation Ltd.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI bank is a leading private sector bank in India. It stands for Industrial Credit and Investment Corporation of India (ICICI).

Concept note-2: -Political parties are non-statutory bodies in a democracy and thus cannot be included as an organ of the government.

Concept note-3: -At present, there are 12 public sector banks in India. While the bank’s shareholders hold the majority of stakes in private sector banks, the majority in public sector banks are held by the government. At present, there are 21 private sector banks in India.

Concept note-4: -Union Bank of India is one of the leading public sector banks of the country. The Bank is a listed entity and the Government of India holds 83.49 percent in Bank’s total share capital.
