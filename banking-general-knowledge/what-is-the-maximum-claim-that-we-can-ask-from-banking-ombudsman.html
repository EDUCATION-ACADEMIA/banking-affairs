What is the maximum claim that we can ask from Banking Ombudsman

(A) 10lakh

(B) ** 20lakh

(C) 25lakh

(D) 26lakh

(E) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Banking Ombudsman may award compensation not exceeding ₹ 1 lakh (₹ One Hundred Thousand) to the complainant for mental agony and harassment.

Concept note-2: -An Ombudsman can entertain a complaint for a maximum amount of Rs 20 lakhs.

Concept note-3: -Compensation not exceeding rupees hundred thousand can also be awarded by the Banking Ombudsman to the complainant for loss of time, expenses incurred as also, harassment and mental anguish suffered by the complainant.

Concept note-4: -The pecuniary jurisdiction of the Banking Ombudsman to pass an Award has been increased from existing rupees one million to rupees two million.
