Personal Loans are also called as by which of the following names?

(A) Signature Loans

(B) Unsecured Loans

(C) Consumer Loans

(D) ** All of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Personal loans are Unsecured loans because these loans are given without any security or collateral. These are advanced given to Borrowers on the basis of the borrower’s credit history and ability to repay the loan from personal income so it’s also called a Signature Loan.

Concept note-2: -A personal loan allows you to borrow money to pay for personal expenses and then repay those funds over time. Personal loans are a type of installment debt that allows you to obtain a lump sum of funding. For example, you might use a personal loan to cover: Moving expenses.

Concept note-3: -An Unsecured Loan is a loan provided solely based on the creditworthiness of the borrower without pledging any collateral as security in the event of default or non-payment of dues. Unsecured loans are also referred to as personal loans and generally provided to borrowers with high credit ratings.

Concept note-4: -A personal loan is money you borrow from a bank or other financial institution with a set repayment period and consistent monthly payments. Most personal loans are unsecured, so you won’t have to put down collateral to borrow the money.
