Functional of financial services exclude: ____

(A) Mobilisation of savings

(B) ** Collection Tax

(C) Allocation of funds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Asset management and all other forms of fund management’ are liable to service tax under ‘banking and other financial service’ [section 65(12)].

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer Functions of financial services exclude Specialised services. The function of financial services is to use the funds of the company in such a way that the maximum possible utility is achieved.

Concept note-3: -The financial services sector is comprised of banking, mortgages, credit cards, payment services, tax preparation and planning, accounting, and investing.

Concept note-4: -Reducing unemployment and helping speculators to bet on price movements are not functions of a financial system.
