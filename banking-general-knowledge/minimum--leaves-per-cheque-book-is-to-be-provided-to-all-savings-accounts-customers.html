minimum ____ leaves per cheque book is to be provided to all savings accounts customers

(A) 200

(B) 75

(C) 100

(D) ** 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dear Customer, We hereby inform you that w.e.f. Jun 1, 2011, standard Current Account customers placing a request for a cheque book will receive only 25 cheque leaves per cheque book.

Concept note-2: -Did you know? First, ten cheque leaves will be free of cost for cheque book services in a financial year. After that, ten leaf cheque books will levy ₹40 plus GST; 25 leaf cheque books at ₹75 plus GST and emergency cheque books will attract a charge of ₹50 plus GST for ten leaves or part thereof.

Concept note-3: -Most banks in India have set a limit of INR 1 lakh on Cash withdrawal limit from bank per day by cheque. This limit typically applies to self-use or self-addressed cheques.

Concept note-4: -The lender provides First 10 cheque leaves free in a financial year. Thereafter, it charges Rs 40 plus GST for 10 Leaf Cheque Book.
