As we all know, Govt of India collects tax revenue on various activities in the country. Which of the following is a part of the tax revenue of the Govt?

1. :

Tax on Income

2. :

Tax on Expenditure

3. :

Tax on Property or Capital Asset

4. :

Tax on Goods and Services

(A) Both 1 and 2 only

(B) Both 1 and 4 only

(C) ** All the above

(D) Only 2, 3 and 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tax on income, tax on expenditure, tax on property or capital assets and the tax on goods and services-all form a part of tax revenue of the Government. This means that revenue is collected by the government from ll these items.

Concept note-2: -The 5 major sources of revenue for the Government are Goods and Services Tax (GST), Income tax, corporation tax, non-tax revenues, union excise duties .
