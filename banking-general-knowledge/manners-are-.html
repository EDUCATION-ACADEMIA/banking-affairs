Manners are ____

(A) dissatisfied with a product or service and about general experience

(B) oversimplified image or idea

(C) information provided by clients

(D) ** a person’s outward bearing or way of behaving toward others.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -3. Manner, air, bearing all refer to one’s outward aspect or behavior. Manner applies to a distinctive mode of behavior, or social attitude toward others, etc.: a gracious manner. Air applies to outward appearance insofar as this is distinctive or indicative: an air of martyrdom.

Concept note-2: -/ˈmæn·ərz/ ways of behaving toward people, esp. ways that are socially correct and show respect for their comfort and their feelings: He had the bad manners to keep interrupting whoever was speaking.

Concept note-3: -Polite means showing regards for others in manners, speech, and behavior.
