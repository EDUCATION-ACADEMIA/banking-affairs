What is the fund transfer limit for doing Mobile/ DTH recharge per month in bob World?

(A) ** 250000

(B) 500000

(C) 1000000

(D) 100000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-2: -The IMPS limit depends on the respective bank’s policy and can range anywhere between ₹10, 000 and ₹2 lakh.

Concept note-3: -IMPS service is used to transfer funds within INDIA with denominations of INR only. As per NPCI circular, the maximum amount that can transferred is Rs 2 Lakhs per transaction.
