In January 2019, LIC has completed the process of picking up a controlling____stake in the nearly crippled IDBI Bank.

(A) ** 51%

(B) 49%

(C) 75%

(D) 25%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The insurance behemoth, Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) has completed the acquisition of 51 per cent controlling stake in IDBI Bank, marking the entry of more than 60 years old state-owned insurer into the banking space.

Concept note-2: -The government and LIC together hold 94.72 per cent stake in IDBI Bank, which will come down to 34 per cent after the strategic sale.

Concept note-3: -The government and LIC hold 94.72 per cent stake together in IDBI Bank.

Concept note-4: -The government, with a more than 45% stake in IDBI Bank, is currently classified as a co-promoter of the lender. The government is looking to sell its 30.48% stake in IDBI Bank, while state-backed LIC will offload its 30.24% share in the lender.
