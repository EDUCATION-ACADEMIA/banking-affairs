Which of the following aspects are outlined by the loan policy of a bank?

(A) Rating standards

(B) Lending procedures

(C) Financial covenants

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elements of loan structure include loan-to-value (LTV), interest rate, amortization period, and collateral security requirements.

Concept note-2: -The bank will check your repayment capacity before everything else. While applying for a loan, the borrower has to give a letter to the bank, authorising them to run your credit history. Banks will evaluate your repayment history with others and the amount of debt you have currently.
