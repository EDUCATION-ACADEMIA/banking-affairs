What is the minimum amount of money that can be transferred through RTGS?

(A) ** Rs 2 lakh

(B) Rs 2.5 lakh

(C) Rs 3 lakh

(D) Rs 5 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The RTGS system is primarily meant for large value transactions. The minimum amount to be remitted through RTGS is ₹ 2, 00, 000/-with no upper or maximum ceiling. 7.

Concept note-2: -While NEFT has no minimum or maximum limit on the amount to be transferred, RTGS transactions can only be performed if the amount to be transferred is equal to or more than Rs. 2 lakh.

Concept note-3: -2 lakhs is done through IMPS. In such cases reversal could take a maximum of 3 days. 5.
