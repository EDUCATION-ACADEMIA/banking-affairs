The Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Bill, 2016 was recently given legal backing by Supreme Court. This act was passed in LokSabha as a____:

(A) Ordinary Bill

(B) ** Money Bill

(C) Constitution Amendment Bill

(D) Financial Bill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016. Under the Aadhaar Act 2016, Indian nationals can enroll to secure an Aadhar number by providing their demographics and biometrics. The Issuing Authority cannot deactivate or omit the Aadhaar number after its issue.

Concept note-2: -Aadhaar. BENGALURU: Section 33 of Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016 does provide for disclosure of information/identity in certain cases, the high court observed recently.
