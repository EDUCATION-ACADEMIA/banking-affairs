AU Small Finance Bank has received Reserved Bank of India (RBI) approval to operate as Scheduled Commercial Bank recently. Where is headquarters of AU Small Finance Bank?

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Patna

(C) Gandhinagar

(D) ** Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Au Small Finance Bank Limited has commenced operations as a small finance bank with effect from April 19, 2017. The Reserve Bank has issued a licence to the bank under Section 22 (1) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 to carry on the business of small finance bank in India.

Concept note-2: -About AU Small Finance Bank Today, we are recognised as one of the best Scheduled Commercial Banks in India. We are also listed as a Fortune India 500 company for providing a convenient banking experience to all our customers.

Concept note-3: -Headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan, the retail-focused Non-Banking Finance Company for two decades provided speedy and customised financial solutions to rural and urban population.

Concept note-4: -Au Financiers was founded by entrepreneur Sanjay Agarwal in 1996. It started with one branch in Jaipur but today it has around 200 branches across the country and focuses on lending to the low-income section of the population.
