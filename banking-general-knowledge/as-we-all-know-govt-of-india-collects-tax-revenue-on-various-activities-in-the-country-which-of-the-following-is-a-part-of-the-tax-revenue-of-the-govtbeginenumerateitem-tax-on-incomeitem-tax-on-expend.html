1. :

Tax on Income

2. :

Tax on Expenditure

3. :

Tax on Property or Capital Asset

4. :

Tax on Goods and Services

(A) Both 1 and 2 only

(B) Both 1 and 4 only

(C) ** All the above

(D) Only 2, 3 and 4
