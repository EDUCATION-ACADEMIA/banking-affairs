The web platform PAISA has been designed and developed by which of the following banks?

(A) ** Allahabad Bank

(B) UCO Bank

(C) ICICI

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Implementing agencies: The web platform has been designed and developed by Allahabad Bank which is the Nodal bank.

Concept note-2: -PAiSA: Portal for Affordable credit and Interest Subvention Access. Menu.

Concept note-3: -Paisa is a project under the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana. It stands for Portal of Affordable Credit and Interest Subvention Access. This portal is designed and developed by Allahabad Bank of India which is also a nodal bank for this project.
