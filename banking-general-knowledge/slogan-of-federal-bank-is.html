Slogan of Federal Bank is____?

(A) For all your needs

(B) One family one bank

(C) Together we can

(D) ** Your Perfect Banking Partner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Federal Bank is the first bank in India to be the sponsor bank for White Label ATMsIn August 2013; Federal Bank launched FedBook the first electronic passbook launched by a bank in India. FedBook is a mobile app through which customers can view their passbook details.

Concept note-2: -’Faithful and friendly’ was the tagline of Syndicate Bank. On 1 April 2020, Syndicate bank was merged into Canara Bank.
