How many Baroda deposit cards per account can be issued to the customer?

(A) 5

(B) ** 10

(C) 2

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Daily cash withdrawal Rs. 1, 50, 000 from ATM. Daily POS/E-commerce transactions Up to Rs 5, 00, 000.

Concept note-2: -Yes, you can generally have two credit cards from the same bank, as most issuers allow it. Getting more than one card with the same bank may actually be a good idea in certain situations, too.

Concept note-3: -Yes you can have many Atm cards to a single Account. But each card has separate annual fee which is charged for every active card you hold. Also it is somewhat risky for you as if u have lost any one card, it will give access to your account to other person.

Concept note-4: -You can have many Debit Cards attached with your account, but all Debit Cards should be of different variant. For example, SBI Classic Card, SBI Global Card, SBI Silver/Gold/Platinum etc Cards. You should maintain an Average Balance for 3 months which is required to avail for these cards.
