Who among the following has created the Logo of Aadhar Card?

(A) ** AtUl sudhakarrao pandey

(B) Nandan Nileknai

(C) R Chandra Shekhar

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Who among the following has created the Logo of Aadhar Card? Notes: Atul Sudhakarrao Pande, a Mumbai resident has created the logo of the Aadhaar card. His design was selected among the 2000 entries made for the logo design.

Concept note-2: -The Aadhaar logo was unveiled at the UIDAI Ecosystem event held at VigyanBhawan, New Delhi on 26th April 2010. Atul S. Pande, the winner of the logo competition, received Rs. 1, 00, 000 in prize money.

Concept note-3: -Tembhli is a village in Nandurbar district of Maharashtra state in India, from where the ambitious Aadhaar Scheme was launched on 29 September 2010. Ranjana Sonawane became the first person to be given an Aadhaar identity number.
