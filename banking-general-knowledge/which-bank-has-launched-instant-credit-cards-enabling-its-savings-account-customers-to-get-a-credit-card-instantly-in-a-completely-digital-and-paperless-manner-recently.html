Which Bank has launched instant credit cards enabling its savings account customers to get a credit card instantly, in a completely digital and paperless manner recently?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) ** ICICI Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) Bank of Baroda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI Bank | ICICI Bank introduces ‘PayLater’, an instant digital credit facility for small payments.

Concept note-2: -1. HDFC Bank instant approval credit card. HDFC Bank credit cards are not only 100% secure, but they also provide instant activation and ownership.

Concept note-3: -ICICI Bank Instant Platinum Credit Card is a secured credit card that is issued against a fixed deposit. Anyone who can open a FD account with at least Rs. 20, 000 can avail this credit card. Cardholder will earn interest on the deposited amount.

Concept note-4: -The three variants of the card are named Celesta, Imperio and Signet, each of which is designed to cater to the needs of different segments of customers. Federal Bank on Friday launched its credit card in association with Visa.

Concept note-5: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.
