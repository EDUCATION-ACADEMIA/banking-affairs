The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) released the Global Liveability Index 2018, the ranking of 140 global cities based on their living conditions recently. Which of the following city is tops in this Index?

(A) New York

(B) Osaka

(C) Melbourne

(D) ** Vienna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Austrian Capital, Vienna, topped the Global Liveability Report, 2022, with a 99.1 rating. It replaced Auckland, which topped in 2021. Vienna ranked the 12th position in 2021 since most of its museums and restaurants were closed due to Covid-19. It rebounded to 1st place, which it also held in 2018 and 2019.

Concept note-2: -For the third time in the past five years, Vienna captured the first spot in the 2022 Global Liveability Index, the annual report published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).

Concept note-3: -EIU’s complete liveability dataset ranks the challenges to an individual’s lifestyle in 173 cities worldwide.
