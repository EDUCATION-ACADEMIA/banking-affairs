Which state has the highest number of RRBs in India?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Bihar

(C) West Bengal

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which state has the highest number of RRBs in India? Notes: Uttar Pradesh has the highest number of 36 RRBs, followed by, Madhya Pradesh with 19 and Bihar with 16 RRBs. There are no RRBs in Goa and Sikkim.

Concept note-2: -The services of RRBs are not available in the states of Goa and Sikkim. Madhya Pradesh has second highest number of branches while Nagaland has the least number of branches.

Concept note-3: -Prathama Bank, with head office in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh was the first RRB. It was sponsored by Syndicate Bank and had an authorised capital of Rs. 5 crore.
