What is the authorised capital of National Insurance Company Limited (NICL)?

(A) Rs.1500 crore

(B) Rs.100 crore

(C) ** Rs.200 crore

(D) Rs.500 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -According to the current norms, insurers are required to have a minimum paid-up capital of Rs 100 crore.
