Which of the following is NOT a source of funds of a commercial bank?

(A) Capital

(B) Call money

(C) Deposits

(D) ** Cash reserves with RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commercial banks make money by providing and earning interest from loans such as mortgages, auto loans, business loans, and personal loans. Customer deposits provide banks with the capital to make these loans.

Concept note-2: -Therefore, Issuance of Letters of Credit is not the fund based business of commercial banks.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India is not a commercial bank because it is the central bank of India with the authority to issue currency in the country.

Concept note-4: -Out of the given options, fixed deposit rate is not decided by RBI.
