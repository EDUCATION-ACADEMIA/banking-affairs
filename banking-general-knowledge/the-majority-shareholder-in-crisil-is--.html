The Majority shareholder in CRISIL is ____ ?

(A) ** Standard and Poors

(B) Fitch Ratings Inc.

(C) Moody’s

(D) Dun and Bradstreet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Standard and Poor’s (S&P) is the majority shareholder of CRISIL, so it is also called Standard & Poor’s company.

Concept note-2: -The majority shareholder of CRISIL is Standard & Poor’s, a division of McGraw Hill Financial & a provider of financial market intelligence.

Concept note-3: -CRISIL is majority owned by S&P Global Inc, a leading provider of transparent and independent ratings, benchmarks, analytics and data to the capital and commodity markets worldwide.

Concept note-4: -S&P acquires majority stake in CRISIL The strategic alliance with S&P since 1996 culminates in S&P’s acquiring majority control of CRISIL.
