Which of the following is/are the reasons owing to which about 105 million new people are added to the list of hungry people and the total number of the same has reached about 1 billion?

1. :

Unprecedented increase in food prices, particularly in poor nations

2. :

Cut in the supply of foodgrains to poor nations by many European countries

3. :

Sudden increase in population of the countries like Uganda, Ethiopia, Sudan and Nabibia as many refugees have taken shelter there.

(A) ** Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) Only 3

(D) All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hunger is the main outcome of poverty In India, poor government policies and misuse of the financially weaker section by the wealthier community are the primary causes of poverty. Hunger is the main effect of poverty.

Concept note-2: -Poverty. Poverty is the greatest cause of hunger around the world – in both higher-wealth and low-to middle-income countries.
