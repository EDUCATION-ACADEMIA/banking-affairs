The trident-shaped symbol of Buddhism does not represent

(A) ** Nirvana

(B) Sangha

(C) Buddha

(D) Dhamma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The trident-shaped symbol of Buddhism does not represent? Answers. Correct Answer is Nirvana. The Buddhism trident shaped symbol is a reference to the bhuddha, dhamma and sangha.

Concept note-2: -An ananda-chakra. A trident, or trisula, with three branches, representing the threefold jewels of Buddhism: Buddha, the Dhamma and the Sangha.

Concept note-3: -Solution(By Examveda Team) Nirvana because in buddhism trident shaped symbol is a reference to the triple gem bhuddha, dhamma and sangha.
