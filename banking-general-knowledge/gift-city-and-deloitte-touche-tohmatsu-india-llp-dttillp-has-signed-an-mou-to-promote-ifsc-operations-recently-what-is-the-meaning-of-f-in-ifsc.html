(B) Funding

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gujarat International Finance Tech-City (GIFT City) is a business district currently being constructed in the state of Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Tax incentives in GIFT-IFSC Tax holiday for capital gains for aircraft leasing companies, tax exemption for aircraft lease rentals. No GST on services received by these companies, provided to IFSC SEZ units or offshore clients.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat International Finance Tech-city (GIFT) SEZ is India’s first International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) under Special Economic Zone Act, 2005 (“SEZ Act 2005”).

Concept note-4: -Institutions such as Standard Chartered Bank, Hongkong & Shanghai Bank Corporation Ltd, Citibank NA, Barclays Bank, Deutsche Bank, and JPMorgan Chase Bank National Association have set up IFSC Banking Units (IBUs) at GIFT City.
