NPCI has authorized our bank to become which unit In BBPS platform.

(A) Only Biller operating unit

(B) Only customer operating unit

(C) ** Both Biller & Customer Operating unit

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) has been authorized by the RBI as the Bharat Bill Payment Central Unit (BBPCU), which is responsible for setting business standards, rules and procedures for technical and business requirements for all participants.

Concept note-2: -The BBPS will be an authorised payment system operated by National Payment Corporation of India (NPCI).

Concept note-3: -The Bharat Bill payment system is a Reserve Bank of India (RBI) conceptualised system driven by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).

Concept note-4: -The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) will function as the authorized Bharat Bill Payment Central Unit (BBPCU) to set the standards for BBPS processes which need to be adhered to by all operating units under the system.
