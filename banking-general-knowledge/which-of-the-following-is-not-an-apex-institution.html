Which of the following is not an apex institution?

(A) ** SBI

(B) SIDBI

(C) NABARD

(D) State Cooperative bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reserve Bank of India is known as the apex bank of India.

Concept note-2: -The central bank is the apex institution of the monetary and banking structure of our economy, regulating the entire banking system.

Concept note-3: -National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) is an apex regulatory body for overall regulation of regional rural banks and apex cooperative banks in India. It is under the jurisdiction of Ministry of Finance, Government of India.
