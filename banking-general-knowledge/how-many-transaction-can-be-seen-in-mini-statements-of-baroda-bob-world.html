How many transaction can be seen in MINI statements of Baroda BOB WORLD?

(A) 20

(B) 15

(C) ** 10

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Step 3: Account holder will receive Bank of Baroda Mini Statement by SMS which consists of the last 5 transactions (both debit and credit).

Concept note-2: -You get the complete list of transactions in your account statement. Mini statement is just a part of this statement containing the latest 5-10 transactions.

Concept note-3: -You can get a copy of your mini statement by calling 8468001122 or texting ‘MINIspace>last 4-digits of A/C No. ‘ to 8422009988. You can also log in to the BOB Connect Net Banking Portal or download and use the Baroda M-Connect Plus mobile application. Mini statements are also available at any BOB or other bank’s ATM.

Concept note-4: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day. Customer can apply for Debit Card and manage other card activities like: Set daily debit card limit for ATM transactions.
