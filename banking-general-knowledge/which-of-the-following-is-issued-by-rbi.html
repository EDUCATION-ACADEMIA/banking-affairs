Which of the following is issued by RBI?

(A) ** Currency notes

(B) Coins

(C) Both 1 and 2

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Banknotes in India are currently being issued in the denomination of ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100 ₹200, ₹500, and ₹2000. These notes are called banknotes as they are issued by the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-2: -Presently, the Reserve Bank issues banknotes in denominations of ₹2, ₹5, ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, ₹500 and ₹2000. Coins in circulation comprise 50 paise and ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, ₹10 and ₹20 denominations. VIII.

Concept note-3: -Rupees Five-First Note issued by Reserve Bank of India This was followed by Rs 10 in February, Rs 100 in March and Rs 1, 000 and Rs 10, 000 in June 1938.
