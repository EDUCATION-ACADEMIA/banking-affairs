BANKING GENERAL KNOWLEDGE

[SOURCES]
Merchant banks shall not associate with any business other than that of the securities market.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which of the following is true about merchant bank? Notes: Merchant bank underwrites securities for corporations, advices clients on mergers and get involves in in ownership of commercial ventures.

Concept note-2: -CHAPTER III: GENERAL OBLIGATIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES 13A. Merchant banker not to associate with any business other than that of the securities market.

Concept note-3: -Investment banks conduct trade finance activities while merchant banks take part in international finance and underwriting activities.

Concept note-4: -Merchant banks specialize in international trade, providing services for multinational corporations. Unlike retail or commercial banks, merchant banks do not provide financial services to the general public.
