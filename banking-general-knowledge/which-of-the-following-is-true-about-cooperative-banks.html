Which of the following is true about Cooperative Banks?

(A) ** They are established under the Cooperative Societies Act

(B) They have profit as their main motive

(C) All are scheduled banks

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UCBs are primarily registered as cooperative societies under the provisions of either the State Cooperative Societies Act of the State concerned or the Multi State Cooperative Societies Act, 2002 if the area of operation of the bank extends beyond the boundaries of one state.

Concept note-2: -Under the provisions of Section 23 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Cooperative Societies), primary (urban) cooperative banks are required to obtain permission from the Reserve Bank of India for opening branches.
