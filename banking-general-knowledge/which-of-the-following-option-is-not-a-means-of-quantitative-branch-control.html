Which of the following option is not a means of quantitative branch control?

(A) bank rate policy

(B) open market policy

(C) Cash deposit ratio

(D) ** Publicity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank rate, Statutory Cash Reserve Requirement, Statutory Liquidity Ratio are the instruments of quantitative credit control. Moral Suasion is not quantitative credit control instrument of credit control.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is ‘A’-Bank Rate. The important quantitative methods of credit control is (a) bank rate. The methods used by the central bank to regulate the flows of credit into particular directions of the economy are called qualitative or selective methods of credit control.

Concept note-3: -Margin requirements is not a quantitative credit control tool of RBI.

Concept note-4: -Moral suasion is a qualitative method, and other options are quantitative methods of monetary policy.
