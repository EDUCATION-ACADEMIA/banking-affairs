Which of the following was set up as a exclusive data communication network for banks in terms of recommendations of committee on communication network of banks?

(A) ** BANKNET

(B) SWIFT

(C) MICR

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5 The Committees on Communication Network for Banks and SWIFT Implementation, chaired by Shri T.N.A. Iyer, Executive Director, Reserve Bank of India recommended the setting up of BANKNET and also suggested that banks in India should join the SWIFT Network.

Concept note-2: -An interbank network, also known as an ATM consortium or ATM network, is a computer network that enables ATM cards issued by a financial institution that is a member of the network to be used to perform ATM transactions through ATMs that belong to another member of the network.

Concept note-3: -RBINet is a communica-tion software, developed in ‘C’ and available for both DOS and UNIX machines. It allows free format messaging and file transfer on the existing BANKNET infrastructure with the help of UNIX servers installed at the 4 NCCs.
