Which scheme has announced in Interim Budget 2019-20 to provide pensionary benefits to at least 10 crore labourers and workers in the unorganised sector?

(A) Integrated Child Development Scheme

(B) ** Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Mandhan

(C) Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

(D) Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is expected that at least 10 crore labourers and workers in the unorganised sector will avail the benefit of ‘Pradhan Mantri Shram-Yogi Maandhan’ within next five years making it one of the largest pension schemes of the world.

Concept note-2: -Government of India has introduced a pension scheme for unorganised workers namely Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan (PM-SYM) to ensure old age protection for Unorganised Workers.

Concept note-3: -Unorganised Sectors Are More Secure With NPS Under this voluntary pension scheme, each subscriber would receive a guaranteed minimum pension amount of 3000 every month, after reaching 60 years. And in case a subscriber dies, 50% of his pension will be given to his spouse as a family pension.

Concept note-4: -The Union Interim Budget 2019 was presented by Finance Minister Piyush Goyal on 1st February 2019.
