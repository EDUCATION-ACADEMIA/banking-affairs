Which among the following Banks has launches ‘Saral-Rural Housing Loan’ Scheme for weaker sections?

(A) ** ICICI Bank

(B) SBI

(C) PNB

(D) Syndicate Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The scheme offers a flexible tenure of 3 to 20 years. The ICICI Bank’s Saral Rural Housing Loan can be applied at any ICICI Bank branch in the locations where the loan is being offered.

Concept note-2: -ICICI Home Finance launches “SARAL” scheme for affordable house loan.

Concept note-3: -What is the Full form of ICICI? The full form of ICICI is Industrial Credit and Investment Corporation of India. It was ICICI Bank’s parent organisation which had been incorporated in 2002 with ICICI Bank.
