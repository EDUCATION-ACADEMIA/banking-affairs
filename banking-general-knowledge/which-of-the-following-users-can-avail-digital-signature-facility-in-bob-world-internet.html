Which of the following users can avail Digital Signature facility in bob World Internet?

(A) Retail Users with View Rights

(B) Retail Users with TXN Rights

(C) ** Corporate users with TXN rights

(D) Corporate Users with View rights

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -OD accounts are not eligible to open deposits, invest in PPF or SSY accounts. Only Savings and current accounts are eligible.

Concept note-2: -Corporate: All non-individuals i.e., companies, partnership firms, HUFs, sole proprietors should use corporate form. The form should be duly filled in, signed by all signatories i.e., all joint account holders in case of a joint account, all partners in case of a partnership firm etc.

Concept note-3: -Enter Account number, registered mobile number and registered email id.
