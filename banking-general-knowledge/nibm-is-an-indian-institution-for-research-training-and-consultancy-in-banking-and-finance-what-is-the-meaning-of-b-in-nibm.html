NIBM is an Indian institution for research, training, and consultancy in banking and finance. What is the meaning of “B” in NIBM?

(A) Base

(B) ** Bank

(C) Branch

(D) Banking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Institute of Bank Management (NIBM) was established in 1969 by the Reserve Bank of India, in consultation with the Government of India, as an autonomous apex institution for research, training, education and consultancy in bank management.

Concept note-2: -Named after the famous National Institute of Bank Management (NIBM), NIBM Road is a threekm long stretch that runs along the south-eastern micro-market of Mohammadwadi and covers some parts of Undri.

Concept note-3: -Yes, highly recognized and respected within the industry. A lot of executives from multinational companies carry an MBA or Executive MBA from NIBM.

Concept note-4: -Selection of candidates is done on the basis of CAT 2022/ XAT 2023/ CMAT 2023 / GMAT / GRE scores followed by the Writing Ability Test and Interview in selected centres.
