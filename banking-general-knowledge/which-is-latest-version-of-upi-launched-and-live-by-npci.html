Which is latest version of UPI launched and live by NPCI?

(A) UPI 1.0

(B) ** UPI 2.0

(C) UPI 3.0

(D) UPI 4.0

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), the umbrella organisation for all retail payments in India, on Thursday launched the second iteration of its Unified Payments Interface (UPI). Called UPI 2.0, the new digital payment platform comes with a wide range of new features.

Concept note-2: -Launched in August 2018, UPI 2.0 is the second version of the fund transfer platform. It supersedes the former version which was structured mainly to facilitate peer-to-peer transactions with a platform that also accommodates merchant payments.

Concept note-3: -The UPI 2.0 innovative features, such as the one-time mandate activation and facility to link overdraft (OD) accounts, provide need-based credit facilities to users. The facility would allow users to avail of credit through OD accounts, even when their account does not have sufficient balance.
