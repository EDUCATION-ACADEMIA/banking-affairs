Which among the following NBFC are not excluded from the Ombudsman scheme for NBFC?

(A) ** Deposit accepting NBFCs

(B) NBFC-IFC

(C) Core Investment Company

(D) IDF-NBFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The non banking financial company-infrastructure finance company (NBFC-IFC), core investment company (CIC), infrastructure debt fund-non-banking financial company (IDF-NBFC) and an NBFC under liquidation, are excluded from the ambit of the Scheme.

Concept note-2: -The Non-Banking Financial Company-Infrastructure Finance Company (NBFC-IFC), Core Investment Company (CIC), Infrastructure Debt Fund-Non-Banking Financial Company (IDF-NBFC) and an NBFC under liquidation, are excluded from the ambit of the Scheme. 3.

Concept note-3: -NBFC in liquidation; NBFC having only captive customers.

Concept note-4: -All NBFCs are not entitled to accept public deposits. Only those NBFCs to which the Bank had given a specific authorisation and have an investment grade rating are allowed to accept/ hold public deposits to a limit of 1.5 times of its Net Owned Funds.

Concept note-5: -NBFC cannot accept demand deposits; NBFCs do not form part of the payment and settlement system and cannot issue cheques drawn on itself; deposit insurance facility of Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation is not available to depositors of NBFCs, unlike in case of banks.
