Which Bank has launched EazyPay mobile application for merchants that allows all-in-one acceptance payments platform?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) Union Bank of India

(C) ** ICICI Bank

(D) Axis Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI Bank launches ‘Eazypay’, India’s first mobile app for merchants to accept payments on mobile phone through multiple digital modes.

Concept note-2: -It is a mobile based application which allows you to quickly raise an invoice and your customers can pay you immediately through any convenient payment mode – Unified Payment Interface (UPI), Credit Card, Debit Card, Internet Banking or Pockets wallet.

Concept note-3: -All ICICI Bank Current Account customers who are registered on Corporate Internet Banking (CIB) can access InstaBIZ application on their Android and iOS devices.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai: ICICI Bank today announced that it has transformed its state-of-the-art mobile banking app, iMobile, into an app that offers payments and banking services to customers of any bank.

Concept note-5: -iMobile Pay – ICICI Mobile Banking App.
