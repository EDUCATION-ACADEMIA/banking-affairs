New scheme to installation of small renewable energy project up to 2 MW, solar pump or solarisation of existing grid connected agriculture pumps is called

(A) ** PMKUSUM

(B) PMLUSUM

(C) PMRUSUM

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM KUSUM Scheme ( Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthan Mahabhiyan ) Scope. The scheme will be operational on Pan India. Objectives of the Scheme. Ø For installation of Solar Pumps and Grid connected solar and other Renewable Energy Power Plants (REPP).

Concept note-2: -Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) has launched the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evem Utthan Mahabhiyan (PM KUSUM) Scheme for farmers for installation of solar pumps and grid connected solar and other renewable power plants in the country.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bhaloji, Jaipur. The first solar power plant under the PM-KUSUM Scheme has been set up in India at Bhaloji, Jaipur.

Concept note-4: -Kusum Solar Pump Yojana Registration The scheme promotes the installation of Solar plants on barren and unproductive land owned by the farmers. The electricity that will be generated will be bought by DISCOM, and it will produce income for 25 years. Its maintenance is also easy. Banks will provide a 30% loan facility.
