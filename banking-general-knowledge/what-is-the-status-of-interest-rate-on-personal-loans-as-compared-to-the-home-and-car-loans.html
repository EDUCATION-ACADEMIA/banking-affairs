What is the status of Interest rate on Personal Loans as compared to the Home and Car Loans?

(A) ** Higher than Home and Car Loans

(B) Lower than Home and Car Loans

(C) Equal to Home and Car Loans

(D) Can not be compared

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The general rate of interest offered when it comes to car loans ranges between 8.5% to around 14% while personal loan interest rates can easily go up to 20% and sometimes even more.

Concept note-2: -However, since a personal loan is an unsecured mode of finance, the interest rates are higher than secured loans. A good credit score and high and stable income ensure you can avail this loan at a competitive interest rate. Personal loans can be used for the following purposes: Manage all expenses of a family wedding.

Concept note-3: -Personal loan interest rates are higher than those of home loans. These are unsecured loans, and the borrower is not required to pledge any collateral.

Concept note-4: -Key Takeaways. Personal loan interest rates are expressed as a percentage of the amount you borrow. Most personal loans are unsecured-that is, not backed up by a recoverable asset or collateral. Unsecured personal loans charge a higher interest rate than secured loans.
