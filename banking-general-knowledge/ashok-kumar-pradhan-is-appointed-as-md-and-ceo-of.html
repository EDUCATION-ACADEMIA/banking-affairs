Ashok Kumar Pradhan is appointed as MD and CEO of

(A) Bank of Maharashtra

(B) ** United Bank of India

(C) Punjab & Sind Bank

(D) UCO Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Shanti Lal Jain assumed charge as Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of Indian Bank on 1st September 2021.

Concept note-2: -Ashok Kumar Pradhan (born 13 December 1953) is an Indian industrialist and politician from Uttar Pradesh. He as served as the Member of Parliament (MP) in Lok Sabha representing Khurja (Noida) from 1996 to 2009 as Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) candidate.

Concept note-3: -The Centre has extended the tenure of managing director (MD) and CEO of Bank of Baroda (BoB) Sanjiv Chadha for about five months till 30 June, 2023. The order comes ahead of his three-year tenure that ends on 19 January.
