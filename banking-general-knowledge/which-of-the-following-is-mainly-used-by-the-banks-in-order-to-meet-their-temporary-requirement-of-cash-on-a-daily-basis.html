(A) Commercial Paper

(B) Certificate of Deposit

(C) Collateralized Borrowing and Lending Obligations (CBLO)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Call money is mainly used by the banks to meet their temporary requirement of cash. They borrow and lend money from each other normally on a daily basis. It is repayable on demand and its maturity period varies in between one day to a fortnight. The rate of interest paid-on-call money loan is known as the call rate.
