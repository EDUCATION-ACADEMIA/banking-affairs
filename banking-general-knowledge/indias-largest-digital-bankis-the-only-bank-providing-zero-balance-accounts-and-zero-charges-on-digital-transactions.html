India’s largest digital bank____is the only bank providing zero balance accounts and zero charges on digital transactions.

(A) India Post Payments Bank

(B) ** Paytm Payments Bank

(C) Airtel Payments Bank

(D) Fino Payments Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s largest digital bank Paytm Payments Bank is the only bank providing zero balance accounts and zero charges on digital transactions.

Concept note-2: -Paytm Payments Bank is India’s only mobile-first bank with zero balance-zero digital transaction charge. Paytm offers Paytm wallet and Savings bank account from Paytm Payment bank.

Concept note-3: -Digitally-enabled banks allow users to access all banking services 24×7 without the need to personally visit the bank branch – all you need is a mobile phone or a computer!
