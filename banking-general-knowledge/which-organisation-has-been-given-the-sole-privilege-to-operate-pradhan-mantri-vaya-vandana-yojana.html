Which organisation has been given the sole privilege to operate Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana?

(B) IRDAI

Concept note-2: -The Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana (PMVVY) scheme is provided by the Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India. Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) is the only operator for this scheme.
