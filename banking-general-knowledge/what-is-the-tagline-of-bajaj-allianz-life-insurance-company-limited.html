What is the tagline of Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company Limited?

(A) Prithvi, Agni, Jal, Akash, Sabki Suraksha Hamare Paas

(B) Zimmedari ka humsafar

(C) You click, we cover

(D) ** Jiyo Befiqar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2008, the Company expanded its gamut of products to include different customer needs and adopted a new tagline “Jiyo Befikar”, meaning ‘live worry free’.

Concept note-2: -Be it care at the stage of consideration, purchase, experience, claim or renewal, with “Caringly Yours” our intention is to make insurance a seamless part of the customers lives where they will be able to relate their everyday worries to the product and allied services that we will be able to provide.

Concept note-3: -With its flexible products and benefit solutions, Max Life is driven by a strong set of customer-focused values that form the core of its business and exemplify its slogan, “Your Partner for Life”.

Concept note-4: -The main slogan of LIC is-“Yogakshemam Vahamyaham” meaning “Your welfare is our responsibility”. It is in Sanskrit and is obtained from the 22nd verse of the Bhagavad Gita’s 9th chapter.
