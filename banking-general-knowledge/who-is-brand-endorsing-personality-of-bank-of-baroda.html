Who is Brand Endorsing Personality of Bank of Baroda?

(B) Kiran Bedi

(C) Amitabh Bachchan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-3: -It comprises dual ‘B’ letterforms that hold the rays of the rising sun. We call this the Baroda Sun. The sun is an excellent representation of what our bank stands for.
