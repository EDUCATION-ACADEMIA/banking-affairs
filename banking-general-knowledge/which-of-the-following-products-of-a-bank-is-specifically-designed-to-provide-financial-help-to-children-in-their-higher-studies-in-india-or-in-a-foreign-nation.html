Which of the following products of a bank is specifically designed to provide financial help to children in their higher studies in India or in a foreign nation?

(A) Personal Loan

(B) Corporate Loan

(C) ** Educational Loan

(D) Mortgage Loan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Educational loan helps a student to get financial help so that he/she can study in their higher studies in India or in a foreign nation. Thus, exclusively educational loan deals with the financial help for pursuing higher studies.

Concept note-2: -Banks are important facilitators of international trade. Besides providing liquidity they guarantee payment for around a fifth of world trade, in particular when the contract enforce-ment of the importing country is weak.

Concept note-3: -As per AS 26, Financial Asset includes all deposits and investments in Shares, Debentures, etc.
