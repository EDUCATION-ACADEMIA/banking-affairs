I am not getting my Account statements and other certificates through BOB WORLD, what may be the probable cause

(A) ** Communication Email ID is not updated with Bank

(B) Communication address is not updated with Bank

(C) Mobile Number is not updated with Bank

(D) Both Email ID is not updated with Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda 3 Months Account Statement First, you need to register your email id with your BOB account. You can register your email id online using mobile banking and after that, you can request any 3 months account statement. To register your email id, open Mobile Banking and go to Service Requests.

Concept note-2: -By using this service, customers who have registered their mobile number, can get mini statement of their accounts by just giving a missed call from their registered mobile number on 8468001122.

Concept note-3: -To get the statement for the time duration as per wish, you need to download Bank of Baroda’s mobile banking app bob World and log in with your customer ID and MPIN. After logging in the app, you need to select the time period of the mini statements you want and download the PDF.

Concept note-4: -Due to the open nature of the Internet, all web-based services such as YAB’s Online Banking are inherently subject to risks such as online theft of your User ID/UserName, Password, virus attacks, hacking, unauthorized access and fraudulent transactions.
