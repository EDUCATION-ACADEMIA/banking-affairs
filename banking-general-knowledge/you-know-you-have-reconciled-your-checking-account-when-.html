You know you have reconciled your checking account when ____

(A) The ending balance in your monthly bank statement matches your check register.

(B) ** You matched transactions between your bank statement and check register and accounted for those that have not yet hit your bank statement.

(C) The beginning balance in your monthly statement matches your check register.

(D) All the checks in your check register have been matched to your bank statement.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After all adjustments are made to the books, the balance should equal the ending balance of the bank account. If the figures are equal, then a successful bank reconciliation statement has been prepared.

Concept note-2: -You should reconcile your checking account as soon as you receive your bank statement. A check cannot be cashed until it has been reconciled. A joint checking account can be opened by two or more people. A money order can never bounce.
