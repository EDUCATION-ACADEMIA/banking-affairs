As on date, there are____Banking Ombudsman centre located in India.

(A) ** 20

(B) 19

(C) 18

(D) 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are 22 regional offices of Banking Ombudsmen in India.

Concept note-2: -About Lokpal Vide notification dated 26th May 2022, Shri Justice Pradip Kumar Mohanty, Member (Judicial) has been authorised to act as the Chairperson, Lokpal with effect from 28th May, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Scheme integrates the existing three Ombudsman schemes of RBI namely, (i) the Banking Ombudsman Scheme, 2006; (ii) the Ombudsman Scheme for Non-Banking Financial Companies, 2018; and (iii) the Ombudsman Scheme for Digital Transactions, 2019.

Concept note-4: -Presently, Offices of RBI Ombudsmen (ORBIOs) are functioning from 22 locations across India.
