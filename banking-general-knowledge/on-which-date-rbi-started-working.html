On which date, RBI started working?

(A) 1 January 1934

(B) 1 January 1935

(C) ** 1 April 1935

(D) 31 March 1934

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 (II of 1934) provides the statutory basis of the functioning of the Bank, which commenced operations on April 1, 1935.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India was established on April 1, 1935 in accordance with the provisions of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. The Central Office of the Reserve Bank was initially established in Kolkata but was permanently moved to Mumbai in 1937.

Concept note-3: -Sir Osborne Smith was the first Governor of the Reserve Bank.
