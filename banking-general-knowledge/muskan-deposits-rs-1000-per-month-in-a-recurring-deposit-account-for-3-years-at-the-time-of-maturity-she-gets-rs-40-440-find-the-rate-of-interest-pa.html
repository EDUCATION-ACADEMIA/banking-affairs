Muskan deposits Rs. 1000 per month in a recurring deposit account for 3 years. At the time of maturity, she gets Rs. 40, 440. Find the rate of interest p.a.

(A) ** 8%

(B) 9%

(C) 10%

(D) 10.2%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The matured value after 3 years of deposit is Rs 40440 .

Concept note-2: -Hence Matured value =Rs. 40440.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is B Mrs. Goswami deposits Rs 1000 every month in a recurring deposit account for 3 years at 8% interest per annum.
