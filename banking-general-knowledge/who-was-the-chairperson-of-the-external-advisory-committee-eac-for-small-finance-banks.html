Who was the chairperson of the External Advisory Committee (EAC) for Small Finance Banks?

(A) Deepak Phatak

(B) MS Sahoo

(C) ** Usha Thorat

(D) Nachiket M Mor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India on Wednesday announced the names of the members of the External Advisory Committee (EAC) for small finance banks as well as payments banks. 1. Chairperson: Usha Thorat, former Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-2: -The Usha Thorat Committee is an advisory committee formed on July 10, 2020. The namesake for this Committee is Usha Thorat. She had served as the former deputy governor of RBI (Reserve Bank of India). The primary purpose of this Committee is to advise a capital market regulator.

Concept note-3: -Nachiket Mor and on Small Finance Banks chaired by Smt. Usha Thorat. Now an internal committee of the Reserve Bank, composed of its Deputy Governors and the Governor, is going through each of these applications again.
