Shaktikanta Das is the____governor of the Reserve Bank of India.

(A) 26th

(B) 24th

(C) 27th

(D) ** 25th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Shaktikanta Das, IAS Retd., former Secretary, Department of Revenue and Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India assumed charge as the 25th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India effective December 12, 2018.

Concept note-2: -Urjit Patel was the 24th governor of the Reserve Bank of India. He served as the governor of the Reserve Bank of India from 2016 to 2018. He is the first RBI governor to resign due to personal reasons.

Concept note-3: -Dr. Raghuram Rajan assumed charge as the 23rd Governor of the Reserve Bank of India on September 4, 2013.

Concept note-4: -Shaktikanta Das is the present RBI Governor, appointed for a period of three years.
