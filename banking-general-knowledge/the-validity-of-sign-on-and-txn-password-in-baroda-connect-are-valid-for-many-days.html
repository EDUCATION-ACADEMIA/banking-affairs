The validity of Sign on and Txn password in Baroda Connect are valid for many days?

(A) ** 360 days & 365 days

(B) 365 days & 360 days

(C) 360 days for both

(D) 365 days for both

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Please note that the Sign on password for BoB login is the Internet banking password only and this is valid for 180 days from the day of creation. So, in case you have completed the 180 period, then you have to create a new password by following the above password reset process.

Concept note-2: -contact Toll free Helpline no-1800 22 33 44/1800 102 44 55 to get the Transaction password activated.

Concept note-3: -Note: If you have not received the OTP then send following message ‘BOBOTP’ to 5616150. On the next screen, you can reset Bank of Baroda login password. Password should be a combination of letters, numbers and special characters and different from your BOB transaction password. Here is an example Kumar321.
