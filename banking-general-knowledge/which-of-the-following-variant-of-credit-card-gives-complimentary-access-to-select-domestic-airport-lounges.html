Which of the following variant of credit card gives complimentary access to select domestic Airport Lounges

(A) Easy

(B) Select

(C) ** Premier

(D) Prime

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Joining Fees. Bank Of Baroda Premier Credit Card is a lifetime free credit card offered by the BOB offering great benefits in the travel where you can enjoy complimentary domestic lounge access. Following are the benefits of the card: 5x reward points on spending on dining, travel, and international transactions.

Concept note-2: -A good example of such cards is the HDFC Bank Regalia First Credit Card. The card offers exclusive benefits to its cardholders. Complimentary access to more than 1, 000 Priority Pass Airport Lounges across the globe. Access to VISA/ MasterCard Lounges.

Concept note-3: -American Express Platinum credit card is a super-premium credit card that offers unlimited complimentary domestic and international airport lounge access. You will also enjoy complimentary access to over 30 golf courses in India and 50+ golf courses across the world.

Concept note-4: -“Card” means any valid and unexpired Visa Infinite, Visa Signature, Business Platinum, Business Signature and Corporate Platinum card issued in India.

Concept note-5: -Complimentary amenities at airport lounges include snacks and beverages, alcohol, WiFi, power outlets for gadgets, conference rooms, restrooms, etc. The facilities also vary from one card to another.
