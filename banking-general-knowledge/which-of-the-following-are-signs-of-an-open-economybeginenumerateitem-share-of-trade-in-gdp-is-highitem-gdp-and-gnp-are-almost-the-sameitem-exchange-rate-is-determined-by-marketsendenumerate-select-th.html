Concept note-2: -The four components of gross domestic product are personal consumption, business investment, government spending, and net exports.

Concept note-3: -Measuring GDP. GDP measures the monetary value of final goods and services-that is, those that are bought by the final user-produced in a country in a given period of time (say a quarter or a year). It counts all of the output generated within the borders of a country.

Concept note-4: -The GDP includes only the finished goods and services and does not include the intermediate goods produced.
