Which of these RRB is not sponsored by State bank of India?

(A) Saurashtra Grameen Bank

(B) Utkal Grameen Bank

(C) Uttarakhand Grameen Bank

(D) ** Punjab Grameen Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Punjab Gramin Bank is one of the leading Regional Rural Bank in India having Punjab National Bank, one of the premier banks in India as its Sponsor bank.

Concept note-2: -Regional Rural Banks are sponsored by any commercial bank, PSU or private bank. The central government, sponsor bank, and state government have share capital in the ration of 50:35:15.

Concept note-3: -RRBs operate majorly towards agriculture finance, small sector loans, handicrafts and other small sector loans, whereas commercial banks or PSBs provide many other facilities like car loans, housing loans, credit to large companies along with agriculture finance etc.
