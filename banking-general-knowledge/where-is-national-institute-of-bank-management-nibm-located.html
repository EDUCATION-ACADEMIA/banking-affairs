Where is National Institute of Bank Management (NIBM) located?

(A) Nasik

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** Pune

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Institute of Bank Management (NIBM) is an autonomous institute located in Pune, India.

Concept note-2: -The process of selection is very similar to other B-schools but the intake capacity makes the process a bit competitive. The total number of seats in the PGDM B&FS is 120. Around 1000 applicants attend the WAT and PI stage every year. Amongst them, only 120 students will get selected.

Concept note-3: -The Institute is located in sylvan surroundings in a picturesque valley in the Pune city in the State of Maharashtra.
