Baroda Paypoint is a web/mobile based payment collection portal for Merchants who ____

(A) Don’t have their own website

(B) Don’t have their own online infrastructure

(C) Don’t have their own IT Team / developer

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PayPoint India has entered into a partnership with Bank of Baroda, enabling the bank to further expand its network by utilising PayPoint’s customer service points to a reach out to a larger pool of customers and achieve a bigger geographical spread.

Concept note-2: -A merchant account is a specific bank account necessary for accepting debit/card and electronic card payments. Issued by institutions ranging from payment service providers, acquirers, and payment gateways, such accounts allow businesses to receive funds from customers safely and promptly.

Concept note-3: -In simple terms, a payment gateway is a network through which your customers transfer funds to you. Payment gateways are very similar to the point-of-sale terminals used at most brick and mortar stores. When using a payment gateway, customers and businesses need to work together to make a transaction.
