What is not true about Payment bank?

(A) It cannot issue loans

(B) It can accept deposits

(C) ** It can issue credit cards

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Payment banks cannot issue credit cards. It cannot accept time deposits or NRI deposits. It cannot issue loans. It cannot set up subsidiaries to undertake non-banking financial activities.

Concept note-2: -Definition: A payments bank is like any other bank, but operating on a smaller scale without involving any credit risk. In simple words, it can carry out most banking operations but can’t advance loans or issue credit cards.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The Correct Answer is Option 4 i.e Credit cards can be issued.

Concept note-4: -Issuance of ATM / Debit Cards. Payments banks, however, cannot issue credit cards.

Concept note-5: -Payment Banks can Accept demand deposits. Payment Banks can Issue ATM/Debit cards, but can’t issue credit cards.
