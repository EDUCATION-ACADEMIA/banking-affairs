If the total deposit created by commercial banks is Rs 20000 cr and LRR is 20%, then amount of initial deposit will be

(A) Rs 2000 cr

(B) Rs 3000 cr

(C) ** Rs 4000 cr

(D) Rs 14000 cr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The initial deposits of ₹10, 000 have been made into the bank, and the banks are required to maintain 20% of the deposits with them as the LRR is 20%, therefore the bank has to maintain 20% of ₹10, 000 i.e. ₹2, 000 with itself and can lend the rest of the money i.e. ₹8, 000 as loans to the public.

Concept note-2: -Money Multiplier = 10.2=5 1 0.2 = 5 where reserve ratio is 20 percent or 0.2. 2.
