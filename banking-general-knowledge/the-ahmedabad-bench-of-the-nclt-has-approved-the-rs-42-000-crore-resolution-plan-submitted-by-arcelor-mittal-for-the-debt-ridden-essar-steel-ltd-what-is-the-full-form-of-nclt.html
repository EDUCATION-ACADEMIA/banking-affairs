The Ahmedabad bench of the NCLT has approved the Rs 42, 000-crore resolution plan submitted by Arcelor Mittal for the debt-ridden Essar Steel Ltd. What is the full form of NCLT?

(A) ** National Company Law Tribunal

(B) National Company Law Treaty

(C) National Company Law Transit

(D) National Council Law Tribunal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The committee of creditors of bankrupt Essar Steel had chosen ArcelorMittal as the highest bidder and submitted the Rs42, 000-crore plan of the world’s largest steel maker before the NCLT bencha at Ahmedabad for approval. This was ArcelorMittal’s second bid for Essar Steel.

Concept note-2: -Arcelor-Nippon JV to acquire Essar’s steel infrastructure for $2.4 billion.

Concept note-3: -The The NCLT and NCLAT are two important institutions in India that play a crucial role in resolving disputes related to corporate law. The NCLT is the primary forum for resolving disputes, while the NCLAT is the appellate tribunal for hearing appeals against orders passed by the NCLT.

Concept note-4: -The tribunal was established under the Companies Act 2013 and was constituted on 1 June 2016 by the government of India and is based on the recommendation of the V. Balakrishna Eradi committee on law relating to the insolvency and the winding up of companies.
