All Scheduled Commercial Banks excluding____are eligible to implement under Gold Monetization Scheme, 2015?

(A) Private Sector Banks

(B) IDBI Bank Limited

(C) State Bank of India

(D) ** Regional Rural Banks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gold Monetisation Scheme | Union Bank of India.

Concept note-2: -Features of the Gold Monetization Scheme The minimum amount of gold to be deposited under the scheme is 30 grams of raw gold in any form like bars, coins, jewellery but does not include stones and other metal.

Concept note-3: -Regional Rural Banks (RRB) are Indian Scheduled Commercial Banks ( Government Banks) operating at regional level in different states of India. They have been created with a view of serving primarily the rural areas of India with basic banking and financial services.
