FDI to India decreased to____billion dollars last year from 44 billion dollars in 2016 while outflows from India, the main source of investment in South Asia, more than doubled, according to a new trade report by the UN.

(A) 39 billion dollars

(B) 38 billion dollars

(C) 41 billion dollars

(D) ** 40 billion dollars

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why FDI Inflows declined? Experts say that the proximate reason is that big-ticket mergers and acquisitions (M&As) which attracted FDI flows in recent years were not repeated.

Concept note-2: -Foreign direct investment (FDI) inflows in ASEAN surged by 42% in 2021 to US$174 billion.

Concept note-3: -FDI equity inflows into India contracted by 14 per cent to USD 26.9 billion during the April-September this fiscal, according to the DPIIT data.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Services Sector.
