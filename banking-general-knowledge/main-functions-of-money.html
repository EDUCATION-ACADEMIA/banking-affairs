Main functions of Money?

(A) Medium of Exchange

(B) Unit of Account

(C) Store of Value

(D) ** A, B and C of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Money serves several functions: a medium of exchange, a unit of account, a store of value, and a standard of deferred payment.

Concept note-2: -Money functions as a medium of exchange, allowing individuals to trade goods and services with one another. It also serves as a store of value, allowing people to save wealth over time. Lastly, it functions as a unit of value, enabling people to compare the worth of different items.
