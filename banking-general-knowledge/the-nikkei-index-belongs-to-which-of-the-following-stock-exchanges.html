The Nikkei index belongs to which of the following stock exchanges?

(A) ** Tokyo

(B) Beijing

(C) New York

(D) Bangkok

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Nikkei 225 is a price-weighted equity index, which consists of 225 stocks in the Prime Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange .

Concept note-2: -The Nikkei is Japan’s leading stock index comprised of the country’s top 225 blue-chip stocks.

Concept note-3: -The Tokyo Stock Exchange (, Tōkyō Shōken Torihikijo), abbreviated as Tosho () or TSE/TYO, is a stock exchange located in Tokyo, Japan.

Concept note-4: -The Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) is the largest stock exchange in Japan, headquartered in its capital city of Tokyo. The Tokyo Stock Exchange was established on May 15, 1878. As of Sept. 14, 2021, the exchange had 3, 784 listed companies.

Concept note-5: -Tokyo Stock Price Index (TOPIX) is a measure of the overall trend in the stock market, and is used as a benchmark for investment in Japanese stocks.
