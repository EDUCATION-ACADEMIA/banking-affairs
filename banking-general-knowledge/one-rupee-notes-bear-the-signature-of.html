One rupee notes bear the signature of

(A) Governor of Reserve Bank of India

(B) Prime Minister of India

(C) President of India

(D) ** Secretary, Ministry of Finance (Government of India)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under section 22 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, RBI has sole right to issue currency notes of various denominations except one rupee note s. The One Rupee note is issued by Ministry of Finance and it bears the signatures of Finance Secretary, while other notes bear the signature of RBI Governor.

Concept note-2: -The One Rupee note is issued by the Ministry of Finance and it bears the signatures of Finance Secretary, while other notes bear the signature of Governor RBI.

Concept note-3: -As a result, the one rupee note is the only note bearing the signature of the Finance Secretary and not the Governor of the RBI.

Concept note-4: -The one rupee note and coins are issued by ministry of finance and it bears the signature of Finance Secretary.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 4 i.e. The Finance Secretary.
