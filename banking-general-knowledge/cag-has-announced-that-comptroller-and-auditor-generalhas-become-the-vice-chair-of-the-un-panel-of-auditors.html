(A) Shaktikanta Das

(B) Alok Verma

(D) ** Rajiv Mehrishi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Comptroller and Auditor General Rajiv Mehrishi has become the Vice-Chair of the UN Panel of Auditors, CAG said on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -Governor of UT of Jammu Kashmir G. C. Murmu is the current CAG of India. He assumed office on 8 August 2020.

Concept note-3: -Pierre Moscovici, the First President of the Cour des comptes, as well as all the members of the Panel, we will persevere in enhancing accountability, transparency, and governance of the organizations within the United Nations system synergistically.

Concept note-4: -Every person appointed to be the Comptroller and Auditor-General of India shall, before he enters upon his office, make and subscribe before the President or some person appointed in that behalf by him, an oath or affirmation according to the form set out for the purpose in the Third Schedule.
