INS Virat will be converted into floating museum soon. As per primary estimates, the project worth almost____rupees.

(A) 950 crore

(B) 778 crore

(C) ** 852 crore

(D) 490 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Correct Option: D. The ex-aircraft carrier of the Indian Navy, INS Vikrant is being converted into a floating museum and rechristened Indian Museum Ship (IMS) Vikrant. She is now anchored opposite to Middle Ground near Gateway of India in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: The Maharashtra state cabinet on Thursday cleared the decks for the Navy’s longest-serving aircraft carrier, INS Viraat, now retired, to be converted into India’s first-ever moored maritime museum-cum-marine adventure centre.

Concept note-3: -In January 1995, the navy decided to keep Vikrant in “safe to float” state. She was laid up and formally decommissioned on 31 January 1997.
