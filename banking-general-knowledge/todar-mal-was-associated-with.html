Todar Mal was associated with

(A) Music

(B) Art

(C) ** Finance

(D) Law

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Todar Mal was associated with Finance in Akbar’s durbar and was one of the Navaratnas . He was the finance minister of the Mughal Empire and was associated with land revenue reforms. Tansen was associated with Music in Akbar’s durbar.

Concept note-2: -Complete answer: Todar Mal was associated with the land revenue system of the Mughal Empire.

Concept note-3: -Raja Todar Mal (1 January 1500 – 8 November 1589) was the Finance Minister (Mushriff-i-Diwan) of the Mughal empire during Emperor Akbar’s reign.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Todarmal was one of the Navratna of Akbar Darbar. He was the finance minister of the Mughal empire during Akbar’s reign.

Concept note-5: -Akbar’s revenue minister was Raja Todar Mal. He was the finance minister or revenue minister and one of the Navaratnas in Akbar’s court.
