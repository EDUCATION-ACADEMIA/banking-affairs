What is the defect of the barter system?

(A) Lack of double coincidence of wants

(B) Difficulty in the measurement of value

(C) Difficulty in store of value

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The problems associated with the barter system are inability to make deferred payments, lack of common measure value, difficulty in storage of goods, lack of double coincidence of wants.

Concept note-2: -Barter system had many drawbacks like lack of double coincidence of wants, lack of a common unit of value, difficulty of future payments or contractual payments and difficulty of storage of value and transfer of value.

Concept note-3: -Expert-verified answer The drawbacks of barter system are: Lack of double coincidence of wants. The lack of agreement between buyer and seller of goods arises problems in the absence of medium of exchange. Lack of divisibility.
