Concept note-1: -The Economic Survey of India is an annual document of the Ministry of Finance, Government of India. The Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance presents the Survey in the Parliament every year, just before the Union Budget. It is prepared under the guidance of the Chief Economic Adviser of India.

Concept note-2: -The Economic Survey of India is an annual report released by the Finance Ministry.

Concept note-3: -It is prepared under the guidance of the Chief Economic Advisor (CEA), who also presents it in both Houses of Parliament during the budget session. Further Reading: Union Budget 2021.
