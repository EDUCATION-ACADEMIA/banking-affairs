[SOURCES]
world investment report is published by

(A) UNDP

(B) World Bank

(C) IMF

(D) ** UNCTAD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Investment Report supports policymakers by monitoring global and regional investment trends and national and international investment policy developments. The report reviews investment in the Sustainable Development Goals and in climate change mitigation and adaptation.

Concept note-2: -The United National Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) brings out the World Investment Report annually. The focus of the report is on the assessment of foreign direct investment (FDIs) trends worldwide. World Investment Report 2022 was released in June 2022.

Concept note-3: -UNCTAD Reports The report focuses on trends in Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) worldwide and emerging measures to improve its contribution to development. Global FDI decreased by up to 40% in 2020. It is projected to decrease by a further 5% to 10% in 2021.

Concept note-4: -In news– The United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) has published the Global Investment Trend Monitor Report – 2022 recently.
