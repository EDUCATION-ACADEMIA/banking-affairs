The Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP) has been renamed as the____with a mandate to deal with matters related to start-ups, facilitating ease of doing business among others

(A) Department for Product of Industry and Internal Trade

(B) Department for Promotion of Industry and International Trade

(C) Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Tracking

(D) ** Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The department in its current form came into being on 27 January 2019, when the erstwhile Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion was renamed to Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) after internal trade was added to its mandate.

Concept note-2: -Yes, the Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion (DIPP) was renamed as the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT).

Concept note-3: -The Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion (DIPP)is responsible for formulation and implementation of promotional and developmental measures for growth of the industrial sector, keeping in view the national priorities and socio-economic objectives. It is responsible for the overall Industrial Policy.
