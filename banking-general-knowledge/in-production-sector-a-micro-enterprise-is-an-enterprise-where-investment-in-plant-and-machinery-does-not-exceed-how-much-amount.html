In production sector, a micro enterprise is an enterprise where investment in plant and machinery does not exceed how much amount?

(A) Rs 10 lakh

(B) Rs 15 lakh

(C) Rs 20 lakh

(D) ** Rs 25 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A micro enterprise is an enterprise where the investment in equipment does not exceed Rs. 10 lakh; A small enterprise is an enterprise where the investment in equipment is more than Rs. 10 lakh but does not exceed Rs.

Concept note-2: -Micro Enterprise: Where the investment in Plant and Machinery or Equipment does not exceed one crore rupees and turnover does not exceed five crore rupees. Small Enterprise: Where the investment in Plant and Machinery or Equipment does not exceed ten crore rupees and turnover does not exceed fifty crore rupees.

Concept note-3: -As per this announcement, the definition of Micro manufacturing and services units was increased to Rs. 1 Crore of investment and Rs. 5 Crore of turnover.

Concept note-4: -The limit of investment on plant and machinery for micro enterprises is 1 crore.
