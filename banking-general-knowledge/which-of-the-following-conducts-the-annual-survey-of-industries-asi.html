Which of the following conducts the Annual Survey of Industries (ASI)?

(A) ** Central Statistics Office

(B) National Sample Survey Organisation

(C) Ministry of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises

(D) Ministry of Economy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Statistical Office (NSO) under Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation, Government of India conducts various surveys in India since 1950. The Annual Survey of Industries (ASI) is the principal source of Industrial Statistics in India.

Concept note-2: -was carried out by the Indian Statistical Institute, Calcutta. The SSMI was conducted annually up to 1958 by the then Directorate of National Sample Survey (now the NSS Office).

Concept note-3: -Directorate of Economic and Statistics.

Concept note-4: -CSO is an attached Office and NSSO is subordinate Office under the control of Ministry of S&PI.

Concept note-5: -As per the dissemination policy of the Ministry, Data Informatics and Innovation Division (DIID) disseminates the Unit-level data of ASI to the public.
