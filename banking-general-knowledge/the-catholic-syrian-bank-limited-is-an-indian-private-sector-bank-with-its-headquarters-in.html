The Catholic Syrian Bank Limited is an Indian private sector bank with its headquarters in

(A) Karur

(B) ** Thrissur

(C) Kochin

(D) Thiruvananthapuram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CSB Bank Limited (erstwhile Catholic Syrian Bank Limited) is an Indian private sector bank with its headquarters at Thrissur, Kerala, India. The bank has a network of over 560 branches and more than 390 ATMs across India.

Concept note-2: -Established in 1920 as The Catholic Syrian Bank Limited, CSB Bank Limited had its modest beginning in November 1920 and is currently the oldest private sector bank in Kerala.

Concept note-3: -The full form of CSB is the Catholic Syrian Bank Limited. CSB is an Indian private bank with its head office is in Thrissur, Kerala. It is one of India’s oldest banks. The bank has the largest network throughout India with over four fifty branches and even more than two fifty-seven ATMs.

Concept note-4: -MUMBAI: The 100-year-old CSB Bank (formerly Catholic Syrian Bank), which is now majority owned by Canadian-billionaire Prem Watsa’s Fairfax Group, has charted a route to transform itself into a new-generation private bank.
