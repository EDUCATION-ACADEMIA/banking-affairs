What is the meaning of “E” in AEPS?

(A) ** Enabled

(B) Enquiry

(C) Equity

(D) Essential

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Through the Aadhaar enabled Payment System or AePS, all Aadhaar card holders can make payments, transfer funds, make withdrawals, deposit cash and other bank related activities by using Aadhaar based authentication.

Concept note-2: -Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AePS) – Aadhaar Pay. NPCI.

Concept note-3: -A. Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) is a payment service that allows a bank customer to use Aadhaar as his/her identity to access his/her Aadhaar enabled bank account and perform basic banking transactions like balance enquiry, cash withdrawal, remittances through a Business Correspondent.
