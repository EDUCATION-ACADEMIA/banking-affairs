(A) A = 5, 000(1 + 4)3

(B) ** A = 5, 000(1 + 0.04)3

(C) A = 5, 000(1 + 0.4) x 3

(D) A = 5, 000(0.04)3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, the compound interest is Rs. 624.32 on Rs. 5000 if it is compounded half yearly for 1 year 6 months at 8 % per annum.

Concept note-2: -Compounded monthly, is the same as he got at 6% interest per annum for 3 years. Compounded annually. Therefore, compound interest is 955.08.
