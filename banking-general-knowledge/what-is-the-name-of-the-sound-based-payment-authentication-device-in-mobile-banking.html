What is the name of the sound-based payment authentication device in mobile banking?

(A) Cash On mobile

(B) Sound tag

(C) ** Tone Tag

(D) Caller Tune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Along with all of these devices, ToneTag has developed the RetailPod, a proprietary device that enables a sound-based tap-to-pay feature for feature phones, as seen in the RBI video on UPI123Pay mentioned before.

Concept note-2: -new feature named VoiceSe, developed by ToneTag in partnership with NSDL Payments Bank and NPCI, will allow India’s 400 million feature phone users to access the payment services through voice | .

Concept note-3: -It costs around Rs 750 to make one pod, according to Abhishek.

Concept note-4: -UPI 123PAY is an instant payment system for feature phone users that allows them to safely and securely use the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) payment service. Feature phone users can conduct a variety of transactions based on four technological alternatives via UPI 123PAY.

Concept note-5: -A payment gateway is an application that makes the online payment process very simple and easy. It is actually a medium that connects a merchant’s mobile application or website with the bank.
