Financial year in banks is a period from

(A) January to December

(B) May to June

(C) ** April to March

(D) January to April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is April 1. In India, this 1 year period starts from 1st April and ends on 31st March.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Financial Year In India, the fiscal year starts on April 1 and ends on March 31. AY 2023-24 will be the review year for FY 2022-23. In certain circumstances, the financial year may vary from the previous year.

Concept note-3: -The fiscal year for the RBI, however, begins on July 1 in the first year and ends on June 30 in the next year.

Concept note-4: -A Financial Year (FY) is the period between 1 April and 31 March – the accounting year in which you earn an income.
