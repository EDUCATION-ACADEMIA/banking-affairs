In which of the following accounts, you do not get interest?

(A) Savings account

(B) ** Current account

(C) Both 1 and 2

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Current Account.

Concept note-2: -Currents accounts do not earn any interest due to the fluidity they offer. Current accounts usually do not carry a limit on the number of transactions which can be made.

Concept note-3: -A Current Account is actually a no interest-bearing deposit account.

Concept note-4: -Current Deposit Account Banks open a current account for them. Like a savings bank account, this account also requires a certain minimum amount of deposit while opening the account. On this deposit, the bank does not pay any interest on the balances.
