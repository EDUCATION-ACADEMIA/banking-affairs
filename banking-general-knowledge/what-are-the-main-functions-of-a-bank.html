What are the main functions of a bank

(A) Accept deposits

(B) provide loans

(C) Locker services

(D) ** All the options

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Functions of Commercial Banks:-Primary functions include accepting deposits, granting loans, advances, cash, credit, overdraft and discounting of bills.-Secondary functions include issuing letter of credit, undertaking safe custody of valuables, providing consumer finance, educational loans, etc.

Concept note-2: -Major Functions of Banks in India Accepting deposits. Lending loans and advances. Transfer of funds. Issue of notes/ drafts.
