Fixed Deposits can be created for a minimum of 7 days.

(A) ** True

(B) False

(C) Either True or False

(D) None of the options

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The period of the FD ranges from 7 days to 10 years. A short-term FD has a maturity tenure of a minimum of 7 days to a maximum of 12 months.

Concept note-2: -The 7-day call account is an alternative to investing funds into long term fixed deposit accounts. This account carries a higher rate of interest than ordinary savings accounts but gives the customer the flexibility of withdrawals provided that 7 days notice is given.

Concept note-3: -Some banks do offer premature withdrawal facilities with zero penalty charges. However, if the FD is prematurely closed, before completing 7 days from the date of the booking, the bank or the company is not liable to pay any interest.
