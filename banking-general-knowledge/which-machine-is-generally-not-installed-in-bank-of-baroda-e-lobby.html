Which machine is generally not installed in Bank of Baroda E-Lobby

(A) Multifunction Kiosk

(B) Cash Recycler

(C) ** Account Opening Kiosk

(D) Self Service Passbook Printer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ATM withdrawals, cash deposits, card-to-card transfers, mobile phone top-ups, railway booking, passbook printing, NEFT, opening of FD/RD accounts, SMS alerts, cheque drop box, bill payments, mini statements, etc.

Concept note-2: -Banking e-Lobby may be defined as “Bank branch without Bankers” and also this may be seen as “Next Generation Bank Branches” by delivering automated banking Products & Services without manual intervention. In this pursuit, Bank is installing e-Lobbies [BNA, SAPB, ATM, CDM & Cash Recyclers]

Concept note-3: -E-Lobby is the new facility provided by the bank to empower all customers to do their banking transactions as per their convenience without any time restriction.

Concept note-4: -Kiosk Banking provides the customers with an upper limit of Rs. 50, 000 for their bank accounts.
