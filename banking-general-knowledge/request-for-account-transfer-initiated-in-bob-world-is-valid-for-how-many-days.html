Request for account transfer initiated in bob World is valid for how many days?

(A) 15 days

(B) 4 days

(C) ** 30 days

(D) 45 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transaction Limit: Rs. 30 Lakhs per transaction and Rs. 75 lakhs per day.

Concept note-2: -How to check BOB Account Transfer status? To know whether your account successfully transferred to the new branch or not, you can track your account transfer status. Click on Services – Account Transfer in Utilities. Now click on status.

Concept note-3: -It will take a few minutes/hours for the bank to approve the beneficiary. You can transfer money through different types of funds such as RTGS, NEFT, IMPS after the bank approves your beneficiary.

Concept note-4: -If your IMPS transaction times out or fails, check if your money has been debited from the account. If the money is not debited, you can wait for a few minutes and strike another transaction. In another case, if the money is debited from your account, check your account after two working days.
