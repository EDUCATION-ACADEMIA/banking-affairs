1. :

It will put a curb on lending of money by the banks.

2. :

It will help Govt of China to tame the rate of inflation.

It will help the poor to get easy loans at cheaper rates from the banks as higher CRR brings more money for activities of the social sector.

(A) Only 1 is correct

(B) Only 2 is correct

(C) All the above are correct

(D) ** Both 1 & 2 are correct
