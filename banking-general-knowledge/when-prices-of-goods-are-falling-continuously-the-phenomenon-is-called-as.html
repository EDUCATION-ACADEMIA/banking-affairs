When prices of goods are falling continuously, the phenomenon is called as:

(A) Stagflation

(B) Disinflation

(C) ** Deflation

(D) Reflation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(I) Deflation is a decrease in the general price level of goods and services. It occurs when the inflation rate falls below 0%.

Concept note-2: -When prices are falling continuously, the phenomenon is called deflation.

Concept note-3: -Also known as deflation, negative inflation occurs when prices drop for various reasons.

Concept note-4: -Inflation is the rate of increase in prices over a given period of time. Inflation is typically a broad measure, such as the overall increase in prices or the increase in the cost of living in a country.

Concept note-5: -Deflation is when the prices of goods and services go down, which is called deflation. There is deflation when the inflation rate is less than 0 percent.
