(A) India

(B) Britain

(C) France

(D) ** USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Venture capital, as an industry, originated in the United States, and American firms have traditionally been the largest participants in venture deals with the bulk of venture capital being deployed in American companies.

Concept note-4: -The venture capital industry in India originated in the late 1980s, with the Indian government granted legal status to venture capital activities in 1988, and has attracted interest ever since.
