EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singapore moved up a spot from its GII 2021 ranking. The country continued to perform well in the GII’s traditionally strong indicators. Among these is Institutions where the country maintained its number one ranking. Another is Infrastructure where Singapore climbed from 15th spot in 2021 to 11th in 2022.

Concept note-2: -In addition, we have strengthened our innovation linkages, faring well in indicators such as university-industry R&D collaboration. 4 Said IPOS Chief Executive Rena Lee, “We’re heartened that Singapore has moved up a notch to be ranked the 7th most innovative country in the world.

Concept note-3: -Meanwhile, the most recent Global Innovation Index (GII) 2022 released by the World Intellectual Property Organisation (WIPO) ranks Singapore as the seventh most innovative country in the world, with a rise of one position.

Concept note-4: -SINGAPORE – Singapore is now the seventh most innovative economy in the world, up one spot from its ranking in 2021.
