Which of the following is incorrect about Non-banking Financial Companies (NBFCs)?

(A) NBFC cannot accept demand deposits

(B) NBFC is not a part of the payment and settlement system

(C) ** NBFC can issue cheques drawn on itself

(D) NBFCS are fast emerging segment of Indian financial system

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NBFC depositors are not covered under the deposit insurance facilities. Statement 3 is incorrect. NBFCs can not issue cheques or draw a cheque on their own.

Concept note-2: -NBFCs do not form part of the payment and settlement system and cannot issue cheques drawn on themselves; iii. the deposit insurance facility of Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation is not available to depositors of NBFCs, unlike in the case of banks. Thus, option 2 is the correct answer.

Concept note-3: -However, one cannot withdraw money from these companies, which differentiates them from a bank.

Concept note-4: -Answer: [A] NBFCs does not hold a banking license. Notes: NBFC cannot accept demand deposits while NBFC cannot issue Demand Drafts like banks.
