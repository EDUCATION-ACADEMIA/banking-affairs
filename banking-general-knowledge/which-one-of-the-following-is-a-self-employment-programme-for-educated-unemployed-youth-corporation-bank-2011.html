Which one of the following is a Self-Employment Programme for Educated Unemployed Youth? [Corporation Bank 2011]

(B) Swarn Jayanti Sahakari Rozgar Yojana

(C) National Social Assistance Programme

(D) Swarn Jayanti Gram Swarozgar Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution.
