Which of the following is NOT a function of a bank?

(A) Providing project finance

(B) Selling Mutual Funds

(C) ** Deciding policy rates like CRR, Repo Rate/SLR etc

(D) Settlement of payments of behalf of the customers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Facilitating import of goods is not a primary function of a bank.

Concept note-2: -Advancing loans to the public and accepting deposits from the public is not the function of the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-3: -Issuing of coins is not a function of the Central Bank.

Concept note-4: -Hence, Issuing of Notes is not the function of a Commercial Bank. NOTE-The coins are issued for circulation only through the Reserve Bank in terms of the RBI Act.
