Which is the first Indian company to be listed on NASDAQ?

(A) Reliance

(B) TCS

(C) HCL

(D) ** Infosys

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Infosys was the first Indian company to be listed on the NASDAQ, an American Stock Exchange . NASDAQ is the second largest exchange in the world. Q. What was the event due to which the Indian Railways turned to TISCO for their steel requirements?

Concept note-2: -Infosys Technologies is the first Indian company to be added to the NASDAQ-100 index and is the only Indian company to be part of any of the major global indices.

Concept note-3: -Infosys was the first Indian company to be listed on the NASDAQ in 1999.

Concept note-4: -Infosys Ltd. – NYSE: Software & Computer Services; Established in 1981 and listed in the year 1999 in NASDAQ which as eventually shifted to NYSE in 2012. MakeMyTrip Limited – NASDAQ: Travel & Leisure; Established in 2000. Rediff.com India – NASDAQ: Newspaper and Magazine; Established in 1996.

Concept note-5: -The oldest company in India is the Wadia Group whose origins are traceable to 1736, with its subsidiary The Bombay Burmah Trading Corporation Limited, established in 1863, being the oldest publicly traded company in India.
