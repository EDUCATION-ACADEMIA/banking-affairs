(B) 50 years

(C) 40 years

(D) ** 60 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atal Pension Yojana (APY), a pension scheme for citizens of India, is focused on the unorganised sector workers. Under the APY, guaranteed minimum pension of Rs. 1, 000/-or 2, 000/-or 3, 000/-or 4, 000 or 5, 000/-per month will be given at the age of 60 years depending on the contributions by the subscribers.
