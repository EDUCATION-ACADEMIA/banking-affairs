Concept note-2: -About Brickwork Ratings BWR has its corporate office in Bengaluru and a country-wide presence with its offices in Ahmedabad, Chandigarh, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai and New Delhi along with representatives in 150+ locations.

Concept note-3: -Vivek Kulkarni, IAS (Retd)Founder & Managing Director, Brickwork Ratings. Vivek Kulkarni is also the Founder & President of Brickwork Finance Academy and the Founder Chairman of Brickwork India, a Knowledge Process Outsourcing company.
