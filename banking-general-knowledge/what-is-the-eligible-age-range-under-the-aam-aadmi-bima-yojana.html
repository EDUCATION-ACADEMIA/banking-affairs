What is the eligible age range under the Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana?

(A) 16 to 55 years

(B) 21 to 60 years

(C) ** 18 to 59 years

(D) 25 to 68 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The members should be aged between 18 years completed and 59 years nearer birthday. The member should normally be the head of the family or one earning member of the below poverty line family (BPL) or marginally above the poverty line under identified vocational group/rural landless household.

Concept note-2: -Eligibility Criteria for Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana People aged between 18 years and 59 years. People belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL), families slightly above BPL who are a part of the vocational group as specified in the policy, and those belonging to rural landless household.

Concept note-3: -The Aam Aadmi Bima Yojana was launched in India by LIC in order to provide social security along with health and financial aid to the rural population of India who do not have any access to healthcare and medicine.
