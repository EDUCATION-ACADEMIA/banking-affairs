Which is not a source of Agriculture finance in India?

(A) Co-operative societies

(B) Commercial Banks

(C) Regional Rural Banks

(D) ** Central Rural Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This question sates non agricultural source: Co-operative societies, Commercial Bank sand Regional Rural Bank are source of agriculture finance in India.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is NABARD.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. Buying seeds is NOT correct. Agriculture investment loans are available to individuals engaged in agri-related sectors and farmers.

Concept note-4: -Sources of agricultural credit can be broadly classified into institutional and non-institutional sources. Institutional sources include co-operatives, commercial banks including the SBI Group, RBI and NABARD.

Concept note-5: -Which of the following is not a source of rural credit ? a)Regional rural banksb)Moneylendersc)Tradersd)GovernmentCorrect answer is option ‘D’.
