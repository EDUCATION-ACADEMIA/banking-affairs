Bandhan Bank Limited became one of the top 50 most valuable publicly traded firms in India. Where is the Headquarters of Bandhan Bank?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Chennai

(C) Varanasi

(D) ** Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bandhan Bank Ltd. is a banking and financial services company, headquartered in Kolkata. Bandhan Bank is present in 34 out of 36 states and union territories of India, with 5, 723 banking outlets and more than 2.86 crore customers.

Concept note-2: -Bandhan Bank’s core purpose has graduated from financial inclusion to inclusive banking. Bandhan Bank is built on the philosophy of customer-centricity, with a keen focus on people, process and technology.

Concept note-3: -The Economic Times Bengal Corporate Awards Bandhan Bank bagged two prestigious awards at the event. The Bank was recognised as the ‘Fastest Growing Company’, and also received the award for ‘Excellence in Business Performance’ in the category of companies with turnover of Rs. 3, 000 crores.
