Under OLTAS, only a Single Copy Challan is used with a tear off the portion for the Tax Payer. What does OLTAS stand for

(A) On-line Tax Accounting Service

(B) On-line Tax Amounting System

(C) On-line Timing Accounting System

(D) ** On-line Tax Accounting System

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Income Tax Department’s initiative to receive information and maintain records of tax paid through banks through online upload of challan details is named as OLTAS (Online Tax Accounting System).

Concept note-2: -The Income-tax Department has introduced with effect from June 1, 2004 a new system relating to payment of taxes, called Online Tax accounting System (OLTAS). The taxpayer friendly features of OLTAS include a single copy challan-cum-acknowledgment form instead of the present four-copy challan.

Concept note-3: -You can verify the status of the challan in the “Challan Status Inquiry” at Protean-TIN website after 5 to 7 days of making e-payment. In case of non availability of the challan status kindly contact your bank.

Concept note-4: -But without open challan means you need to manually enter challan details(IT department does not have any info of outstanding challan)
