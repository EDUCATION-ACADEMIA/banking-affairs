The 22nd All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) will be established in which state?

(A) Bihar

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Union Minister of State for Planning and Corporate, Rao Inderjit Singh, Prime Minister Narendra Modi will lay the foundation stone of the AIIMS at Rewari making it the 22nd such institute to be inaugurated.

Concept note-2: -On 7 November 2015, Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi had announced development package for Jammu & Kashmir which includes the setting up of two AIIMS, in the capital cities of Jammu and Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -AIIMS – New Delhi Situated in the country capital, AIIMS Delhi conducts research and teaching in 42 disciplines. AIIMS Delhi was ranked first among the medical colleges in India by the Week, Outlook India and India Today. Created as an autonomous institution, AIIMS Delhi was established in 1956.

Concept note-4: -The AIIMS would be established at Manethi village in Haryana’s Rewari district.
