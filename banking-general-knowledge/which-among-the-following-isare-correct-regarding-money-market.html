Which among the following is/are correct regarding Money Market?

(A) Money Market is a market for short-term funds

(B) Maturity in this market ranging from overnight to one year

(C) The basic function of money market is to provide efficient liquidity position

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Treasury Bills, Certificate of Deposit as well as Commercial papers are money market instruments.

Concept note-2: -1) Treasury bills 2) Commercial papers. 3) Debentures 4) Certificates of deposits.

Concept note-3: -Characteristics of Money Market Instruments It is a market for short term financial needs, for example, working capital needs. The main money market instruments are Treasury bills, commercial papers, certificate of deposits, and call money. It is highly liquid as it has instruments that have a maturity below one year.
