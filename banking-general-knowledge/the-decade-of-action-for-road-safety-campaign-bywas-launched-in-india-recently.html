‘The Decade of Action for Road safety’ campaign by____was launched in India recently.

(A) World Bank

(B) ** UNO

(C) WTO

(D) SAARC Nations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In September 2020, the UN General Assembly adopted resolution A/RES/74/299 “Improving global road safety", proclaiming the Decade of Action for Road Safety 2021-2030, with the ambitious target of preventing at least 50% of road traffic deaths and injuries by 2030.

Concept note-2: -The Decade of Action for Road Safety 2011–2020 was officially proclaimed by the United Nations General Assembly in March 2010. Its goal is to stabilize and reduce the forecast level of road traffic deaths around the world. It is estimated that 5 million lives could be saved on the world’s roads during the decade.
