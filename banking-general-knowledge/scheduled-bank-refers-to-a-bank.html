Scheduled bank refers to a bank

(A) authorized by central government to transaction government business

(B) registered with ministry of finance, govt. of India

(C) ** Which is included in the second schedule to the RBI act, 1934

(D) incorporated as a cooperative society

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All banks which are included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 are Scheduled Banks. These banks comprise Scheduled Commercial Banks and Scheduled Co-operative Banks.

Concept note-2: -By definition, any bank which is listed in the 2nd schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 is considered a scheduled bank. The Schedule consists of those banks which satisfy various parameters, criteria under clause 42 of this act.
