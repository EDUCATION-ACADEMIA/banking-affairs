Select the correct option from the codes given below:

(A) Only 1

(B) ** Only 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Definition of Microfinance Loan. 3.1 A microfinance loan is defined as a collateral-free loan given to a household having annual household income up to ₹3, 00, 000. For this purpose, the household shall mean an individual family unit, i.e., husband, wife and their unmarried children.

Concept note-2: -They have to get NBFC License with RBI and fulfill the conditions as laid down for them. NBFC MFI is a non-deposit taking NBFC (other than a company licensed u/s 25 of the Indian Companies Act, 1956) that meets the following conditions: Minimum Net Owned Funds (NOF) of Rs. 5 crore.
