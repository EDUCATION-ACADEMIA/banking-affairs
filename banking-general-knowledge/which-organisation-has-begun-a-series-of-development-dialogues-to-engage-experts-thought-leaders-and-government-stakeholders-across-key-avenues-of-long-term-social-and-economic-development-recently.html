Which Organisation has begun a series of Development Dialogues to engage experts, thought leaders and government stakeholders across key avenues of long-term social and economic development recently?

(A) FICCI

(B) NABARD

(C) RBI

(D) ** NITI Aayog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NITI Aayog, the policy think-tank of the Government of India, in keeping with its manadate to foster stakeholder involvement and coordinate the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals, has initiated a series of consultations called the ‘Development Dailogues’.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog is developing itself as a state-of-the-art resource centre with the necessary knowledge and skills that will enable it to act with speed, promote research and innovation, provide strategic policy vision for the government, and deal with contingent issues.

Concept note-3: -NITI Aayog was formed via a resolution of the Union Cabinet on 1 January 2015.
