Which Bank’s credit card issuer has announced the launch of ‘ELA’ (Electronic Live Assistant), a virtual assistant for customer support and services?

(A) ** State Bank of India

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) Bank of Baroda

(D) Axis Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution. SIA(SBI Intelligent Assistant) fluently answers inquiries on all banking products and services such as how to open an account or apply for loans, etc.

Concept note-2: -State Bank Virtual card, also known as Electronic Card or e-Card, is a limit Debit Card created for ecommerce transactions. It provides an easy and secure way of transacting online without providing the Primary Card/ Account information to the merchant.

Concept note-3: -ILA is SBI Card’s Interactive Live Assistant that can provide you with the latest information on our products & services. You can chat with ILA to get information on Card features, benefits, services and much more.
