Concept note-1: -What Is a Point-of-Sale Terminal? A point-of-sale (POS) terminal is a hardware system for processing card payments at retail locations. Software to read magnetic strips of credit and debit cards is embedded in the hardware.

Concept note-2: -POS is the acronym for Point of Sales. A POS terminal is a machine installed at Merchant Establishments which enables the merchants to accept payments through payment cards (credit cards, debit cards, gift cards etc.).

Concept note-3: -A point of sale (POS) is a place where a customer executes the payment for goods or services and where sales taxes may become payable. A POS transaction may occur in person or online, with receipts generated either in print or electronically.

Concept note-4: -The correct option is D. Point of sale machine. Point of Sale machine is another name for a card machine that is used to swipe the debit/credit card.
