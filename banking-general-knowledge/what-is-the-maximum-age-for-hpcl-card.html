What is the maximum age for HPCL Card.

(A) 40

(B) 50

(C) ** 65

(D) 60

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Minimum and Maximum Age Limit Criteria for the Candidates who is applying for HPCL Recruitment 2023 is 18 years and 25 years.

Concept note-2: -Age Limit ( As on 01 February 2023 ) : 18 to 25 years. Age Relaxation : 5 yrs for SC/ST. 3 yrs for OBC-NC.

Concept note-3: -The online application process started on 23rd June 2022. The last day to fill up the application was 22nd July 2022. In this article, the candidates will get detailed information about the HPCL Recruitment 2022 Notification.

Concept note-4: -There is no age limit to apply for GATE.
