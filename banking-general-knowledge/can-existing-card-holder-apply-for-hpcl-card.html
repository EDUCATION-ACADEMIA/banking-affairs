Can existing Card Holder apply for HPCL Card

(A) ** yes

(B) no

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eligibility Criteria The salaried applicant needs to have an income of ₹30, 000 per month and self-employed needs to have an annual income of ₹6, 00, 000 or above.

Concept note-2: -With a nominal joining fee of Rs. 199, this card offers decent benefits on fuel as well as other categories, such as movie tickets and dining. You can avail this credit card for free by spending Rs. 50, 000 in a year which is feasible if you are a loyal customer of HPCL.

Concept note-3: -ICICI HPCL Super Saver Credit Card is a good option for you, if you are looking for great fuel benefits along with benefits on grocery and departmental stores. The card offers good cashback on fuel spends with up to 5% cashback. However, the benefits are capped to only HPCL fuel pumps and not all fuel spends.

Concept note-4: -All you have to do is to produce your Amex Card at the HPCL Petrol Pump, swipe it on an Amex Bank EDC Terminal only and save surcharge on your fuel purchases. The card can also be used for all your shopping, dining and travelling needs.
